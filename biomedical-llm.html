生醫文本 LLM

Med-Gemini、MedGemma、BioMistral、ChemCrow——什麼時候該換掉通用 LLM？多半，你不該。

Med-Gemini, MedGemma, BioMistral, ChemCrow — when should you swap out a general LLM? Usually, you shouldn't.

術語速查

生醫 LLM 必懂十詞

這 10 個詞貫穿本章——先讀過一次，後面 domain fine-tune、RAG、MedQA、ChemCrow 的討論就會順暢許多。

These ten terms recur throughout. Skim once and the discussion of domain fine-tune, RAG, MedQA, and ChemCrow flows easily.

🌐

Med-Gemini

Google 2024 在 Gemini Pro 上做醫療 SFT 與 RLHF 的閉源模型，MedQA-USMLE 91.1%（Singhal 2024 預印本）。僅雲端 API，US/UK pilot；無法本地部署。

Google's 2024 closed-source medical SFT + RLHF on Gemini Pro, hitting 91.1% on MedQA-USMLE (Singhal 2024 preprint). Cloud API only; US/UK pilots; not self-hostable.

🏥

MedGemma

Google 2026/1 釋出 4B / 27B 開源權重，醫療文本 + 影像多模態，MedQA ~91%。Custom license（非完全 free use），可在 hospital firewall 內自架，是 PHI 受限情境的首選。

Google's Jan 2026 release of 4B / 27B open weights, multimodal med text + image, MedQA ~91%. Custom license (not fully free), self-hostable inside a hospital firewall — top pick when PHI must stay on-prem.

🧪

Med-PaLM 2

Singhal et al. 2023 Nat Med。第一個過 USMLE 通過線（86.5%）的醫療 LLM，奠定「domain fine-tune 可行」基礎。2025 後逐步被 Med-Gemini / MedGemma 取代。

Singhal et al. 2023 Nat Med. The first medical LLM to clear the USMLE passing bar (86.5%), establishing "domain fine-tune is viable". Largely superseded by Med-Gemini / MedGemma since 2025.

📚

BioMistral

Labrak et al. 2024 (EACL)。以 Mistral-7B 為基底繼續預訓練於 PubMed Central（3M+ 全文）。Apache 2.0 完全 free use，單張 24GB GPU 可跑推論，是 lab 內部部署的高 CP 值選擇。

Labrak et al. 2024 (EACL). Continued pre-training of Mistral-7B on PubMed Central (3M+ full-texts). Apache 2.0, no use restrictions; runs inference on a single 24GB GPU — a strong cost-effective lab-internal option.

📜

BioGPT

Luo et al. 2022 Brief Bioinform。Microsoft 在 GPT-2 上做 PubMed abstracts 預訓練。對「2022 年前」資料不錯，但 cutoff 早，已不適合處理當代醫療指引。

Luo et al. 2022 Brief Bioinform. Microsoft's GPT-2 continued on PubMed abstracts. Decent on pre-2022 content but with an early cutoff — unsuitable for current medical guidelines.

🔍

PubMedBERT

Gu et al. 2021 ACM HEALTH。Microsoft 從零訓 BERT（不繼承通用 BERT），純粹 PubMed 語料。在 BioASQ、BC5CDR、ChemProt 等 NER / RE benchmark 上長期領先；不擅長 generation。

Gu et al. 2021 ACM HEALTH. Microsoft's BERT trained from scratch (not continued from general BERT) on pure PubMed. Long-time leader on BioASQ, BC5CDR, ChemProt NER / RE benchmarks; weak at generation.

⚗️

ChemCrow

Bran et al. 2024 Nat Mach Intell。把 GPT-4 包成 agent，接 18 個化學工具（RDKit、retrosynthesis、reaction prediction），自主規劃合成路徑。代表「LLM + tool use」對 domain fine-tune 的另一條路徑。

Bran et al. 2024 Nat Mach Intell. Wraps GPT-4 as an agent calling 18 chemistry tools (RDKit, retrosynthesis, reaction prediction) to autonomously plan syntheses. Represents the "LLM + tool use" alternative to domain fine-tuning.

🎓

Domain Fine-tune

在通用基底模型上以醫療 / 生物文本繼續訓練（continued pre-training 或 instruction tuning）。2023 是必經之路，2026 後因 frontier general LLM 太強，多數情境的 ROI 已不顯著。

Continued training of a general base model on medical / biological text (continued pre-training or instruction tuning). A must in 2023; by 2026, ROI is marginal in most settings as frontier general LLMs have caught up.

🪞

RLAIF

Reinforcement Learning from AI Feedback (Lee et al. 2023, Bai et al. 2022 Constitutional AI)。用大模型代替昂貴的人類 rater 給偏好訊號，是 Med-PaLM 2 → Med-Gemini 對齊流程的關鍵組件。

Reinforcement Learning from AI Feedback (Lee et al. 2023, Bai et al. 2022 Constitutional AI). Substitutes expensive human raters with a larger model for preference signals — a key component in the Med-PaLM 2 → Med-Gemini alignment pipeline.

📝

MedQA

Jin et al. 2021。USMLE 風格選擇題 benchmark，是醫療 LLM 的事實標準。問題是它是「考試題」非「臨床決策」，高分 ≠ 臨床安全。MedQA-USMLE 91% 已被 frontier 通用 LLM 達成。

Jin et al. 2021. A USMLE-style MCQ benchmark, the de facto standard for medical LLMs. But it's "exam questions" not "clinical decisions" — high score ≠ clinical safety. Frontier general LLMs now hit ~91%.

🩺

USMLE

美國醫師執照考試（United States Medical Licensing Examination）。Step 1/2/3 涵蓋基礎、臨床、實務。MedQA 取材自 USMLE 真題；通過線約 60%（依屆別），ICU/急診 decision 不會直接出題。

United States Medical Licensing Examination. Steps 1/2/3 cover basic, clinical, practice. MedQA samples real USMLE items; passing bar ~60% (year-dependent). ICU / ED decisions are not directly asked.

🏷️

Clinical NER

Named Entity Recognition：從病歷 / discharge summary 抽出疾病、藥物、症狀、生化值。代表 dataset：i2b2、MIMIC-III/IV、n2c2。PubMedBERT / ClinicalBERT 至今在這類 token-level 任務仍勝過 generation LLM。

Named Entity Recognition: extracting diseases, drugs, symptoms, labs from clinical notes / discharge summaries. Key datasets: i2b2, MIMIC-III/IV, n2c2. PubMedBERT / ClinicalBERT still outperform generation LLMs on these token-level tasks.

概覽

生醫專用 LLM 是必要的嗎？

2023 年 Med-PaLM 2 在 MedQA-USMLE 上拿到 86.5% 的成績，曾被認為是「醫療 LLM 必須 fine-tune」的證據。但到了 2026 年，故事反轉了：通用 Claude Opus 4.6 / GPT-5.5 Pro / Gemini 2.5 Pro 在 MedQA 上已可達 91–93%，搭配 RAG（檢索 UpToDate / PubMed）後，臨床準確度甚至超越多數領域 fine-tune 模型。

那麼領域 LLM 還有什麼價值？答案是：本地部署、隱私、可預測成本、ChemCrow 式工具使用。MedGemma 1.5（Google 2026/1 釋出）權重開源，可在醫院內網跑；BioMistral 用 Apache 2.0 沒有 use restriction；ChemCrow 把 GPT-4 包成自主合成規劃 agent。這一章把選型邏輯講清楚。

In 2023, Med-PaLM 2 hitting 86.5% on MedQA-USMLE was the canonical proof that "medical LLMs must be fine-tuned." By 2026, the story flipped: general Claude Opus 4.6 / GPT-5.5 Pro / Gemini 2.5 Pro hit 91–93% on MedQA, and paired with RAG (UpToDate, PubMed) they often beat domain fine-tunes on real clinical accuracy.

So what's still worth a domain LLM? On-prem deployment, privacy, predictable cost, ChemCrow-style tool use. MedGemma 1.5 (Google, Jan 2026) ships open weights you can run inside a hospital firewall. BioMistral is Apache 2.0 with no use restrictions. ChemCrow turns GPT-4 into an autonomous synthesis-planning agent. This chapter makes the selection logic explicit.

💡
核心心法：「通用 LLM + 高品質 RAG + 嚴格 prompt」是 80% 生醫情境的最佳解。只有當 (a) 必須完全 on-prem、(b) 需要特殊工具呼叫（化學 / 蛋白）、(c) 預算極受限三者之一成立時，才考慮領域模型。 Core mindset: "General LLM + high-quality RAG + strict prompt" is the best answer in 80% of biomed scenarios. Reach for a domain model only when (a) on-prem is mandatory, (b) you need specialised tool calls (chemistry / protein), or (c) budget is very tight.
核心概念

一、四種生醫 LLM 的譜系

🏥

Med-PaLM 2 → Med-Gemini → MedGemma

Google 系。Med-PaLM 2（Nat Med 2024，MedQA 86.5%）已退役；Med-Gemini 91.1%；MedGemma 1.5（2026/1）開源權重、MedQA ~91%，可下載自架。

Google line. Med-PaLM 2 (Nat Med 2024, 86.5%) is retired; Med-Gemini hits 91.1%; MedGemma 1.5 (Jan 2026) ships open weights, ~91% on MedQA, self-hostable.

🧬

BioMistral / BioGPT / PubMedBERT

學術系。BioMistral 以 Mistral-7B 為基底、繼續在 PubMed Central 訓練，Apache 2.0；BioGPT / PubMedBERT 是更早期的 encoder，常見於 NER 與 classification。

Academic line. BioMistral continues Mistral-7B on PubMed Central, Apache 2.0; BioGPT / PubMedBERT are earlier encoders still common for NER and classification.

⚗️

ChemCrow / Coscientist

工具型 agent。ChemCrow (Bran et al., Nat Mach Intell 2024) 把 GPT-4 接上 18 個化學工具（RDKit、reaction prediction、retrosynthesis），自主規劃合成路徑。

Tool-using agents. ChemCrow (Bran et al., Nat Mach Intell 2024) wraps GPT-4 with 18 chemistry tools (RDKit, reaction prediction, retrosynthesis) to autonomously plan syntheses.

🤖

通用 LLM + RAG

Claude / GPT / Gemini 接 PubMed / UpToDate / 自家 EHR 文件向量庫。在 2026 多項 head-to-head 評比中，這條路在「真實臨床查詢」上經常勝過 fine-tune model。

Claude / GPT / Gemini hooked to PubMed / UpToDate / your EHR document vector store. In 2026 head-to-heads, this path often beats fine-tuned models on real clinical queries.

實作程式碼

三、三種典型呼叫方式

# BioMistral 7B — Hugging Face transformers
from transformers import AutoTokenizer, AutoModelForCausalLM
import torch

tok = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained("BioMistral/BioMistral-7B")
mdl = AutoModelForCausalLM.from_pretrained(
    "BioMistral/BioMistral-7B",
    torch_dtype=torch.bfloat16, device_map="auto")

prompt = "Question: What is the first-line antibiotic for community-acquired pneumonia in a healthy adult? Answer:"
inputs = tok(prompt, return_tensors="pt").to("cuda")
out = mdl.generate(**inputs, max_new_tokens=200, temperature=0.2)
print(tok.decode(out[0], skip_special_tokens=True))
# MedGemma 1.5 (4B / 27B) — on-prem inference via vLLM
# pip install vllm
from vllm import LLM, SamplingParams

llm = LLM(model="google/medgemma-1.5-27b-it",
          dtype="bfloat16", tensor_parallel_size=2)

sp = SamplingParams(temperature=0.1, max_tokens=512)
prompt = """You are a clinical reasoning assistant.
Patient: 58yo M, T2DM, presents with chest pain radiating to left arm.
Q: What is the immediate workup priority?"""

resp = llm.generate([prompt], sp)
print(resp[0].outputs[0].text)
# Runs entirely inside hospital firewall — no PHI leaves the building.
# ChemCrow — GPT-4 + chemistry tools for synthesis planning
# pip install chemcrow
from chemcrow.agents import ChemCrow

agent = ChemCrow(model="gpt-4-1106-preview", temp=0.1)

task = """Propose a 3-step synthetic route from commercially
available starting materials to atorvastatin's lactone form.
For each step, give reagents, conditions, expected yield,
and cite a literature precedent (DOI or patent)."""

result = agent.run(task)
print(result)
# ChemCrow autonomously calls: retrosynthesis, RDKit, SciFinder-like
# tools, reaction-prediction model — outputs a planned route + sources.

真實案例

兩個深入案例：MedQA 高分卻無法部署、本地 BioMistral 規避雲端 BAA

🌐

案例 1：Med-Gemini 91.1% MedQA，臨床部署卻被 latency 與 cost 擊敗

2024 年 Singhal 等人在 Med-Gemini 預印本上報告 MedQA-USMLE 91.1%，是當時的 SOTA。某美國醫療系統試圖把它整合到 ED triage 工作流程——預期一名急診醫師在病人 intake 時可即時得到 differential diagnosis 與建議檢查。實際部署 4 個月後，他們把 Med-Gemini 拔掉、改用「Claude 3.5 Sonnet + RAG（UpToDate、機構自家 clinical pathway 文件）」。原因有三：(1) latency 過大：Med-Gemini 在峰值時段平均回應 8–14 秒，醫師等不下去；GPT-5 / Claude 4.6 frontier 模型同樣的 prompt 約 2–4 秒。(2) cost 失控：medical fine-tune 的雲端 inference 單價是通用模型的 2.5–3 倍，每月帳單超出預算 60%。(3) 引用無法追溯：Med-Gemini 的回答無法指向具體 source，而 RAG 路徑可直接附上 UpToDate 段落引用，後者更符合 documentation 需求。啟示：MedQA benchmark 高分不等於可部署。臨床落地考慮的是 latency、cost、traceability，frontier general + RAG 在 2026 多數情境下勝出。

Case 1: Med-Gemini hits 91.1% MedQA but loses clinical deployment on latency and cost

In 2024 Singhal et al. reported MedQA-USMLE 91.1% for Med-Gemini, then SOTA. A US health system attempted to integrate it into ED triage — an ED clinician would get differential diagnoses and recommended workups at patient intake. Four months in, they ripped Med-Gemini out and switched to "Claude 3.5 Sonnet + RAG (UpToDate, institutional clinical pathways)". Three reasons: (1) Latency was too high: Med-Gemini averaged 8–14 seconds at peak, intolerable for clinicians; frontier GPT-5 / Claude 4.6 served the same prompt in 2–4 seconds. (2) Cost spiralled: cloud inference on medical fine-tunes ran 2.5–3× the cost of general models, blowing the monthly budget by 60%. (3) Citations weren't traceable: Med-Gemini answers didn't point to specific sources, while the RAG pipeline attached exact UpToDate paragraphs — much better for documentation. The lesson: high MedQA scores ≠ deployable. Clinical reality is latency, cost, traceability; frontier general + RAG wins in most 2026 settings.

🔒

案例 2：某 lab 自架 BioMistral 規避雲端 BAA 限制處理 patient genomic data

2025 年某美國醫學中心的轉譯醫學 lab 想用 LLM 自動把 patient genomic report（VCF + clinical notes 摘要）轉成可讀的家屬解釋稿。他們最初找 OpenAI Azure / Anthropic Bedrock 走 HIPAA BAA（Business Associate Agreement），但發現：(a) BAA 簽署流程要 8–12 週、(b) 每次 prompt 必須走 audit log、(c) 機構政策禁止任何 raw germline variant 上雲端，即便加密。最後他們在自家 GPU server 部署 BioMistral-7B + Apache 2.0 license，配合 LoRA fine-tune 5 萬筆內部報告（去識別化後），達成單張 A100 24GB 即可推論。在 100 份報告 blinded 評測中，遺傳諮詢師認為 BioMistral 草稿可用率 73%（vs Claude API + RAG 的 89%），但整體流程（從上線到產出第一份報告）BioMistral 路徑只花 6 週，而 Claude BAA 路徑 14 週仍未完成。啟示：在 PHI 受嚴格管制的環境下，開源領域 LLM 的價值不在於「比通用模型強」，而在於「能合法地跑」。BioMistral / MedGemma 等開源權重 + 自架是這類情境的剛需。

Case 2: A lab self-hosts BioMistral to bypass cloud BAA hurdles on patient genomic data

In 2025 a US academic medical centre's translational medicine lab wanted an LLM to auto-draft family-readable explanations from patient genomic reports (VCF + clinical note summaries). They first explored OpenAI Azure / Anthropic Bedrock with HIPAA BAA (Business Associate Agreement) but hit roadblocks: (a) BAA execution took 8–12 weeks; (b) every prompt had to pass an audit log; (c) institutional policy banned any raw germline variants from going to the cloud, even encrypted. They deployed BioMistral-7B (Apache 2.0) on their own GPU server with LoRA fine-tuning on 50k de-identified internal reports — single-A100-24GB inference. In 100 blinded reports, genetic counsellors rated BioMistral drafts usable 73% of the time (vs. 89% for Claude API + RAG), but end-to-end (zero to first report), BioMistral took 6 weeks while the Claude BAA path was still incomplete at 14 weeks. The lesson: under strict PHI rules, the value of open-source domain LLMs isn't "stronger than general" — it's "legally deployable". BioMistral / MedGemma + self-hosting is essential when the policy demands it.

常見誤區

新手用生醫 LLM 最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：把 MedQA 高分當「臨床安全」
為什麼錯：MedQA 是 multiple-choice 風格的考試題，跟「真實臨床決策」差距巨大。Singhal 2024 在 Med-PaLM 2 自己的後續評測中也承認，MedQA 分數與 holistic clinical evaluation 的相關性僅 r = 0.4–0.6。換言之，91% MedQA 不等於 91% 的臨床安全率。
正確做法：除了 MedQA，要看 holistic clinical evaluation（HumanRater 評分含 helpfulness、harm 風險、引用準確）、specialty-specific benchmark（如 NEJM AI 的 case challenges）、以及 simulated clinical scenarios。任何 deployment 都要 IRB + clinical validation。
Pitfall 1: Treating high MedQA as "clinical safety"
Why wrong: MedQA is multiple-choice exam material, far from real clinical decisions. Singhal 2024's own Med-PaLM 2 follow-up admits that MedQA scores correlate only r = 0.4–0.6 with holistic clinical evaluation. 91% MedQA ≠ 91% clinical safety.
Fix: beyond MedQA, look at holistic clinical evaluation (HumanRater on helpfulness, harm risk, citation accuracy), specialty-specific benchmarks (NEJM AI case challenges), and simulated clinical scenarios. Any deployment requires IRB + clinical validation.
⚠️
誤區 2：以為 domain fine-tune 一定贏通用 LLM + RAG
為什麼錯：2023 年 Med-PaLM 2 的 76→86% 跳躍曾被視為 fine-tune 的勝利；但到了 2026 年，GPT-5、Claude 4.6 等 frontier 通用模型在 MedQA 已達 91–93%，加 RAG（PubMed、UpToDate）後在 holistic evaluation 上常常超過 Med-Gemini。原因是 frontier 模型推理能力 + RAG 提供即時證據 + 引用可追溯，比靜態 fine-tune 更靈活。
正確做法：選型時跑你的真實任務（不是 MedQA）做 head-to-head：通用 + RAG vs 領域 fine-tune。若差異 < 5% 且 RAG 路徑 latency / cost / traceability 更好，選 RAG。
Pitfall 2: Assuming domain fine-tune always beats general LLM + RAG
Why wrong: Med-PaLM 2's 76→86% jump in 2023 looked like a fine-tune victory; but by 2026 GPT-5 / Claude 4.6 hit 91–93% MedQA, and with RAG (PubMed, UpToDate) they often beat Med-Gemini on holistic evaluation. Frontier reasoning + RAG fresh evidence + traceable citations outpaces static fine-tune.
Fix: when selecting, run your real task (not MedQA) head-to-head: general + RAG vs domain fine-tune. If the gap is <5% and RAG wins on latency / cost / traceability, pick RAG.
⚠️
誤區 3：用 BioGPT 處理當代醫療指引
為什麼錯：BioGPT 的 training cutoff 在 2021–2022。對於 2024 後的 ADA 糖尿病指引、ACC 心血管指引、NCCN 腫瘤指引、SSC sepsis bundle 2024 update 等，模型完全不知道。它仍會「自信地」生成過時的劑量、cutoff、treatment line。
正確做法：時效性敏感任務（臨床指引、新藥、新試驗）必用通用 frontier LLM + RAG（指向最新版指引文件），絕不用 BioGPT。BioGPT 適合的場景是「歷史文獻探勘、老 PubMed corpus 上的 NER / RE 預訓練起點」。
Pitfall 3: Using BioGPT on current medical guidelines
Why wrong: BioGPT's cutoff is 2021–2022. The 2024 ADA diabetes guideline, ACC cardiovascular guideline, NCCN oncology guideline, SSC sepsis bundle 2024 update — it knows none of these. It still "confidently" outputs stale doses, cutoffs, treatment lines.
Fix: time-sensitive tasks (clinical guidelines, new drugs, new trials) require frontier general LLM + RAG (pointed at the latest guideline docs). Never use BioGPT for these. BioGPT is fine for historical literature mining and as a starting point for NER / RE on older PubMed corpora.
⚠️
誤區 4：在弱 base model 上做 domain fine-tune
為什麼錯：fine-tune 不會「無中生有」新的推理能力——若 base 模型本身 reasoning 弱，繼續訓練醫療文本只會放大 base 的限制。某團隊 2024 嘗試在 Llama 2 7B 上做醫療 SFT，MedQA 提升 6%，但 USMLE simulated clinical reasoning 反而下降——因為模型開始 overfit medical jargon，丟失 base 的 chain-of-thought 能力。
正確做法：選 fine-tune base 時，先看 base 在通用 reasoning benchmark（MMLU、GPQA、BBH）的表現；base 不行就不要 fine-tune。BioMistral 之所以可用，正是因為 Mistral-7B 本身就是當時開源中最強的 base。
Pitfall 4: Domain fine-tuning a weak base model
Why wrong: fine-tuning doesn't conjure new reasoning — if the base's reasoning is weak, continued medical training just amplifies the limits. A 2024 team tried medical SFT on Llama 2 7B; MedQA rose 6%, but USMLE simulated clinical reasoning fell — the model over-fit medical jargon and lost the base's chain-of-thought ability.
Fix: when picking a base for fine-tuning, check its general reasoning benchmarks (MMLU, GPQA, BBH) first; if the base is weak, don't fine-tune at all. BioMistral works because Mistral-7B was the strongest open base at the time.
⚠️
誤區 5：ChemCrow 沒接 lab safety database 就跑合成規劃
為什麼錯：ChemCrow agent 能自主規劃合成路徑，但原版 18 工具不含 lab safety check（如 PubChem hazard、SDS、危險組合警告）。Bran et al. 2024 自己也承認，未經安全過濾的 plan 偶爾會建議「危險中間體」或「不應同 pot 反應的試劑組」。已有討論在 social media 對此提出倫理警示。
正確做法：部署 ChemCrow 或自家 chemistry agent 時，必加：(a) PubChem GHS hazard lookup、(b) DEA controlled substance check、(c) 機構 EHS rule 的 retrieval layer、(d) human-in-the-loop 在執行任何 wet-lab 前審核 plan。沒有 safety layer 不要讓 agent 自動下單試劑。
Pitfall 5: Running ChemCrow synthesis planning without lab safety hooks
Why wrong: ChemCrow autonomously plans syntheses, but the original 18-tool suite has no lab safety check (no PubChem hazard / SDS / dangerous-combo warnings). Bran et al. 2024 acknowledge unfiltered plans occasionally propose hazardous intermediates or incompatible co-reactants. Social-media ethics threads have flagged this.
Fix: when deploying ChemCrow or your own chemistry agent, you must add: (a) PubChem GHS hazard lookup, (b) DEA controlled-substance check, (c) institutional EHS retrieval layer, (d) human-in-the-loop review before any wet-lab execution. Don't let an agent auto-order reagents without a safety layer.

進階細節

🔬 Domain fine-tune ROI、Singhal 2024、ChemCrow 18 工具架構、PubMedBERT vs BERT for NER

① Domain fine-tune ROI 計算框架。什麼時候 fine-tune 值得？簡單公式：ROI = (Δaccuracy × use_count × value_per_correct) − (training_cost + maintenance_cost)。在 2023 年 Med-PaLM 2 時代，Δaccuracy ≈ 10pp、use_count 大（USMLE benchmark）、value_per_correct 高（醫療 stakes）、frontier base 還不夠強，所以 fine-tune ROI 為正。2026 年三個變數都變了：frontier 通用 LLM 已達 91%+，Δaccuracy 縮到 1–3pp；medical RAG corpus 公開可得，等同 free maintenance；雲端 inference 比 fine-tune 自架便宜。除非有 PHI / on-prem / 特殊工具呼叫的剛需，domain fine-tune ROI 通常為負。

② Singhal 2024 Med-PaLM 2 評測限制。該論文（Nat Med）開創性地引入 human rater 評估 helpfulness、harm、bias、reasoning depth 等多維 quality。但同篇也承認三大限制：(a) rater agreement 不高，physician raters 之間的 inter-rater Cohen's κ 約 0.4–0.6（中度一致），意味著「人類專家自己都不太同意答案好不好」；(b) case 多為簡單病例，缺乏 ICU multi-organ failure、罕病、文化敏感等情境；(c) 沒測 longitudinal patient interaction，只是 single-turn QA。這三個限制在 2024–2026 多項後續評測（Med-Gemini、MedGemma）也未充分解決。

③ ChemCrow 18 工具 architecture（Bran 2024）。ChemCrow 是 LangChain ReAct agent，GPT-4 為 reasoning engine，18 個工具大致分四類：(1) 分子處理 — RDKit canonicalisation、SMILES validation、molecular descriptor calculator；(2) 反應預測 — IBM RXN reaction prediction、Molecular Transformer for forward synthesis；(3) 逆合成 retrosynthesis — AiZynthFinder、ASKCOS；(4) 文獻 / 專利檢索 — Web of Science wrapper、PatBase。Agent 流程：given task → reason about which tool to call → call → observe result → continue reasoning → next tool → … → final plan。已知 limitation：缺 safety layer、缺 cost-aware optimisation、缺 stereochemistry-aware retrosynthesis。後續 Coscientist（Boiko 2023 Nature）擴充到自動執行 wet-lab，引爆 dual-use 倫理討論。

④ PubMedBERT vs general BERT 對 NER 的差異。Gu et al. 2021 的核心發現：在 domain-specific token 上，從零訓的 in-domain BERT（PubMedBERT）勝過從通用 BERT continued 訓練（BioBERT）。原因：vocabulary 不同——通用 BERT 用 WordPiece 把醫療術語切得很碎（"acetaminophen" → "ace ##tam ##ino ##phen"），即便繼續訓練，token boundary 已固定；PubMedBERT 重新建 vocab，"acetaminophen" 可能成為單一 token。在 BC5CDR、ChemProt、JNLPBA 等 entity-level F1 上，PubMedBERT 比 BioBERT 高 1–4pp，比 general BERT 高 5–8pp。這對 NER / RE 是關鍵差異；但對 generation 任務，由於需要對齊 general world knowledge，純 in-domain BERT 反而不利，這也是 BioGPT（從通用 GPT-2 繼續）的設計理由。

① An ROI framework for domain fine-tuning. When is fine-tuning worth it? Rough formula: ROI = (Δaccuracy × use_count × value_per_correct) − (training_cost + maintenance_cost). In 2023 Med-PaLM 2 era, Δaccuracy ≈ 10pp, use_count high (USMLE benchmark), value_per_correct high (clinical stakes), and frontier bases weren't strong enough — so fine-tune ROI was positive. By 2026 all three change: frontier general LLMs hit 91%+, Δaccuracy shrinks to 1–3pp; medical RAG corpora are openly available = free maintenance; cloud inference is cheaper than self-hosted fine-tunes. Unless PHI / on-prem / specialised tool-use is a hard requirement, fine-tune ROI is usually negative.

② Singhal 2024 Med-PaLM 2 evaluation limits. The Nat Med paper pioneered human-rater scoring on helpfulness, harm, bias, reasoning depth — but also openly admits three limits: (a) rater agreement is modest: physician inter-rater Cohen's κ ≈ 0.4–0.6 (moderate), i.e., human experts don't fully agree on what counts as a good answer; (b) cases skew easy, lacking ICU multi-organ failure, rare disease, cultural sensitivity scenarios; (c) no longitudinal patient interaction — single-turn QA only. These limits remained largely unaddressed in 2024–2026 follow-up evaluations of Med-Gemini and MedGemma.

③ ChemCrow 18-tool architecture (Bran 2024). ChemCrow is a LangChain ReAct agent with GPT-4 as the reasoning engine and 18 tools in roughly four categories: (1) molecule handling — RDKit canonicalisation, SMILES validation, molecular descriptor calculator; (2) reaction prediction — IBM RXN, Molecular Transformer for forward synthesis; (3) retrosynthesis — AiZynthFinder, ASKCOS; (4) literature / patent search — Web of Science wrapper, PatBase. Loop: task → reason which tool → call → observe → reason → next tool → ... → final plan. Known limits: no safety layer, no cost-aware optimisation, no stereochemistry-aware retrosynthesis. The follow-up Coscientist (Boiko 2023 Nature) added wet-lab execution and ignited dual-use ethics debates.

④ PubMedBERT vs general BERT for NER. Gu et al. 2021's core finding: on domain-specific tokens, from-scratch in-domain BERT (PubMedBERT) beats continued-from-general BERT (BioBERT). Why: vocabulary — general BERT's WordPiece shreds medical terms ("acetaminophen" → "ace ##tam ##ino ##phen") and continued training can't fix the fixed boundary; PubMedBERT rebuilds vocab, possibly keeping "acetaminophen" as one token. On BC5CDR, ChemProt, JNLPBA entity-level F1, PubMedBERT beats BioBERT by 1–4pp and general BERT by 5–8pp. This matters for NER / RE; for generation, however, pure in-domain BERT hurts because you need general world knowledge — hence BioGPT continued from general GPT-2.

Q1: domain fine-tune 在 GPT-5 / Claude 4.6 時代還必要嗎？

2023 年 Med-PaLM 2 證明 fine-tune 可行；但 2026 frontier 通用模型已逼近其 MedQA 分數，且 RAG 路徑提供 traceable citations。某 PI 主張：「未來 5 年所有醫療任務都該走通用 + RAG，fine-tune 是浪費」；另一 PI 反對：「PHI / 邊緣裝置 / 客製化評估需求永遠存在」。請思考：你會在什麼條件下還選 fine-tune？「便宜的 fine-tune（LoRA + 7B）」與「貴的通用 API」的決策邊界在哪？學術 vs 工業界的答案會一樣嗎？

Q1: Is domain fine-tune still necessary in the GPT-5 / Claude 4.6 era?

2023 Med-PaLM 2 proved fine-tuning works; by 2026 frontier general LLMs match its MedQA and RAG offers traceable citations. One PI argues "all medical tasks should go general + RAG, fine-tune is wasted effort"; another counters: "PHI / edge devices / custom evaluation needs will always exist". Consider: under what conditions would you still pick fine-tune? Where's the boundary between "cheap fine-tune (LoRA + 7B)" and "expensive general API"? Will academia and industry answer the same?

Q2: 醫療 LLM 該對誰開放權重？

MedGemma 1.5 以 custom license 釋出（不完全 free，禁止某些 commercial use）；BioMistral 是 Apache 2.0 完全自由；Med-Gemini 只給 API。某團體主張「醫療 LLM 應 100% 開源，因為這關乎健康公平」；另一團體反對：「開源 medical LLM 可能被用於詐騙、假處方、misinformation，需要 license 與 access control」。請思考：你支持什麼 license 模式？「研究用免費、商用要 license」是合理折衷嗎？非英語 / 低資源國家在這場辯論中該如何安排？

Q2: To whom should medical LLM weights be released?

MedGemma 1.5 ships under a custom license (not fully free, restricts some commercial use); BioMistral is Apache 2.0; Med-Gemini is API-only. One group argues "medical LLMs should be 100% open because health equity is at stake"; another says "open weights can fuel scams, fake prescriptions, and misinformation, so license and access control are needed". Consider: which license model do you support? Is "research free, commercial licensed" a reasonable middle ground? How should non-English / low-resource countries figure in this debate?

Q3: ChemCrow 自主合成規劃該不該強制安全層？

ChemCrow 原版 18 工具沒有 lab safety check；Coscientist（Boiko 2023）甚至能自動下單試劑、控制 wet-lab 機械手臂。某社群提案「所有 chemistry agent 強制接 PubChem GHS / DEA / EHS 過濾層，否則不應發表」；某 author 反對：「研究論文若要求 production-grade safety，會阻礙開放科學」。請思考：學術 paper 中的 agent 該負哪些安全責任？dual-use（如毒物、化武前驅物）資訊該被 hard-coded 過濾嗎？誰來監督？

Q3: Should ChemCrow-style synthesis agents be required to ship a safety layer?

ChemCrow's 18 tools lack a lab safety check; Coscientist (Boiko 2023) even auto-orders reagents and drives wet-lab robotics. A community proposal: "every chemistry agent must integrate PubChem GHS / DEA / EHS filters, or it shouldn't be published"; authors push back: "requiring production-grade safety in research papers chills open science". Consider: what safety duties do paper-stage agents carry? Should dual-use info (toxins, CW precursors) be hard-coded out? Who oversees this?

Q4: 病人能否要求「我的資料不要被 medical LLM 用」？

某醫院打算把去識別化 EHR 用於 fine-tune 內部 LLM。病人組織抗議：「即便去識別化，學習出來的模型仍由我的資料形塑，我有 opt-out 權」；機構回應：「IRB 已通過、HIPAA 已合規，opt-out 會嚴重削弱模型品質」。請思考：你支持哪邊？病人對自身資料的「informational autonomy」邊界在哪？對非營利 vs 商業 fine-tune 該用不同標準嗎？這跟 GDPR right to be forgotten 又該如何接軌？

Q4: Should patients be able to opt-out of medical-LLM training?

A hospital plans to fine-tune an internal LLM on de-identified EHR. Consider: which side do you support? Where are the limits of "informational autonomy" over one's own data? Should non-profit vs commercial fine-tunes face different standards? How does this interact with GDPR's right to be forgotten?
