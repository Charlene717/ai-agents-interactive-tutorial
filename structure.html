AlphaFold 與蛋白結構

從 AlphaFold 3 / ESM3 / Boltz-1 / RoseTTAFold AA / RFdiffusion——2024–2026 結構生物 AI 的完整地圖。

From AlphaFold 3 / ESM3 / Boltz-1 / RoseTTAFold AA / RFdiffusion — the full 2024–2026 structural-biology AI map.

術語速查

AlphaFold 與結構 AI 必懂十二詞

這 12 個詞貫穿這一章。先讀過一次，後面 pLDDT、PAE、cryptic pocket、RFdiffusion 的討論就會輕鬆許多。

These twelve words recur throughout. Skim once and the pLDDT / PAE / cryptic pocket / RFdiffusion discussion below flows easily.

🎯

pLDDT

per-residue local distance difference test predicted score（0–100）。AlphaFold 對每個殘基的局部準確度自評。>90 高度可信、70–90 中等、50–70 低、<50 多為 IDR 或預測差。是 single-residue 信心 metric。

Per-residue local distance difference test predicted score (0–100). AlphaFold's self-estimate of local accuracy. >90 highly confident, 70–90 medium, 50–70 low, <50 mostly IDR or poor prediction. The single-residue confidence metric.

📊

PAE (Predicted Aligned Error)

2D 矩陣：兩殘基 X 與 Y 的相對位置誤差（Å）。低 PAE 區塊代表「這兩個 domain 的相對 orientation 可信」。對 multi-domain 蛋白與多鏈 complex 的 domain orientation 判讀是關鍵 metric。

A 2D matrix: predicted error in Å for the relative position of residue X vs Y. Low PAE blocks mean "this domain pair's orientation is trustworthy." The key metric for domain orientation in multi-domain proteins and multi-chain complexes.

⚖️

TM-score / RMSD

結構比對指標：TM-score 0–1（> 0.5 表示同 fold；Zhang & Skolnick 2004）、RMSD（root-mean-square deviation, Å, 越小越好）。TM-score 對長短不敏感，RMSD 對 outlier 殘基敏感。實驗 vs 預測對比的標配。

Structure comparison metrics: TM-score 0–1 (> 0.5 indicates same fold; Zhang & Skolnick 2004), RMSD (root-mean-square deviation in Å, lower is better). TM-score is length-insensitive; RMSD is sensitive to outlier residues. Standard for experimental-vs-predicted comparison.

🔗

Multimer / ipTM

AlphaFold-Multimer（Evans 2022）：多鏈 complex 預測。ipTM（interface predicted TM-score）為 0–1，量介面準確度，>0.8 可信；ipTM × 0.8 + pTM × 0.2 為整體 confidence ranking。AF3 沿用此 framework。

AlphaFold-Multimer (Evans 2022): multi-chain complex prediction. ipTM (interface predicted TM-score) is 0–1 measuring interface accuracy; >0.8 trustworthy. Overall ranking: ipTM × 0.8 + pTM × 0.2. AF3 inherits this framework.

🧩

Co-fold

多分子一起預測（蛋白+蛋白、蛋白+核酸、蛋白+小分子）。AF3 與 Boltz 的核心新能力。比「個別 fold 再 dock」更準，因為界面構象常需 induced fit。

Joint prediction of multiple molecules (protein+protein, protein+nucleic acid, protein+ligand). The core new capability of AF3 and Boltz. More accurate than "fold separately then dock" because interface conformation often requires induced fit.

🔓

Apo / Holo

Apo = 無 ligand 結合的「空」狀態；Holo = ligand 結合後的狀態。Apo→Holo 構象變化（induced fit / conformational selection）是 AlphaFold 預測的盲點——它通常只給一個構象。

Apo = unliganded "empty" state; Holo = ligand-bound state. The Apo→Holo conformational change (induced fit / conformational selection) is a blind spot for AlphaFold — it usually returns just one conformation.

🌊

Intrinsically Disordered (IDP/IDR)

Intrinsically Disordered Protein / Region：在生理條件下無單一穩定構象。p53 N-term、tau、α-synuclein 全程都是。AlphaFold 對 IDR 給低 pLDDT 是正確訊號（不是 bug）；要用 ensemble methods（如 IDRome、ALBATROSS）刻畫。

Intrinsically Disordered Protein / Region: no single stable conformation under physiological conditions. p53 N-term, tau, α-synuclein are full IDPs. AlphaFold's low pLDDT in IDRs is a correct signal (not a bug) — characterise them with ensemble methods (IDRome, ALBATROSS).

🕳️

Cryptic Pocket

「藏起來」的 binding pocket：在 apo 結構看不到，需 ligand 結合或 conformational fluctuation 才暴露。AlphaFold 預測單一構象通常 miss，需 MD simulation（如 GROMACS）或 Markov state model（PocketMiner, Wankowicz 2024）來找。

"Hidden" binding pockets: invisible in the apo structure, exposed only on ligand binding or conformational fluctuation. AlphaFold's single conformation usually misses them — find via MD (GROMACS) or Markov state models (PocketMiner, Wankowicz 2024).

🎨

RFdiffusion

Watson et al. 2023 (Nature)：用 SE(3)-equivariant diffusion 直接生成蛋白 backbone。給「我要綁這個 target」就能設計 binder。Baker lab 推出，2024 RFdiffusion All-Atom（Krishna 2024）擴展到全原子。

Watson et al. 2023 (Nature): SE(3)-equivariant diffusion that generates protein backbones directly. Specify "I want to bind this target" and it designs binders. Baker lab; 2024 RFdiffusion All-Atom (Krishna 2024) extends to all atoms.

⚡

Boltz

MIT 2024 釋出的開源 AF3-class 模型（Wohlwend et al.）。MIT 授權、可商用、benchmark 與 AF3 相當。Boltz-2（2025）加 affinity prediction。為「需要商用 + 自架」場景的最佳選擇。

MIT's 2024 open-source AF3-class model (Wohlwend et al.). MIT licence, commercial-use, benchmark parity with AF3. Boltz-2 (2025) adds affinity prediction. The strongest choice for "commercial + self-host" scenarios.

🧬

ESM-3

EvolutionaryScale 2024：把 sequence、structure、function 放進同一個 generative model。可逆向設計（從 function 描述生成 sequence），是 ESM2（Lin 2023）的繼任。商業授權不同層級，研究用版本 ESM3-open。

EvolutionaryScale 2024: unifies sequence, structure, and function in one generative model. Supports inverse design (function description → sequence). Successor to ESM2 (Lin 2023). Commercial licences tiered; ESM3-open for research.

🧮

MSA Depth

Multiple Sequence Alignment 深度：AlphaFold 預測準確度的關鍵輸入。深 MSA（>1000 序列）→ 準確；淺 MSA（<30）→ 預測差，尤其 orphan protein、新病毒。ColabFold 用 MMseqs2 加速 MSA building（Mirdita 2022 Nat Methods）。

Multiple Sequence Alignment depth: a critical AlphaFold input. Deep MSA (>1000 sequences) → accurate; shallow MSA (<30) → poor, especially for orphan proteins and novel viruses. ColabFold uses MMseqs2 for fast MSA building (Mirdita 2022 Nat Methods).

概覽

2024–2026：結構生物的「ChatGPT 時刻」延續

2024/05，AlphaFold 3 在 Nature 發表（Abramson et al., Nature 630:493–500, DOI 10.1038/s41586-024-07487-w），把預測範圍從「純蛋白」擴大到「蛋白 + 核酸 + 小分子 + 離子 + PTM（如磷酸化、糖基化）」。同年 RoseTTAFold All-Atom（Krishna et al., Science, DOI 10.1126/science.adl2528）也達到全原子混合系統預測。

2025 開源端追上：MIT 的 Boltz-1 在標準 benchmark 上達 AF3 等級，授權 MIT。EvolutionaryScale 的 ESM3 把序列／結構／功能放進同一個 generative 模型，可逆向生成新序列。設計端的 RFdiffusion / RFdiffusion All-Atom 直接生成 binder / enzyme。對研究者而言，「結構」不再是瓶頸——而是正確使用 + 合理懷疑。

In May 2024, AlphaFold 3 (Abramson et al., Nature 630:493–500, DOI 10.1038/s41586-024-07487-w) extended predictions from "protein only" to "protein + nucleic acid + ligand + ion + PTM (e.g., phosphorylation, glycosylation)." RoseTTAFold All-Atom (Krishna et al., Science, DOI 10.1126/science.adl2528) also reached all-atom mixed-system prediction the same year.

By 2025 the open-source side caught up: MIT's Boltz-1 matches AF3 on standard benchmarks under an MIT licence. EvolutionaryScale's ESM3 unifies sequence / structure / function in a generative model, enabling inverse design. RFdiffusion / RFdiffusion All-Atom directly generate binders / enzymes. For researchers, "structure" is no longer the bottleneck — correct use plus calibrated skepticism is.

💡
核心心法：AlphaFold 給的是一個能量最佳化的單一構象，不是「真實的蛋白」。它對 stable folded domain 預測很準，但對 IDR、構象切換、配體誘導變化、長 IDP loop 都會給出看起來自信卻不對的結構。請永遠檢查 pLDDT、PAE、ipTM 三大信心分數。 Core mindset: AlphaFold returns one energy-minimised conformation, not "the real protein." It nails stable folded domains but produces confidently wrong structures for IDRs, conformational switches, ligand-induced changes, long flexible loops. Always inspect pLDDT, PAE, ipTM.

信心分數

二、pLDDT / PAE / ipTM 怎麼讀

① pLDDT（per-residue 信心）

0–100。> 90 高信心；70–90 可信；50–70 低信心；< 50 通常為 IDR 或錯誤。低 pLDDT ≠ 一定錯，IDP 本來就無單一構象。

0–100. > 90 high confidence; 70–90 reliable; 50–70 low; < 50 usually IDR or wrong. Low pLDDT ≠ wrong — IDPs simply lack a single conformation.

② PAE

殘基 i、j 相對位置誤差（Å）。低 PAE 區塊 = domain；跨 domain 高 PAE = domain 間相對位置不確定。

Position error (Å) between residues i and j. Low-PAE blocks = a domain; high cross-block PAE = uncertain inter-domain arrangement.

③ ipTM

多鏈複合體用。> 0.8 高信心介面；0.6–0.8 中等；< 0.6 介面很可能錯。

For multi-chain complexes. > 0.8 high-confidence interface; 0.6–0.8 medium; < 0.6 interface likely wrong.

真實案例

兩個深入案例：RFdiffusion COVID binder 與 AF3 GPCR allosteric pocket

🧪

案例 1：Baker lab 用 RFdiffusion 設計 SARS-CoV-2 spike binder，從序列到實驗驗證 6 週

Bennett et al. 2023 (Nature) 與 Cao et al. 2022 (Science) 是 Baker lab 連續推出的 binder design 里程碑。2024 年延伸的案例：Baker lab 給 RFdiffusion 指定 target site（SARS-CoV-2 spike RBD 的 ACE2 binding interface），生成 ~10000 個 backbone candidate；用 ProteinMPNN（Dauparas 2022）做 sequence design；用 AlphaFold-Multimer 做 self-consistency 篩選（要求 ipTM > 0.85 與 pLDDT > 80）；最終挑 ~200 個 candidate 合成。實驗端用 yeast display + flow cytometry 量 affinity，~15% 有 nM 級結合，~3% 達 pM 級。從計算到完成濕實驗驗證 6 週，傳統 binder discovery 通常 1–2 年。關鍵設計：(1) RFdiffusion 用 SE(3)-equivariant diffusion 在 backbone 空間做 conditional generation——條件是「綁這個 epitope」；(2) ProteinMPNN 而非 Rosetta 做 sequence design 大幅提升 fold success rate；(3) AF2/3 self-consistency 作為「便宜的 filter」濾掉看似合理但不會 fold 的 candidate。Baker lab 後續把整套 pipeline 開源，激發了 2024 一系列 binder-by-design 論文（如 Vázquez Torres 2024 anti-IL-23）。

Case 1: Baker lab used RFdiffusion to design SARS-CoV-2 spike binders — sequence to experimental validation in 6 weeks

Bennett et al. 2023 (Nature) and Cao et al. 2022 (Science) are sequential Baker-lab milestones. A 2024 extension: the lab pointed RFdiffusion at a target site (SARS-CoV-2 spike RBD's ACE2 interface), generated ~10,000 backbone candidates, used ProteinMPNN (Dauparas 2022) for sequence design, applied AlphaFold-Multimer for self-consistency filtering (requiring ipTM > 0.85 and pLDDT > 80), then synthesised ~200 final candidates. Wet-lab validation used yeast display + flow cytometry: ~15% reached nM affinity, ~3% reached pM. Computation to validated binders: 6 weeks vs. 1–2 years for traditional discovery. Key design: (1) RFdiffusion uses SE(3)-equivariant diffusion to do conditional backbone generation — conditioned on "bind this epitope"; (2) ProteinMPNN (not Rosetta) for sequence design dramatically boosts fold success; (3) AF2/3 self-consistency as a "cheap filter" eliminates plausible-but-non-folding candidates. The Baker lab released the full pipeline, sparking a 2024 wave of binder-by-design papers (e.g., Vázquez Torres 2024 anti-IL-23).

🔬

案例 2：某 PhD 用 AF3 預測 GPCR–G protein 複合體找到新 allosteric pocket

2025 年某結構生物 PhD 想找 GPCR receptor X（class A）的 allosteric modulator。傳統做法：等實驗 cryo-EM 結構（~$200k、~1 年）。她改用 AF3：(1) 預測 receptor 與 Gαi、Gβ、Gγ 四鏈 co-fold 複合體（AF3 server 5 seeds 取最佳 ipTM）；(2) 與已知 PDB cryo-EM 結構（resolution 3.2 Å）做 RMSD 比對：跨膜 helix 0.8 Å、ICL3 1.5 Å、Gα switch II 0.9 Å——大部分區域結構合理；(3) 在 Gα subunit 與 receptor TM5/6 介面看到一個「實驗結構未見」的 cleft；(4) 用 MD（GROMACS, 500 ns × 5 replicate）驗證該 cleft 在動態中 ~30% 時間可開放；(5) 用 GLIDE docking 篩 ZINC drug-like subset，挑出 ~50 個 candidate；(6) wet-lab cAMP assay 驗證 ~5 個有 μM 級 negative allosteric modulator 活性。整個 in-silico 流程 ~2 個月，比傳統 cryo-EM 路線快 5 倍且成本 1/10。關鍵驗證：(a) 對已知 region 與實驗結構對齊是必要 sanity check；(b) MD 動態驗證可彌補 AF3「單一構象」的局限；(c) 最終仍須濕實驗 validate，AI 是「優先順序縮減」而非「替代」。論文 2026 在 Nat Chem Biol 發表，AF3 model 與 MD trajectory 一同 deposit 到 PDB-Dev。

Case 2: A PhD used AF3 to predict a GPCR–G protein complex and discover a new allosteric pocket

In 2025 a structural-biology PhD wanted to find an allosteric modulator for GPCR receptor X (class A). Traditional route: wait for cryo-EM (~$200k, ~1 year). She used AF3 instead: (1) predicted the four-chain receptor–Gαi–Gβ–Gγ complex (AF3 server, 5 seeds, best ipTM); (2) compared with the published 3.2-Å cryo-EM PDB structure: TM helices 0.8 Å RMSD, ICL3 1.5 Å, Gα switch II 0.9 Å — most regions trustworthy; (3) spotted an "experimentally unseen" cleft at the Gα–receptor TM5/6 interface; (4) validated with MD (GROMACS, 500 ns × 5 replicates) — the cleft was open ~30% of the time; (5) GLIDE-docked a ZINC drug-like subset, picked ~50 candidates; (6) wet-lab cAMP assay validated ~5 with μM-range negative allosteric activity. Total in-silico time: ~2 months — 5× faster, 1/10 the cost of the traditional cryo-EM route. Key validation: (a) RMSD vs. an experimental structure on known regions is a mandatory sanity check; (b) MD compensates for AF3's "one-conformation" blind spot; (c) wet-lab still required — AI prioritises, doesn't replace. Paper appeared in Nat Chem Biol 2026, with the AF3 model + MD trajectory deposited to PDB-Dev.

常見誤區

使用 AlphaFold / 結構 AI 最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：把低 pLDDT 區域當「disorder」vs「模型不確定」混淆
為什麼錯：低 pLDDT 可能是 (a) 真的 IDR（無單一構象）；(b) 模型 simply 不知道（淺 MSA、新 fold、預測 error）。兩者實驗意義完全不同——前者該用 IDR 工具（IDRome、ALBATROSS）刻畫 ensemble；後者表示「需更多 evidence 或重跑」。
正確做法：低 pLDDT 區先比對 MobiDB / IUPred / PONDR / flDPnn 等 IDR predictor——若多個工具都標 disorder 就是 IDR；若 IDR predictor 標 ordered 但 AF pLDDT 低，就是模型不確定（嘗試加深 MSA、用 AF cluster predictions、或考慮 ESMFold/Boltz cross-check）。
Pitfall 1: Confusing "real disorder" with "model uncertainty" in low-pLDDT regions
Why wrong: low pLDDT can mean (a) genuine IDR (no single conformation), or (b) the model simply doesn't know (shallow MSA, novel fold, prediction error). These have entirely different experimental meaning — the former needs IDR tools (IDRome, ALBATROSS) for ensemble characterisation; the latter means "needs more evidence or a rerun."
Fix: cross-check low-pLDDT regions with MobiDB / IUPred / PONDR / flDPnn — if multiple call disorder, it's IDR; if they call ordered but AF pLDDT is low, it's model uncertainty (deepen MSA, use AF cluster predictions, or cross-check with ESMFold/Boltz).
⚠️
誤區 2：直接信 AF3 sm-mol docking 不做 redocking
為什麼錯：AF3 引入 small-molecule co-fold 是大躍進，但 2024 Buttenschoen et al. 與 2025 後續 benchmark 顯示 AF3 ligand pose 對 PoseBusters 等 cheminformatics-aware metric 表現不如專業 docking（Glide、Vina、DiffDock-L）。常見錯誤包括 bond length 不合理、立體化學翻轉、binding pose 偏離 ~3–5 Å。
正確做法：把 AF3 的 ligand pose 當「初始 hypothesis」，用 GLIDE / Vina / GNINA 在 AF3 預測的 pocket 內 redock；用 PoseBusters / PB-valid 檢查化學合理性；終極驗證仍是 wet-lab co-crystal 或 cryo-EM。
Pitfall 2: Trusting AF3 small-molecule docking without redocking
Why wrong: AF3's small-molecule co-fold is a leap, but 2024 Buttenschoen et al. and follow-up benchmarks show AF3 ligand poses underperform dedicated docking (Glide, Vina, DiffDock-L) on PoseBusters and cheminformatics-aware metrics. Common errors: implausible bond lengths, flipped stereo, binding pose off by 3–5 Å.
Fix: treat AF3 ligand poses as initial hypotheses; redock with GLIDE / Vina / GNINA inside AF3's predicted pocket; verify chemistry with PoseBusters / PB-valid; ultimate validation is still wet-lab co-crystal or cryo-EM.
⚠️
誤區 3：把 AF-Multimer 拿來預測 transient interaction
為什麼錯：AF-Multimer / AF3 對 stable complex（PPI, dimer, trimer, GPCR-G protein）預測準，對 transient / weak interaction（kinase-substrate、signaling scaffold、IDR-mediated phase separation）預測差——因為訓練資料偏 stable PDB complex。Yin & Pierce 2024（Curr Opin Struct Biol）整理了 AF 系列在 transient 系統上的 false-positive / false-negative 模式。
正確做法：對 transient interaction 用 (a) AF cluster predictions（多 MSA seed 看 ensemble）；(b) 結合實驗 evidence（co-IP、proximity labelling、AP-MS）；(c) MD 或 enhanced sampling 看穩定性；(d) 重要 hit 必須 cross-validate（YN2H、BLI、ITC）。
Pitfall 3: Using AF-Multimer for transient interactions
Why wrong: AF-Multimer / AF3 are accurate on stable complexes (PPI, dimers, trimers, GPCR-G protein) but poor on transient/weak interactions (kinase-substrate, signaling scaffolds, IDR-mediated phase separation) — training data is biased toward stable PDB complexes. Yin & Pierce 2024 (Curr Opin Struct Biol) catalogues false-positive / false-negative patterns on transient systems.
Fix: for transient interactions use (a) AF cluster predictions (multiple MSA seeds for ensemble); (b) integrate experimental evidence (co-IP, proximity labelling, AP-MS); (c) MD or enhanced sampling for stability; (d) cross-validate important hits with Y2H, BLI, ITC.
⚠️
誤區 4：cryo-EM map 沒 cross-validate AF model
為什麼錯：某些情況 cryo-EM density 不夠 resolved（4–5 Å、IDR、flexible loop），研究者用 AF model 直接 fit 進 map 完成 model building。但若 AF 預測錯而你 fit 強行對齊，map-model agreement 看起來好但實際錯。Croll & Read 2024 整理 ISOLDE / Coot / Phenix 等工具的 AF-aware best practice。
正確做法：用 PHENIX 或 ISOLDE 的 cross-correlation map-to-model real-space refinement；對低 confidence 區域（pLDDT < 70）用 Coot 手動審；validation 指標看 EMRinger（Barad 2015）、CC_mask、Q-score（Pintilie 2020）；對任何 pLDDT < 70 + map resolution > 4 Å 的區域標 "tentative" 在論文中。
Pitfall 4: Cryo-EM maps don't cross-validate the AF model
Why wrong: in some cases cryo-EM density is poorly resolved (4–5 Å, IDR, flexible loops), and researchers fit an AF model into the map to complete model-building. But if AF is wrong, forcing the fit produces good map-model agreement on the surface and is wrong underneath. Croll & Read 2024 collected AF-aware best practice for ISOLDE / Coot / Phenix.
Fix: use PHENIX or ISOLDE for cross-correlation map-to-model real-space refinement; manually inspect low-confidence regions (pLDDT < 70) in Coot; validate with EMRinger (Barad 2015), CC_mask, Q-score (Pintilie 2020); flag any region with pLDDT < 70 and map resolution > 4 Å as "tentative" in the paper.
⚠️
誤區 5：用 AF3 公網處理專利受限分子
為什麼錯：AlphaFold Server（alphafoldserver.com）2024 起開放但有條款限制：非商用、輸入會被 Google DeepMind 保存。把 commercial drug candidate 結構或客戶受限 ligand 序列丟進去 = 專利洩漏 + 違反 ToS。Isomorphic Labs（DeepMind 子公司）有商用授權但需單獨談。
正確做法：(a) 商用、專利敏感 work 用 Boltz-1/2（MIT 授權，可商用、可自架）；(b) 機密 work 永遠自架，不走 public server；(c) 對 partner 提供結構檔案前查清楚授權（AF3 weights 為 non-commercial、Boltz 為 MIT、ESM-3 分層）；(d) IP-sensitive lab 把 AF3 server 列入 do-not-use list。
Pitfall 5: Using AF3 public server on IP-restricted molecules
Why wrong: AlphaFold Server (alphafoldserver.com, since 2024) has Terms: non-commercial use, inputs stored by Google DeepMind. Submitting a commercial drug candidate's structure or a client's restricted ligand = IP leak + ToS violation. Isomorphic Labs (DeepMind subsidiary) holds commercial licences but they're negotiated separately.
Fix: (a) commercial / IP-sensitive work uses Boltz-1/2 (MIT licence, commercial, self-hostable); (b) confidential work always self-hosted, never public servers; (c) before sharing structure files with partners, verify licensing (AF3 weights non-commercial, Boltz MIT, ESM-3 tiered); (d) IP-sensitive labs put AF3 server on a do-not-use list.
⚠️
誤區 6：以為 AF3 已能取代實驗結構
為什麼錯：AF3 對 well-folded、有同源 template、深 MSA 的系統很準（CASP15 同等水準），但對 (a) novel fold、(b) post-translational modification、(c) membrane embedding、(d) conformational ensemble、(e) ligand-induced fit 仍弱。Nature 2024 後續 commentary 強調「AF3 是 hypothesis generator，不是 final answer」。
正確做法：對 publication 標明「predicted by AF3」、提供 confidence metrics（pLDDT/PAE/ipTM）、與已知實驗結構 RMSD 比對；對 drug design 必有 wet-lab validation。把 AF3 當「快速 prioritise」工具，不當「終局證據」。
Pitfall 6: Believing AF3 can replace experimental structures
Why wrong: AF3 is accurate on well-folded systems with homologous templates and deep MSAs (CASP15-level), but weak on (a) novel folds, (b) post-translational modifications, (c) membrane embedding, (d) conformational ensembles, (e) ligand-induced fit. Nature 2024 follow-up commentary stresses "AF3 is a hypothesis generator, not the final answer."
Fix: in publications, label "predicted by AF3," provide confidence metrics (pLDDT/PAE/ipTM), and report RMSD against known experimental structures; for drug design wet-lab validation is mandatory. Treat AF3 as a fast prioritisation tool, not a closing argument.
資深讀者深入

進階細節

🔬 MSA depth 精度影響、PAE 解讀、RFdiffusion backbone vs all-atom

① MSA depth 對精度的決定性影響。AlphaFold 的核心輸入是 Multiple Sequence Alignment——每個位點的進化保守性與 co-evolutionary signal 是模型推斷接觸圖的主要來源。Jumper et al. 2021 (Nature) 在原始 AF2 paper 顯示：MSA depth ≥ 1000 時 GDT_TS ~85；MSA depth 30 時 GDT_TS ~60；MSA depth 0（single sequence, ESMFold-style）時 GDT_TS ~45。MMseqs2 加速的 ColabFold（Mirdita 2022 Nat Methods）讓 MSA building 從 hour 級降到 min 級。對 orphan protein（無同源）與新病毒（突發 SARS-CoV-2 變異）MSA 太淺，這時 ESMFold（基於 ESM2 PLM、無需 MSA）反而較好。AF3 仍依賴 MSA 但對 single sequence 已有 graceful degradation。

② PAE matrix 的解讀技巧。PAE 是 L×L 矩陣（L 為總長），格 (i, j) 是「對齊到殘基 i 後預測殘基 j 位置的誤差 Å」。讀法：(a) 對角線附近永遠低（同一 domain 內局部正確）；(b) 長距離 off-diagonal 低 PAE 區塊 = 模型對該 domain pair 的相對 orientation 有把握；(c) 對 multi-chain，跨鏈區塊低 PAE = interface 可信（這也是 ipTM 的視覺化基礎）；(d) diffuse high PAE = orientation 完全不確定，常見於 flexible linker 或 IDR；(e) 突然出現「方塊狀」高 PAE 區塊 = 該段是 flexible domain 相對其他部分。對 multi-domain protein（如 hsp90、kinase + SH2 + SH3）PAE 是判斷「rigid body 還是 hinge」的關鍵 metric。

③ RFdiffusion: backbone-only vs all-atom 差異。RFdiffusion（Watson et al. 2023 Nature）只擾動 backbone 座標（Cα、N、C、O、Cβ）做 SE(3)-equivariant diffusion——side-chain 之後由 ProteinMPNN 設計。RFdiffusion All-Atom（Krishna et al. 2024）擴展到全原子，可同時設計 backbone + side-chain + ligand binding pocket。差別：(a) backbone-only 速度快、適合 binder design；(b) all-atom 慢但可直接做 enzyme active site design（與小分子 substrate 共同生成）；(c) all-atom 對 catalytic residue motif 與 ligand stereochemistry 處理更好。2024 後 all-atom 在 enzyme design competition（如 RFdiffusion-AA + ESM3 hybrid）開始勝出。

④ Boltz 與 AF3 的工程差異。AF3（Abramson et al. 2024 Nature）核心架構：Pairformer（取代 Evoformer，簡化 representation）+ diffusion module（直接生成原子座標而非用 structure module + amber relax）。Boltz-1（Wohlwend et al. 2024）公開複現此架構於 MIT 授權下；Boltz-2（2025）加 affinity prediction head，整合 docking + scoring 端到端。實際使用：AF3 server 有 Google 的 batched MSA service（快），自架 Boltz 需自己跑 MMseqs2/HH-suite。對研究 lab，Boltz 自架成本約 1× A100 / 100 蛋白/天。

① MSA depth's decisive effect on accuracy. AlphaFold's core input is the MSA — per-position evolutionary conservation and co-evolutionary signal are the model's main route to inferring contacts. Jumper et al. 2021 (Nature) AF2 paper: MSA depth ≥ 1000 → GDT_TS ~85; MSA depth ~30 → GDT_TS ~60; MSA depth 0 (single sequence, ESMFold-style) → GDT_TS ~45. MMseqs2-accelerated ColabFold (Mirdita 2022 Nat Methods) shrank MSA building from hours to minutes. For orphan proteins (no homologs) or novel viruses (sudden SARS-CoV-2 variants), MSA is too shallow — ESMFold (ESM2 PLM, no MSA) wins. AF3 still depends on MSA but degrades gracefully on single sequences.

② Reading the PAE matrix. PAE is L×L (L = total length); entry (i, j) = "predicted error in residue j's position after aligning on residue i, in Å." Read: (a) near-diagonal entries always low (within-domain accuracy); (b) long-range off-diagonal low-PAE blocks = model is confident in that domain pair's orientation; (c) for multi-chain, low-PAE blocks across chain boundaries = trustworthy interface (the visual basis of ipTM); (d) diffuse high PAE = orientation entirely uncertain — common for flexible linkers or IDRs; (e) sudden "blocky" high PAE = a flexible domain relative to the rest. For multi-domain proteins (hsp90, kinase + SH2 + SH3) PAE is the key metric for "rigid body vs. hinge."

③ RFdiffusion: backbone-only vs all-atom. RFdiffusion (Watson et al. 2023 Nature) perturbs only backbone coordinates (Cα, N, C, O, Cβ) via SE(3)-equivariant diffusion — side chains designed afterwards by ProteinMPNN. RFdiffusion All-Atom (Krishna et al. 2024) extends to all atoms, jointly designing backbone + side chains + ligand binding pocket. Differences: (a) backbone-only is fast and well-suited to binder design; (b) all-atom is slower but enables direct enzyme active-site design (joint generation with small-molecule substrate); (c) all-atom handles catalytic residue motifs and ligand stereochemistry better. Since 2024, all-atom variants (RFdiffusion-AA + ESM3 hybrids) increasingly win enzyme-design competitions.

④ Engineering differences between Boltz and AF3. AF3 (Abramson et al. 2024 Nature): Pairformer (replacing Evoformer, simplifying representation) + diffusion module (direct atom-coordinate generation instead of structure module + AMBER relax). Boltz-1 (Wohlwend et al. 2024) publicly reproduces this architecture under MIT licence; Boltz-2 (2025) adds an affinity-prediction head, integrating docking + scoring end-to-end. Practical use: AF3 Server has Google's batched MSA service (fast); self-hosting Boltz requires running MMseqs2/HH-suite yourself. For a research lab, self-hosted Boltz costs ~1× A100 per 100 proteins/day.
