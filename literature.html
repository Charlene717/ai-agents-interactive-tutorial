文獻探索 AI

Elicit · NotebookLM · Consensus · SciSpace——四個專為研究設計的 AI 工具。它們各自擅長不同任務，選對工具能省你 80% 的時間。

Elicit · NotebookLM · Consensus · SciSpace — four research-grade AI tools. Each is great at something different. Picking the right one saves 80% of your time.

術語速查

文獻探索 AI 必懂十詞

這 10 個詞會在 systematic review、semantic search、knowledge graph 的章節反覆出現。先讀過一次，後面 Elicit / NotebookLM / Consensus 的對比就會清楚許多。

These ten words recur across systematic review, semantic search, and knowledge graph discussions. Skim once and the Elicit / NotebookLM / Consensus comparisons below will read smoothly.

🕸️

Citation Network

論文之間「誰引用誰」的有向圖。可做 forward citation（你被誰引）與 backward citation（你引了誰）追蹤。OpenAlex 與 Semantic Scholar 提供完整 graph API；Google Scholar 則是封閉系統。

The directed graph of "who cites whom." Supports forward (who cited you) and backward (whom you cited) tracking. OpenAlex and Semantic Scholar expose the full graph via API; Google Scholar is closed.

🧠

Knowledge Graph

結構化的「實體-關係」圖（gene-disease、drug-target、protein-protein）。生醫 KG 包括 SemMedDB（UMLS 三元組）、Hetionet、PrimeKG。RAG + KG 是 2026 年最強組合。

A structured "entity-relation" graph (gene-disease, drug-target, protein-protein). Biomedical KGs include SemMedDB (UMLS triples), Hetionet, PrimeKG. RAG + KG is the strongest 2026 combo.

📋

Systematic Review

遵循 PRISMA 2020（Page et al. BMJ）的高嚴格度文獻綜述：predefined protocol、duplicate screening、risk-of-bias 評估、PROSPERO 註冊。一篇 SR 傳統需 6–12 個月；AI 工具壓到 2–4 週。

High-rigor literature review following PRISMA 2020 (Page et al. BMJ): predefined protocol, duplicate screening, risk-of-bias assessment, PROSPERO registration. Traditionally 6–12 months; AI tools shrink to 2–4 weeks.

🔭

Scoping Review

比 SR 寬鬆：目的是「map the field」而非綜合 effect estimate。Munn 2018 與 Arksey-O'Malley 框架。適合新興領域、policy review。AI 工具同樣加速。

Broader than SR: aims to "map the field" rather than synthesize effect estimates. Munn 2018 + Arksey-O'Malley framework. Good for emerging fields and policy reviews. AI tools help here too.

🔄

Living Review

持續更新的 SR——新證據出現即納入。COVID-19 期間流行（如 Cochrane LR），2025 起多用 AI auto-screen 維持更新頻率。

A continuously updated SR — new evidence is incorporated as it appears. Popularized in COVID-19 (Cochrane LR), and from 2025 increasingly powered by AI auto-screening to sustain cadence.

🔍

Embedding Search

「Semantic search」的別名——把 query 與 document 各自編碼為向量，用 cosine similarity 找近鄰。比 lexical（BM25）強的場合：同義詞、跨語、概念查詢。

Another name for "semantic search" — encode query and doc as vectors and find nearest neighbors by cosine. Beats lexical (BM25) for synonyms, cross-lingual, and concept queries.

🎓

Semantic Scholar

Allen Institute 出品，covers 200M+ paper、有完整 citation graph API（SPECTER embeddings、TLDR 自動摘要）。是 Elicit / SciSpace 等工具的底層資料源之一。

Allen Institute's tool covering 200M+ papers with a full citation-graph API (SPECTER embeddings, TLDR auto-summaries). The data source under many tools like Elicit / SciSpace.

🌐

OpenAlex

替代 Microsoft Academic Graph 的開源學術知識圖譜，covers 250M+ work，free API、無 rate limit（with email），含完整 citation network。比 Google Scholar 更可程式化存取。

Open-source academic knowledge graph replacing Microsoft Academic Graph. 250M+ works, free API (no rate limit with email), full citation network. More programmable than Google Scholar.

❄️

Snowballing

從 seed paper 出發追蹤 forward（誰引用了它）與 backward（它引用了誰）citation，逐輪擴展。SR 必備技術。AI 工具可自動化但仍需人類判斷 relevance。

Starting from seed papers and tracing forward (who cited it) and backward (whom it cited) citations, expanding round by round. Essential SR technique. AI can automate, but humans must judge relevance.

📚

Reference Manager

Zotero（免費）、EndNote、Mendeley、Paperpile。儲存 PDF + metadata、自動匯出 BibTeX、與 Word/Overleaf 整合。Zotero 7（2024）有強大的 plug-in 生態與 ZotFile / Better BibTeX 整合 AI。

Zotero (free), EndNote, Mendeley, Paperpile. Store PDFs + metadata, export BibTeX, integrate with Word/Overleaf. Zotero 7 (2024) has a rich plug-in ecosystem (ZotFile, Better BibTeX, AI integrations).

概覽

為什麼 PubMed + 通用 LLM 不夠？

PubMed 與 Google Scholar 解決「我能找到 paper 嗎」，但研究實務上你常需要更深一層：(1) 跨多篇 paper 抽取證據（例如 systematic review screen 500 篇後做 evidence map）；(2) 嚴格 grounded 的回答（不要任何幻覺，且要每句話可追到 source page）；(3) 共識視圖（這 30 篇 paper 對某假設投 yes/no 各幾票）；(4) 非結構化詢問（用自然語言問「給我所有 2024 後 CKD 的 GLP-1 trial」）。

2026 年有四個工具各自擅長一塊：Elicit（systematic review 規模化、表格抽取）、NotebookLM（強 grounding、podcast 與 study guide）、Consensus（共識 yes/no/mixed）、SciSpace（自然語言搜尋 + 自動產生比較表）。選錯工具的代價：用 Elicit 跑 podcast、用 NotebookLM 做 meta-analysis、用 Consensus 找深度評論——都會浪費 ½ 天時間。

PubMed + Scholar answer "can I find a paper?" Research often needs more: (1) cross-paper evidence extraction (e.g., screen 500 papers, build an evidence map); (2) strict grounding (no hallucination, every claim traceable to a page); (3) consensus view (how many of 30 papers support hypothesis X?); (4) natural-language queries ("all post-2024 GLP-1 trials in CKD").

By 2026, four tools each own a niche: Elicit (review at scale, table extraction), NotebookLM (rock-solid grounding, podcasts, study guides), Consensus (yes/no/mixed consensus), SciSpace (natural-language search + auto comparison tables). Mismatch costs you half a day every time.

💡
核心心法：把這四個工具看成專科科室：Elicit 是「systematic review 加護病房」，NotebookLM 是「教學門診」，Consensus 是「公衛統計室」，SciSpace 是「急診快速分流」。看任務找科室。 Core mindset: Treat the four as specialty clinics: Elicit = systematic-review ICU; NotebookLM = teaching outpatient; Consensus = epi/statistics desk; SciSpace = ER triage. Match task to clinic.
工具核心

四大文獻 AI 速覽

📊

Elicit

2026 年版整合 200M+ paper，最強的「跨論文證據表格」工具。可自動 screen abstract、抽 outcome、cohort size、follow-up。Pro $20/月，學術 enterprise 規模化跑 systematic review。

2026 release covers 200M+ papers. Best for cross-paper evidence tables — auto-screen abstracts, extract outcomes, cohort size, follow-up. Pro $20/mo; enterprise scale for systematic reviews.

📓

NotebookLM

Google 出品，上傳最多 300 個來源（PDF、URL、Google Docs、YouTube），所有回答都嚴格 grounded 並附頁碼。獨家：自動 podcast、study guide、briefing doc。免費 + 付費。

From Google. Upload up to 300 sources (PDF, URL, Docs, YouTube). Every answer is grounded and cites page numbers. Unique: auto-podcast, study guide, briefing doc. Free + paid tiers.

🗳️

Consensus

把學界對某問題的立場匯總為 Yes / No / Possibly 投票條，附原 paper 連結。適合快速感受「這個假設目前共識在哪」。免費 + Pro $9/月。

Aggregates the field's stance on a question into Yes/No/Possibly tallies linked to source papers. Great for sensing where the consensus sits. Free + Pro $9/mo.

🔭

SciSpace

自然語言搜尋 + 自動生成比較表（columns 可自訂：method、N、results、limitations）。也有 chat-with-PDF。Pro $20/月，是 SR 工作流的快速分流。

Natural-language search + auto comparison tables (custom columns: method, N, results, limitations). Plus chat-with-PDF. Pro $20/mo. ER-triage of an SR workflow.

真實案例

兩個真實流程：systematic review 與 podcast 學習

🔬

案例 1：PRISMA 2020 + Elicit 把 2 個月 systematic review 壓到 2 週

某 cardiology 博後團隊（PI + 2 博後 + 1 RA）想做「SGLT2 inhibitors 在 HFpEF 的 RCT 整合」systematic review。傳統流程：(1) PROSPERO 註冊 1 週、(2) PubMed + Embase + Cochrane 搜尋字串設計 1 週、(3) 1,200 篇 abstract 雙人 screen 4 週、(4) 80 篇 full-text 雙人 review + risk-of-bias（Cochrane RoB 2）2 週、(5) data extraction + meta-analysis（RevMan）2 週——共 12 週。AI-augmented 流程：(1) protocol 與搜尋字串用 Claude 起草 + 人工修訂 2 天、(2) Elicit screen 1,200 abstracts（其中 1 人複核 AI 高 confidence excluded，PI 複核 borderline）3 天、(3) 80 篇 full-text 用 NotebookLM 上傳 + page-cited 抽取 outcome / N / CI 4 天、(4) RoB 2 各自做但用 Claude 草擬 justification 3 天、(5) meta-analysis 與 forest plot 用 R metafor 3 天——共 15 天（2 週）。關鍵警示：所有 AI 輸出仍須人類雙重 verify；PRISMA-AI 2024 草案要求附 AI 揭露與 verification log。團隊在 supplement 完整記載每篇被 AI 判定 exclude 的論文，PI 二次抽查 5%。最終投 JAMA Cardiology 接受，reviewer 特別讚賞「AI Use Statement 的透明度」。

Case 1: PRISMA 2020 + Elicit shrank a 2-month systematic review to 2 weeks

A cardiology team (PI + 2 postdocs + 1 RA) ran a systematic review on "SGLT2 inhibitors in HFpEF RCTs." Traditional pipeline: (1) PROSPERO registration 1 wk, (2) PubMed/Embase/Cochrane search string design 1 wk, (3) dual-screen 1,200 abstracts 4 wks, (4) dual review of 80 full-texts + RoB 2 weeks, (5) extraction + meta-analysis (RevMan) 2 wks — total 12 wks. AI-augmented: (1) protocol + search strings drafted by Claude, human-revised, 2 days; (2) Elicit screened 1,200 abstracts (one reviewer re-checked high-confidence excludes, PI checked borderlines) 3 days; (3) 80 full-texts uploaded to NotebookLM for page-cited extraction of outcome/N/CI, 4 days; (4) RoB 2 done independently but Claude drafted justifications, 3 days; (5) meta-analysis + forest plot in R metafor, 3 days — total 15 days (2 wks). Critical caveat: every AI output is human-verified; PRISMA-AI 2024 (draft) requires AI disclosure + verification log. The team supplemented every AI-excluded paper; the PI spot-audited 5%. The paper was accepted at JAMA Cardiology; reviewers explicitly praised the "transparency of the AI Use Statement."

🎧

案例 2：NotebookLM podcast 模式讓 PI 通勤路上吸收 5 篇論文

某 30-40 歲的 mid-career PI 每天通勤 45 分鐘 × 2 趟，過去這段時間只能聽 podcast 或音樂——完全無法用於專業學習。2024 年 9 月 Google 釋出 NotebookLM 的 Audio Overview 功能（兩位 AI host 對談式 podcast，每集 15–25 分鐘），他開始嘗試：每週日晚上把 5–10 篇 saved-up paper（含 NEJM、JAMA、Circulation、JCO 各週重點）上傳到 NotebookLM，產生一集 podcast，週一到週五通勤時聽。一個月後他告訴 lab：「我現在通勤時間能 cover 過去要花 lab meeting 才看的論文，實質研究產出時間多了 6–8 小時/週」。他同時強調：(a) podcast 只用於 high-level grasp，深讀仍須回原文；(b) AI host 偶爾過度簡化，他會在 NotebookLM 中追問 page-cited 細節；(c) 不適合「需要看 figure 的 paper」——這時改用車上 NotebookLM 文字 mode。教訓：AI 不只是「比較快的閱讀」，而是「把過去無法用於閱讀的時間打開」。這個 use case 在中老年 PI 之間口耳相傳，2025 年成為許多 lab 的標準學習方式。

Case 2: NotebookLM's podcast mode lets a PI absorb 5 papers during the commute

A mid-career PI commuted 45 min × 2/day — historically lost time for serious learning, fit only for podcasts or music. In Sept 2024 Google released NotebookLM's Audio Overview (two AI hosts in a 15–25 min dialogue). On Sunday nights he uploads 5–10 saved papers (NEJM, JAMA, Circulation, JCO highlights) and generates one episode for the Mon–Fri commute. After a month he told the lab: "Commute time now covers what used to require a separate lab-meeting slot — I gained 6–8 hours/week of real research time." Caveats he emphasized: (a) podcast is high-level grasp only, deep reading still requires the original; (b) AI hosts occasionally oversimplify — he follows up with page-cited NotebookLM queries; (c) papers needing figures are unsuitable — he switches to NotebookLM's text mode in the car. Lesson: AI isn't just "faster reading" — it unlocks time previously unusable for reading. This pattern spread through mid-career PIs in 2025 and became a standard lab-learning routine.

常見誤區

文獻 AI 工具最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：Elicit 的摘要不查原文就引用
為什麼錯：Elicit 的「Outcome / Methods / Limitations」是 AI extracted，準確率 80–90%——意思是每 5–10 個 cell 就有一個錯誤。常見錯誤包括 N 數錯、follow-up 月數錯、HR 方向反轉、把 ITT 寫成 PP。
正確做法：Elicit 結果視為「候選 + 索引」，最終引用前必須回 PDF 對應 page 確認。NotebookLM 的 page-cited Q&A 是 verification 利器。
Pitfall 1: Citing Elicit summaries without checking the original
Why wrong: Elicit's "Outcome / Methods / Limitations" cells are AI-extracted at 80–90% accuracy — meaning 1 in 5–10 cells is wrong. Frequent errors: incorrect N, follow-up months, HR direction flips, ITT vs PP swaps.
Fix: treat Elicit results as a "candidate index"; before citing, verify the relevant page in the PDF. NotebookLM's page-cited Q&A is a great verification tool.
⚠️
誤區 2：NotebookLM 嚴格 grounding 仍可能誤解 source
為什麼錯：NotebookLM 確實只用上傳 source，但「正確引用 source」≠「正確理解 source」。它仍可能把 secondary outcome 寫成 primary、把 subgroup analysis 寫成 main result、把 hypothesis-generating finding 寫成確認結論。
正確做法：page citation 必須點開 PDF 對應 page 確認「這個 claim 真的能從這段文字推出嗎」。NotebookLM 是 grounded 但不是 perfectly understanding——校對是最後一道防線。
Pitfall 2: NotebookLM is grounded but can still misinterpret a source
Why wrong: NotebookLM does cite only uploaded sources, but "correctly cited" ≠ "correctly understood." It can label a secondary outcome as primary, a subgroup analysis as the main result, or a hypothesis-generating finding as confirmatory.
Fix: every page citation must be cross-checked by opening the actual page and asking "does this claim really follow?" NotebookLM is grounded but not perfectly understanding — manual review is the final line of defense.
⚠️
誤區 3：Consensus 的「Yes/No」簡化複雜爭議
為什麼錯：Consensus 的 Yes/No/Possibly bar 對「intermittent fasting 有效嗎」這種多維度問題會過度簡化——「對哪個 outcome、哪個族群、多長 follow-up」這些 critical qualifier 被壓平。某些「Yes」的論文其實只在 secondary outcome 顯著、或在 healthy adult 而非 T2D 病人有效。
正確做法：把 Consensus 當「整體氣氛 indicator」，重要決策必須點開 individual paper 看 PICO 與 limitations。Consensus 適合「快速感受」而非「決策依據」。
Pitfall 3: Consensus's Yes/No oversimplifies complex disputes
Why wrong: For multi-dimensional questions like "does intermittent fasting work?" Consensus's Yes/No/Possibly bars flatten critical qualifiers — for which outcome, in which population, over what follow-up. Some "Yes" papers actually only show secondary endpoint significance, or work only in healthy adults rather than T2D patients.
Fix: treat Consensus as a "mood indicator." For important decisions, open individual papers and check PICO + limitations. Consensus is for quick sensing, not for citing as evidence.
⚠️
誤區 4：SciSpace 在中文 / 非英文文獻召回率差
為什麼錯：多數 AI 文獻工具的底層 embedding 與 corpus 以英文為主。中文、日文、韓文期刊（如《中華醫學雜誌》《中國肺癌雜誌》）召回率可低至 20–40%；西文期刊收錄較好但仍弱於英文。對中文 cohort 研究、台灣 / 中國 / 日韓人群資料的回顧，AI 工具可能漏掉重要在地文獻。
正確做法：中文文獻補用 CNKI、萬方、台灣華藝；日文補用 J-STAGE、CiNii。對 ethnic-specific 研究問題，AI 工具只是「補充」而非「取代」在地資料庫。
Pitfall 4: SciSpace has poor recall on Chinese / non-English literature
Why wrong: most AI literature tools' embeddings and corpora are English-centric. Chinese/Japanese/Korean journals (e.g., Chin Med J, Chin J Lung Cancer) achieve only 20–40% recall; Western non-English is better but still trails English. For Chinese cohort studies or Taiwan/China/Japan/Korea population reviews, AI tools may miss key local literature.
Fix: supplement with CNKI, Wanfang, and Airiti (Taiwan) for Chinese; J-STAGE and CiNii for Japanese. For ethnic-specific research questions, treat AI tools as supplement — not replacement — for local databases.
⚠️
誤區 5：完全依賴 AI 不做 backward snowballing
為什麼錯：AI 工具的 retrieval 通常基於 keyword + embedding 相似度，會漏掉「沒提到你的 keyword 但概念相關」的論文（如方法論的開創 paper 可能不用同樣術語）。傳統 backward / forward snowballing 仍是 SR 工具箱的關鍵步驟。
正確做法：AI 找到候選 paper 後，再對其 reference list 做 backward snowballing（OpenAlex API + Connected Papers），對其 forward citation 做 forward snowballing。AI 是「加速器」不是「替代品」。
Pitfall 5: Relying solely on AI and skipping backward snowballing
Why wrong: AI retrieval relies on keyword + embedding similarity and misses conceptually-related papers without matching keywords (a methodological seminal paper may use different terminology). Traditional backward/forward snowballing remains essential.
Fix: after AI finds candidates, do backward snowballing on their reference lists (OpenAlex API + Connected Papers) and forward snowballing on citations. AI accelerates — it doesn't replace.
資深讀者深入

進階細節

🔬 Semantic vs Lexical、HyDE prompt、OpenAlex vs Google Scholar 的 reference graph

① Semantic search vs Lexical search 的差別。Lexical（BM25、TF-IDF）依賴詞彙重疊：「PD-1 inhibitor in lung cancer」找不到只寫「pembrolizumab in NSCLC」的論文，即使它就是同一主題。Semantic（embedding-based）把兩者編碼為向量，因為 PD-1 inhibitor 與 pembrolizumab 在語意空間中很近，可以找到。Hybrid retrieval（dense + sparse）是 2026 年的標準：BM25 處理 exact match（PMID、gene symbol、basis name），dense embedding 處理同義詞與概念查詢。BGE-M3、ColBERT-v2、SPLADE 都是 hybrid 設計。

② HyDE prompt（Gao 2023, ACL）對 retrieval 的影響。HyDE = Hypothetical Document Embeddings：對 query 先用 LLM 生成一段「假想答案」，再用這段假想答案的 embedding 去檢索。直覺是：假想答案的 embedding 比 query embedding 更接近真實 document 的 embedding。在 BEIR benchmark 上 HyDE 比純 query embedding retrieval 提升 5–15%。實務上 ChatGPT search 與 Perplexity 都用 HyDE-like prompt augmentation。對研究者意義：若你的 RAG 系統 retrieval 不夠精確，加 HyDE 比換 embedding model 便宜。

③ OpenAlex 的 reference graph 比 Google Scholar 更完整。Google Scholar 不開放 API、不提供 reference list 機讀格式；引用 graph 只能透過爬蟲（被禁）或商業工具（如 Connected Papers）間接取得。OpenAlex（替代 Microsoft Academic Graph）提供完整 250M+ works、citation graph、author / institution 識別碼，free API + 無 rate limit（with email）。Semantic Scholar API 則提供 SPECTER embeddings 與 TLDR 摘要。2026 年的 SR 工作流建議：種子論文 → OpenAlex backward snowballing → Semantic Scholar forward snowballing → Elicit/SciSpace screen → NotebookLM full-text 抽取。

④ Living systematic review 的技術骨架。2025 年 Cochrane 與 NEJM Evidence 開始試行 living SR：(a) 自動 monthly PubMed query；(b) AI auto-screen new hits；(c) LLM 評估是否影響原 SR 結論；(d) 若顯著影響則觸發人工 review 與 versioned update。技術上是 RAG + change detection + workflow automation 的組合。對研究者：未來不用「等 1 年才看到」更新後的 SR。

① Semantic vs lexical search. Lexical (BM25, TF-IDF) relies on word overlap: "PD-1 inhibitor in lung cancer" misses a paper saying "pembrolizumab in NSCLC" even though it's the same topic. Semantic (embedding-based) encodes both into vectors; PD-1 inhibitor and pembrolizumab sit close in semantic space, so they match. Hybrid retrieval (dense + sparse) is the 2026 standard: BM25 handles exact match (PMIDs, gene symbols), dense embeddings handle synonyms and concepts. BGE-M3, ColBERT-v2, SPLADE are all hybrid.

② HyDE prompts (Gao 2023, ACL). HyDE = Hypothetical Document Embeddings: have the LLM draft a "hypothetical answer" first, then use that draft's embedding for retrieval. Intuition: the hypothetical answer's embedding is closer to real documents than the query embedding. BEIR shows 5–15% gain over plain query embedding. ChatGPT search and Perplexity use HyDE-like augmentation in production. Practical implication: if your RAG retrieval is weak, adding HyDE is cheaper than switching embedding models.

③ OpenAlex's reference graph is far more complete than Google Scholar. Scholar offers no public API and no machine-readable reference lists; citation graphs can only be assembled via scraping (forbidden) or commercial tools (Connected Papers). OpenAlex (replacing Microsoft Academic Graph) provides 250M+ works, the full citation graph, author/institution identifiers — free API with no rate limit (with email). Semantic Scholar adds SPECTER embeddings and TLDR summaries. 2026 SR workflow recommendation: seed paper → OpenAlex backward snowballing → Semantic Scholar forward snowballing → Elicit/SciSpace screen → NotebookLM full-text extraction.

④ Living systematic review technical backbone. Cochrane and NEJM Evidence began piloting living SRs in 2025: (a) automated monthly PubMed queries; (b) AI auto-screening of new hits; (c) LLM judging whether new evidence changes the original SR conclusion; (d) human review + versioned update when triggered. Technically: RAG + change detection + workflow automation. Users no longer wait a year for an updated SR.

討論問題

與你的 lab 思辨這四題

Q1: SR 的「人類判斷」哪些可以給 AI？

傳統 SR 強調 dual-reviewer screening、独立 RoB 評估。AI 工具讓單人能在 1/4 時間完成。Cochrane 2024 指出：AI auto-screen 在低 risk-of-bias 設計上 acceptable，但 high-impact 結論仍需人類複核。請思考：你會把哪些步驟（title screen / abstract screen / full-text / RoB / data extraction）放心交給 AI？哪些絕對不行？為什麼？

Q1: Which SR human judgments can be delegated to AI?

Traditional SR mandates dual-reviewer screening and independent RoB assessment. AI tools let a single person finish in 1/4 the time. Cochrane 2024 says AI auto-screening is acceptable for low-RoB designs but high-impact conclusions still need humans. Consider: which steps (title screen / abstract screen / full-text / RoB / data extraction) would you delegate to AI? Which never? Why?

Q2: 文獻 gap 真的可以被 AI 找出嗎？

有人主張 AI 能透過 citation network analysis 找出「未被連結的兩個 cluster」即是 gap；也有人認為真正的 gap 來自領域 insight，不是 graph topology。請思考：你能舉一個你領域的真實例子嗎——AI 是否真能識別出？AI 找出的「gap」與你 PI 一句話指出的「下一個方向」有什麼根本差別？

Q2: Can AI really discover literature gaps?

Some argue AI can identify gaps via citation-network analysis — "unconnected clusters" = gap. Others say real gaps come from domain insight, not graph topology. Consider: can you name a real gap in your field — would AI have found it? How does an AI-detected "gap" differ fundamentally from your PI's one-sentence "next direction"?

Q3: 中文 / 非英文文獻在 SR 的位置

AI 工具對中文文獻召回率差。同時，國際期刊 SR 規範並未強制 multilingual search。你的 PI 認為「英文 SR 不需要看中文文獻」；但你研究 Asian-specific cancer，中文 cohort 資料可能關鍵。請思考：你會如何說服 PI 補做中文搜尋？該如何在 AI workflow 中整合 CNKI / 華藝？對 ethnic-specific research questions 你會建立什麼樣的工具堆疊？

Q3: Where do non-English papers fit in SR?

AI tools have poor Chinese-literature recall. International SR guidelines don't strictly mandate multilingual search. Your PI thinks "English SR doesn't need Chinese papers" — but you work on Asian-specific cancer where Chinese cohorts may be pivotal. Consider: how would you persuade your PI to add Chinese search? How would you integrate CNKI/Airiti into the AI workflow? What stack would you build for ethnic-specific questions?

Q4: Living SR 會取代傳統 SR 嗎？

Cochrane 2025 試行 living SR：AI monthly auto-screen + LLM 評估是否影響結論。優點是「永遠新」；缺點是 version control、引用問題、reviewer review burden 持續。請思考：你領域哪類問題最適合 living SR？哪類仍須傳統 SR？對 PubMed 引用「version 2025-Mar vs 2025-Sep」的學術倫理你怎麼看？這是進步還是新的混亂源？

Q4: Will living SRs replace traditional SRs?

Cochrane 2025 pilots living SRs: AI monthly auto-screen + LLM checks whether new evidence shifts conclusions. Upside: always current. Downside: version control, citation problems, persistent reviewer burden. Consider: which questions in your field fit living SRs? Which still require traditional? How do you view citing "version 2025-Mar vs 2025-Sep" — progress or a new source of chaos?
