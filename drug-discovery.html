藥物與化學 AI

AlphaFold-Multimer, Boltz-1, RFdiffusion, ChemCrow — every one of the five stages from target to clinic now has AI acceleration.

術語速查

藥物 AI 必懂十二詞

讀懂這 12 個術語，後面的 case、pitfall、advanced 才不會卡住。從工具名（ChemCrow、RFdiffusion、Boltz）到化學家行話（Hit、Lead、ADMET）一次到位。

These 12 terms unlock the cases, pitfalls, and advanced sections. From tool names (ChemCrow, RFdiffusion, Boltz) to medicinal-chemistry jargon (Hit, Lead, ADMET), all in one place.

⚗️

ChemCrow

Bran 2024 Nat Mach Intell；GPT-4 + 18 個化學工具（retrosynthesis、RDKit、reaction prediction）組成的 agent，能自主規劃多步合成路徑並執行毒性、溶解度查詢。

Bran 2024 Nat Mach Intell; an agent wrapping GPT-4 around 18 chemistry tools (retrosynthesis, RDKit, reaction prediction) that autonomously plans multi-step syntheses and runs toxicity / solubility checks.

🧬

RFdiffusion

Watson 2023 Nature + 2023/10 All-Atom 版本；以 diffusion model 從零生成蛋白骨架，2024 起與 ProteinMPNN + AlphaFold validate 構成標準 de novo binder pipeline。

Watson 2023 Nature + the Oct 2023 All-Atom release; a diffusion model that generates protein backbones from scratch. With ProteinMPNN + AlphaFold validation it has become the standard de novo binder pipeline.

🤝

AlphaFold-Multimer

Evans 2021 + AlphaFold 3 2024；專為蛋白-蛋白 / 蛋白-配體 complex 設計。Drug discovery 用它預測 target 與 binder 介面，比單體預測更貼近功能。

Evans 2021 + AlphaFold 3 in 2024; built for protein-protein / protein-ligand complexes. In drug discovery it predicts target-binder interfaces — far more useful than monomer prediction alone.

⚡

Boltz-1 / Boltz-2

MIT Jameel Clinic 2024–2025；開源 AF3 等級的蛋白 + 配體 / 核酸聯合預測。MIT license，商用友善，是社群版 AF3 的事實標準。

MIT Jameel Clinic 2024–2025; open-source AF3-tier joint structure prediction for protein + ligand / nucleic acid. MIT-licensed and commercially friendly — the de facto community alternative to AF3.

🎯

Hit

初步篩選（HTS、DEL、virtual screen）發現有「弱活性」（µM 級別 IC50）的化合物；通常 hit rate 0.1–1%。Hit 還沒到「藥」，只是入場券。

A compound flagged by primary screening (HTS, DEL, virtual screen) with "weak" activity (µM-range IC50); hit rates are usually 0.1–1%. A hit is the entry ticket, not yet a drug.

🏆

Lead

從 hit 經過 SAR 優化、活性提到 nM 級、ADMET 初步通過的化合物，準備進入動物 PK / 毒性測試。Lead optimization 通常需要合成 30–300 個分子。

A hit optimised through SAR until activity reaches nM range and basic ADMET passes — ready for animal PK / tox studies. Lead optimisation typically synthesises 30–300 analogues.

💊

ADMET

Absorption / Distribution / Metabolism / Excretion / Toxicity 五大藥動 + 毒性性質。AI 預測 ADMET 在已知化學空間表現好，OOD（新骨架）誤差大。

Absorption / Distribution / Metabolism / Excretion / Toxicity — the five PK + tox properties. AI ADMET predictors work well in-distribution but err sharply OOD on novel scaffolds.

📝

SMILES

Simplified Molecular-Input Line-Entry System，化合物的字串表示法（如 aspirin = CC(=O)OC1=CC=CC=C1C(=O)O）。LLM / Transformer 處理分子的標準輸入格式。

Simplified Molecular-Input Line-Entry System — a string encoding of a molecule (aspirin = CC(=O)OC1=CC=CC=C1C(=O)O). The standard input format for LLM / Transformer molecular models.

🔄

Retrosynthesis

從目標分子反推合成路線。AiZynthFinder（AstraZeneca）、ASKCOS（MIT）、Manifold（Postera）是三個主流 AI retrosynthesis 工具。

Working backwards from a target molecule to a synthesis route. AiZynthFinder (AstraZeneca), ASKCOS (MIT), and Manifold (Postera) are the three mainstream AI retrosynthesis tools.

✨

Generative Chemistry

用 VAE / GAN / diffusion / autoregressive Transformer 生成新分子。常見 metrics：Validity（合法 SMILES）、Novelty（不在訓練集）、FCD（與真實藥的分布距離）。

Using VAE / GAN / diffusion / autoregressive Transformers to generate new molecules. Common metrics: Validity (legal SMILES), Novelty (not in training set), FCD (distribution distance to real drugs).

🆕

De novo Design

從零設計新分子或新蛋白（不基於現有 ligand）。RFdiffusion 是 de novo protein design 的代表；REINVENT、Pocket2Mol 是 de novo small-molecule design 工具。

Designing molecules or proteins from scratch (not based on existing ligands). RFdiffusion is the flagship for de novo proteins; REINVENT and Pocket2Mol are leading de novo small-molecule tools.

🏢

Insilico Medicine

2014 創立，總部香港 + 紐約。INS018_055（TNIK inhibitor，肺纖維化）是全球第一個 AI 找 target + AI 設計分子完整走到 Phase II 的化合物，target 到 IND 18 個月。

Founded 2014, HQ Hong Kong + NYC. INS018_055 (TNIK inhibitor, IPF) is the first compound with AI-discovered target and AI-generated structure to reach Phase II — target to IND in 18 months.

概覽

AI 真的加速了新藥開發嗎？

2024–2026 是 AIDD（AI-driven drug discovery）從「會議簡報」走進「IND filing」的轉折期。Insilico Medicine 的 INS018_055（TNIK inhibitor，肺纖維化）2024 進入 Phase II，是全球第一個從 AI 設計 target + AI 生成分子完整走到 IND 的 case；Recursion 用 phenomic screening 與 AI 預測，與 Roche、Bayer 合作多年；Schrödinger / Atomwise / Isomorphic Labs（DeepMind 分拆）也有 pipeline 進臨床。

但「AI 設計藥物」≠「AI 拿到 FDA 通過」。AI 加速的是「target identification → hit discovery → lead optimization」這段——化合物還是要過動物實驗、Phase I/II/III。AI 的真實貢獻：把 hit discovery 從 18 個月 + $100M 縮成 6 個月 + $20M；把 lead optimization 從 1000 個分子試合成縮成 30 個。

2024–2026 is when AIDD (AI-driven drug discovery) graduated from conference slides to IND filings. Insilico's INS018_055 (TNIK inhibitor, IPF) entered Phase II in 2024 — the first molecule with AI-discovered target and AI-generated structure to reach IND. Recursion runs phenomic screening + AI prediction in multi-year deals with Roche and Bayer. Schrödinger / Atomwise / Isomorphic Labs (a DeepMind spin-out) also have pipelines in the clinic.

But "AI designed the drug" ≠ "FDA approved it." AI accelerates the target identification → hit discovery → lead optimization span — compounds still need animal studies and Phase I/II/III. Real AI contribution: hit discovery from 18 months + $100M down to 6 months + $20M; lead optimization from synthesising 1000 candidates down to 30.

💡
核心心法：AI 不取代藥廠，是「壓縮前段時程的工具」。研究人員的價值在於：選對 target、設計可合成且 ADMET 通過的分子、解讀活體結果。Generative model 設計出「漂亮但不可合成」的分子是常見陷阱。 Core mindset: AI doesn't replace pharma — it's a time-compressor for the early stages. Researcher value is choosing the right target, designing molecules that are both synthesisable and ADMET-passing, and interpreting in-vivo results. Generative models routinely propose "beautiful but unmakeable" molecules.

  - core scaffold: 4-aminoquinazoline
  - solubility > 50 µM
  - molecular weight < 450
Propose 3 candidate structures and a 4-step synthesis for the
most promising one, including reagents and references."""

result = agent.run(task)
print(result)

# literature lookup, reaction prediction — autonomously.

# Next steps:
#   1. ProteinMPNN to design sequence

#   3. Pick top 10 by pLDDT + interface score, order for SPR
# ChemBERTa — predict ADMET properties from SMILES
from transformers import AutoTokenizer, AutoModel
import torch, torch.nn as nn

tok = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained("DeepChem/ChemBERTa-77M-MTR")
backbone = AutoModel.from_pretrained("DeepChem/ChemBERTa-77M-MTR")

# Fine-tune head for hERG cardiotoxicity binary classification
class ADMETHead(nn.Module):
    def __init__(self): super().__init__(); self.fc = nn.Linear(384, 2)
    def forward(self, ids, mask):
        h = backbone(ids, attention_mask=mask).last_hidden_state[:,0]
        return self.fc(h)

mdl = ADMETHead()
# Train on hERG dataset (~5–10k labelled SMILES), predict for new compounds.
smi = "CC(=O)OC1=CC=CC=C1C(=O)O"  # aspirin
inp = tok(smi, return_tensors="pt", padding=True)
print(mdl(**inp))

真實案例

兩個深入案例

🧪

案例 1：Insilico INS018_055——target 到 IND 只用 18 個月

Insilico Medicine（香港 + 紐約）2021 年用自家 PandaOmics 平台從 ~1.5 萬個基因中篩出 TNIK 為 idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis（IPF）的新 target——傳統方法通常需要 3–5 年 GWAS + 轉錄體 + 文獻挖掘。接著用 Chemistry42（generative chemistry 平台）設計分子，從零產出 ~ 60 個 lead candidates、合成 ~ 30 個、驗證後 INS018_055 進入 IND。2022 年 IND filing、2024 年 Phase II 開啟，從 target identification 到 IND 全程 18 個月、約 $2.6M——傳統 drug discovery 早期需要 $30–50M + 3–5 年。INS018_055 是全球第一個 AI 找 target + AI 生成分子完整走到 Phase II 的化合物，2024 年發表於 Nature Biotechnology（Ren et al. "A small-molecule TNIK inhibitor targets fibrosis"）。重點：這不代表「AI 取代藥廠」——INS018_055 仍需 Phase II/III 證明療效，臨床真實效益尚未知。但 AI 在「target → IND」這段時程壓縮已被工業界接受為 game-changer。

Case 1: Insilico INS018_055 — target to IND in 18 months

Insilico Medicine (HK + NYC) used its PandaOmics platform in 2021 to screen ~15K genes and identify TNIK as a novel target for idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF) — work that traditionally needs 3–5 years of GWAS + transcriptomics + literature mining. They then used Chemistry42 (a generative-chemistry platform) to design molecules from scratch, produced ~60 lead candidates, synthesised ~30, and validated INS018_055 to IND status. IND filing in 2022, Phase II opened in 2024 — target identification to IND in 18 months at roughly $2.6M. Traditional early-stage drug discovery costs $30–50M over 3–5 years. INS018_055 is the first compound with both AI-discovered target and AI-generated structure to reach Phase II, published in 2024 in Nature Biotechnology (Ren et al. "A small-molecule TNIK inhibitor targets fibrosis"). Important caveat: this does not mean "AI replaces pharma" — INS018_055 still needs Phase II/III to prove efficacy. But the "target → IND" time compression is now industry-accepted as a game-changer.

🧬

案例 2：Baker Lab RFdiffusion All-Atom 設計 cytokine receptor binder

2024 年 1 月，David Baker 實驗室（UW IPD）在 Science 發表 Krishna et al. "Generalized biomolecular modeling and design with RoseTTAFold All-Atom"——將 RoseTTAFold 升級為 RFAA（All-Atom），可同時建模蛋白、DNA、RNA、配體、糖、金屬離子。配合 2023/10 釋出的 RFdiffusion All-Atom，他們完成了「從零設計 cytokine receptor binder」的工作：選定 IL-7Rα 為 target，使用 RFdiffusion AA 產生 ~ 9000 個 binder backbone（hotspot 為 IL-7Rα 之 D1 domain）→ ProteinMPNN 設計序列 → AlphaFold 2 / 3 與 Boltz-1 cross-validation → 篩選 pLDDT ≥ 90 + interface PAE ≤ 8 + Rosetta dG ≤ -30 的 candidates → 表達純化 96 個 → SPR / BLI 測 affinity → 6 個達到 KD < 100 nM、3 個達到 KD < 10 nM。其中最佳 binder 在小鼠 PK 與 IL-7 signal block assay 中表現優於商業化抗體 fragment。關鍵：這條 pipeline 從電腦設計到 in vitro 驗證只用了 4 個月、總成本 ~ $80K——傳統 antibody phage display + maturation 通常需 12–18 個月、$500K+。但限制：成功率仍只有 ~3%（96 個合成 / 9000 個設計），且 target 必須有清楚 hotspot；無 hotspot 的多態 target（如 GPCR）仍是挑戰。

Case 2: Baker Lab — designing a cytokine receptor binder with RFdiffusion All-Atom

In January 2024 David Baker's lab (UW IPD) published Krishna et al. "Generalized biomolecular modeling and design with RoseTTAFold All-Atom" in Science — upgrading RoseTTAFold to RFAA (All-Atom) so it models proteins, DNA, RNA, ligands, sugars, and metal ions jointly. Paired with the Oct 2023 release of RFdiffusion All-Atom, they completed a flagship task: de novo design of a cytokine receptor binder. They picked IL-7Rα as the target, used RFdiffusion AA to generate ~9,000 binder backbones (hotspots on IL-7Rα's D1 domain), passed them through ProteinMPNN for sequence design, cross-validated with AlphaFold 2 / 3 and Boltz-1, filtered by pLDDT ≥ 90, interface PAE ≤ 8, and Rosetta dG ≤ -30, expressed and purified 96, and measured affinity by SPR / BLI. Six binders hit KD < 100 nM and three reached KD < 10 nM. The best binder outperformed a commercial antibody fragment in mouse PK and IL-7 signal-block assays. Why this matters: from in silico design to in vitro validation in 4 months at ~$80K — traditional antibody phage display + maturation needs 12–18 months and $500K+. Limits: success rate is still ~3% (96 synthesised / 9,000 designed) and a clear hotspot is required; polymorphic targets without obvious hotspots (e.g. GPCRs) remain hard.

常見誤區

AIDD 新手最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：Generative model 產出「漂亮但不可合成」分子
為什麼錯：純 generative model（VAE / diffusion）的 objective 通常只看 docking score / property prediction，沒有合成可行性 constraint。30–50% 的設計分子在 retrosynthesis 上 fail，或需要 10+ 步罕見 reagent。
正確做法：pipeline 串接 retrosynthesis filter（AiZynthFinder、ASKCOS、Manifold），只保留 ≤ 5 步、commercial reagent 路線；或用 SyntheMol（Swanson 2024 Nat Chem）這類 synthesis-aware generator。
Pitfall 1: Generative models propose "beautiful but unmakeable" molecules
Why wrong: VAE / diffusion objectives usually optimise docking or property scores without synthesisability constraints. 30–50% of designs fail retrosynthesis or need 10+ steps with exotic reagents.
Fix: chain a retrosynthesis filter (AiZynthFinder, ASKCOS, Manifold) and keep only ≤5-step routes from commercial reagents — or use a synthesis-aware generator like SyntheMol (Swanson 2024 Nat Chem).
⚠️
誤區 2：ADMET 預測沒做 prospective 驗證就「上線」
為什麼錯：ADMET model 在 retrospective hold-out 上 AUC 看似 0.85+，但在新 chemical series（out-of-distribution）上 AUC 常掉到 0.55–0.65。把舊模型直接套到新化合物等於擲銅板。Yang 2019、Walters 2021 都警告過這個問題。
正確做法：每個新 series 都做 prospective wet-lab validation（≥ 30 個化合物），驗證 calibration；不通過則 fine-tune 或回到 measured ADMET。
Pitfall 2: Deploying ADMET predictors without prospective validation
Why wrong: retrospective AUCs of 0.85+ can collapse to 0.55–0.65 on new chemical series (OOD). Applying an old model to a new scaffold is a coin flip. Yang 2019 and Walters 2021 both warned about this.
Fix: prospective wet-lab validate every new series (≥30 compounds) and check calibration; otherwise fine-tune or fall back to measured ADMET.
⚠️
誤區 3：把 docking score 當 binding affinity
為什麼錯：docking score（AutoDock Vina、Glide）與真實 IC50 / KD 的相關性只有 r ≈ 0.4–0.6（Bender 2021 Nat Rev Drug Discov）。AlphaFold 3 / Boltz-1 的 confidence 也不等於 affinity——它們是 pose accuracy 的指標。直接用 docking score 排序選 lead 經常選錯。
正確做法：docking 只做粗篩；用 FEP+ (Schrödinger)、ABFE (OpenFE)、或 ML-based MM-PB/GBSA 做 lead ranking；最終用 SPR / ITC 量真實 KD。
Pitfall 3: Treating docking scores as binding affinities
Why wrong: docking scores (AutoDock Vina, Glide) correlate with real IC50 / KD at only r ≈ 0.4–0.6 (Bender 2021 Nat Rev Drug Discov). AlphaFold 3 / Boltz-1 confidences measure pose accuracy, not affinity. Ranking leads by docking score routinely picks wrong winners.
Fix: dock only for coarse screening; rank leads with FEP+ (Schrödinger), ABFE (OpenFE), or ML MM-PB/GBSA; measure real KD by SPR / ITC at the end.
⚠️
誤區 4：對 IP 敏感的分子用公開 LLM API
為什麼錯：把未公開 lead structure 的 SMILES 丟進 ChatGPT 公開版（沒 BAA / DPA），等於放棄營業秘密與 patent novelty。OpenAI 公開版 ToS 保留訓練權；且即使不訓練，competitor 也可能用相似 prompt 撈到資訊。已有藥廠在 2024 因實習生這樣操作被迫放棄 patent 申請的案例。
正確做法：對 IP 敏感分子用 Azure OpenAI（zero-retention）、Claude for Work、或本地 Llama-3 / DeepSeek-V3；公司應有 LLM-use policy 明確列出「公開 API 禁止輸入」清單。
Pitfall 4: Using public LLM APIs on IP-sensitive molecules
Why wrong: pasting an unpublished lead's SMILES into public ChatGPT (no BAA / DPA) waives trade secrecy and patent novelty. Public OpenAI ToS reserves training rights; even without training, competitors may extract similar info with adjacent prompts. A pharma case in 2024 had to abandon a patent application after an intern did exactly this.
Fix: for IP-sensitive molecules use Azure OpenAI (zero-retention), Claude for Work, or self-hosted Llama-3 / DeepSeek-V3; company LLM-use policy should explicitly list "do not paste" categories.
⚠️
誤區 5：hit-to-lead 沒做 DEL / HTS 平行驗證
為什麼錯：純 virtual screen 找到的「AI hit」假陽性率可能高達 70–90%——AlphaFold 結構與 docking 沒看到的 cryptic pocket、protein dynamics、buffer effect 都會讓 in silico hit 在 wet-lab 完全沒活性。把 AI hit 直接送進複雜 SAR 而不平行做 DEL（DNA-encoded library）或 HTS 驗證，可能浪費半年。
正確做法：對每個新 target 平行跑 DEL（10⁸–10¹⁰ compounds）+ AI virtual screen，比較 hit overlap；用 wet-lab orthogonal assay（biophysical + cellular）確認後再進 SAR。
Pitfall 5: Hit-to-lead without DEL / HTS in parallel
Why wrong: pure virtual-screen "AI hits" have false-positive rates of 70–90% — cryptic pockets, protein dynamics, and buffer effects that AlphaFold + docking can't see often kill in vitro activity. Marching AI hits straight into complex SAR without parallel DEL (DNA-encoded library) or HTS validation can burn six months.
Fix: for every new target, run DEL (10⁸–10¹⁰ compounds) + AI virtual screen in parallel, compare hit overlap, and confirm with orthogonal biophysical + cellular assays before SAR.

進階細節

🔬 AiZynthFinder / ASKCOS、FCD / Validity / Novelty、RFdiffusion ↔ RFAA、FEP+ vs ML scoring

① Retrosynthesis 演算法。AiZynthFinder（Genheden 2020 J Cheminf，AstraZeneca 開源）用 Monte Carlo Tree Search + neural policy network，預設 USPTO + Reaxys-licensed reaction template；ASKCOS（MIT，Coley 2018 ACS Cent Sci）是更早的學術版本，模板涵蓋 USPTO 1976–2016。Manifold（Postera）是商業版，整合 chemicals.com inventory 與 cost estimate。三者都輸出 retrosynthetic tree，但 AiZynthFinder 開源 + 自訂 reaction template 最受工業界喜愛。2024 起新一代如 Chimera（Schwaller）用 Transformer 直接生成 retrosynthetic SMILES，速度提升 5–10×。

② Generative model evaluation：FCD / Validity / Novelty。Validity = 合法 SMILES（RDKit parse 成功）比例，benchmark 應 ≥ 95%。Novelty = 不在訓練集中的比例，但要小心：100% Novelty 可能代表模型「逃離」化學空間。FCD（Fréchet ChemNet Distance，Preuer 2018 J Chem Inf Model）用預訓練的 ChemNet 抽 features，計算 generated 與 reference set 在 feature space 的 Fréchet distance，類似電腦視覺的 FID。FCD < 5 通常代表 generated 分布貼近真實藥；> 10 則離真實藥很遠。其他常用：QED（Bickerton 2012，drug-likeness 0–1）、SA score（Ertl 2009，synthesis accessibility 1–10）。GuacaMol benchmark（Brown 2019）與 MOSES benchmark（Polykovskiy 2020）是兩個標準評估套件。

③ RFdiffusion 與 RoseTTAFold All-Atom 的關係。RFdiffusion（Watson 2023 Nature）只處理蛋白骨架，無 side chain 與 ligand。RoseTTAFold All-Atom / RFAA（Krishna 2024 Science）擴展為 all-atom 模型，可同時建模 protein + DNA / RNA + ligand + 糖 + 金屬離子。RFdiffusion All-Atom（2023/10 釋出）是把 RFAA 與 diffusion 結合，使生成模型在 backbone diffusion 過程中即可考慮 side chain 與 ligand context。實務上 RFdiffusion AA 用於 enzyme design（需要 substrate 在 active site）、cofactor-binding protein 設計、小分子 binder 設計——這是 2023 原版 RFdiffusion 做不到的。

④ Physics-informed vs ML-based scoring。Physics-informed（FEP+, ABFE, QM/MM）基於統計力學第一性原理，理論上最準（FEP+ 在 well-prepared system 上 MAE ≈ 1 kcal/mol），但慢（每 ligand pair 數小時 GPU）。ML-based（ChemBERTa、DeepDTA、TankBind、Boltz-2 affinity head）快（毫秒級），但在 OOD 上不可靠。2024–2026 的折衷：(a) ML 粗篩 → FEP+ 精排 → wet-lab；(b) Δ-ML（學習 FEP 與 ML 預測之差）；(c) physics-informed neural network（PINN）把 docking energy 作為 loss 一項。Schrödinger 2024 報告 FEP+ + AI 在 lead optimization 上每 lead 試合成數從 1000 縮為 30。

① Retrosynthesis algorithms. AiZynthFinder (Genheden 2020 J Cheminf, AstraZeneca open source) uses Monte Carlo Tree Search + a neural policy network, with USPTO + Reaxys-licensed reaction templates. ASKCOS (Coley 2018 ACS Cent Sci, MIT) is the earlier academic version covering USPTO 1976–2016. Manifold (Postera) is the commercial alternative, integrating chemicals.com inventory and cost estimation. All three return a retrosynthetic tree, but AiZynthFinder's open-source + custom-template stance wins industrial mindshare. From 2024, next-gen tools like Chimera (Schwaller) generate retrosynthetic SMILES directly with Transformers, 5–10× faster.

② Generative-model evaluation: FCD / Validity / Novelty. Validity = fraction of legal SMILES (RDKit parses), benchmark ≥ 95%. Novelty = fraction not in training set — but beware: 100% Novelty may mean the model "escaped" chemistry space. FCD (Fréchet ChemNet Distance, Preuer 2018 J Chem Inf Model) uses a pre-trained ChemNet to extract features and computes the Fréchet distance between generated and reference sets — the chemistry analogue of FID in computer vision. FCD < 5 means a distribution close to real drugs; > 10 means far off. Other common metrics: QED (Bickerton 2012, drug-likeness 0–1) and SA score (Ertl 2009, synthetic accessibility 1–10). GuacaMol (Brown 2019) and MOSES (Polykovskiy 2020) are the two standard benchmark suites.

③ RFdiffusion and RoseTTAFold All-Atom. RFdiffusion (Watson 2023 Nature) handles only protein backbones — no side chains, no ligands. RoseTTAFold All-Atom / RFAA (Krishna 2024 Science) extends to an all-atom model that jointly handles protein + DNA / RNA + ligand + sugars + metal ions. RFdiffusion All-Atom (Oct 2023) couples RFAA with diffusion so that side-chain and ligand context are considered during backbone diffusion. In practice RFdiffusion AA is what enables enzyme design (substrate in active site), cofactor-binding proteins, and small-molecule binder design — none of which the 2023 vanilla RFdiffusion could do.

④ Physics-informed vs ML-based scoring. Physics-informed methods (FEP+, ABFE, QM/MM) are first-principles, theoretically most accurate (FEP+ MAE ≈ 1 kcal/mol on well-prepared systems), but slow (hours of GPU per ligand pair). ML methods (ChemBERTa, DeepDTA, TankBind, Boltz-2 affinity head) are fast (millisecond) but unreliable OOD. 2024–2026 compromise: (a) ML coarse screen → FEP+ fine ranking → wet-lab; (b) Δ-ML learning the FEP-minus-ML residual; (c) physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) using docking energy as a loss term. Schrödinger 2024 reports FEP+ + AI cuts compounds synthesised per lead from 1000 to 30 in lead optimisation.

Q3: 純 in silico design 能否縮短 / 取代動物毒理學試驗？

Q3: Can pure in silico design replace animal toxicology?
