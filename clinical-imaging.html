醫療影像 Foundation 模型

RETFound、CONCH、Virchow 2、MedGemma——眼科、病理、放射科都有自己的 GPT 級基底模型。

RETFound, CONCH, Virchow 2, MedGemma — ophthalmology, pathology, and radiology each have their own GPT-tier foundation model.

術語速查

醫療影像 FM 必懂十詞

這 10 個詞貫穿整章——先讀過一次，後面 RETFound、CONCH、Virchow、FDA / MDR 的討論就會順暢許多。

These ten terms recur throughout. Skim once and the discussion of RETFound, CONCH, Virchow, and FDA / MDR flows easily.

👁️

RETFound

Zhou et al. 2023 Nature。Moorfields Eye Hospital + UCL 在 1.6M OCT + fundus 影像上做 MAE pretraining 的眼科 foundation model；下游 DR、AMD、青光眼分級只需 200–1000 張標註即可達 expert-level。

Zhou et al. 2023 Nature. Moorfields Eye Hospital + UCL pretrained an ophthalmology foundation model with MAE on 1.6M OCT + fundus images; downstream DR, AMD, glaucoma grading hit expert-level with only 200–1000 labels.

🔬

CONCH

Lu et al. 2024 Nat Med。Harvard / MGH 在 1.17M 病理 image-text pairs 上做 CLIP-style vision-language pretraining；支援病理 classification / segmentation / zero-shot captioning。

Lu et al. 2024 Nat Med. Harvard / MGH pretrained a vision-language model CLIP-style on 1.17M pathology image-text pairs; supports classification, segmentation, and zero-shot captioning.

🧪

Virchow

Vorontsov et al. 2024 / Virchow 2 (Paige.AI 2025)。在 3.1M WSI 上預訓練的病理 foundation model，授權給藥廠 / 醫院做 biomarker discovery 與 IHC 量化；部分產品已走 FDA 510(k) 路徑。

Vorontsov et al. 2024 / Virchow 2 (Paige.AI 2025). Pathology foundation model pretrained on 3.1M WSI, licensed to pharma / hospitals for biomarker discovery and IHC quantification; some products have followed the FDA 510(k) pathway.

🌐

MedGemma

Google 2026/1 釋出 4B / 27B 開源權重，文本 + 影像多模態 medical foundation model；對 X-ray、MRI、皮膚科影像皆可生成描述與回答 query。

Google's Jan 2026 release of 4B / 27B open weights — a multimodal medical foundation model that processes text + images, generating descriptions and answering queries on X-ray, MRI, dermatology, etc.

🔗

Vision-Language Model (VLM)

Image 與 text 在同一個 embedding 空間共同學習的模型（CLIP, ALIGN, CONCH, Med-Gemini）。允許 zero-shot caption、cross-modal retrieval、free-text query。醫療上特別有用：寫報告、回答影像問題、檢索類似 case。

A model that learns image and text in a shared embedding space (CLIP, ALIGN, CONCH, Med-Gemini). Enables zero-shot captioning, cross-modal retrieval, free-text queries. Especially useful in medicine: drafting reports, answering image questions, retrieving similar cases.

📁

DICOM

Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine。醫療影像的事實標準格式，包含 pixel data + metadata（patient ID、modality、window/level、acquisition params）。AI pipeline 必須懂如何讀 metadata（如 windowing）才能正確輸入模型。

Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine. The de facto standard for medical imaging, containing pixel data + metadata (patient ID, modality, window/level, acquisition parameters). AI pipelines must understand metadata (e.g., windowing) to correctly feed the model.

🗄️

PACS

Picture Archiving and Communication System。醫院儲存、查詢、傳輸醫療影像的系統，與 EHR / RIS（Radiology Information System）整合。AI 工具的臨床部署通常以 PACS-integrated worklist 形式呈現。

Picture Archiving and Communication System. The hospital infrastructure for storing, querying, and transmitting medical images, integrated with EHR / RIS (Radiology Information System). Clinical AI deployment typically appears as a PACS-integrated worklist.

📜

FDA 510(k)

美國 FDA 醫材上市途徑之一：證明你的 device 與已有 cleared "predicate" device 「實質等同」（substantial equivalence）。多數放射 AI 工具（CADt、CADe、CADx）走這條路；不需要 PMA 那麼嚴格的臨床試驗。其他兩條：De Novo（新類別）、PMA（高風險 Class III）。

One of FDA's pathways to market: prove "substantial equivalence" to a cleared predicate device. Most radiology AI tools (CADt, CADe, CADx) take this route — less rigorous than a PMA's full clinical trial. The other two paths: De Novo (new classification) and PMA (high-risk Class III).

⚙️

SaMD (Software as a Medical Device)

IMDRF 定義：以軟體形式存在、用於醫療目的的醫材（非附屬於 hardware）。FDA、EU MDR、TFDA 都採此分類。AI 影像分析、CDSS、symptom checker 皆屬此類；分 Class I-IV，越高風險審查越嚴。

IMDRF definition: medical-purpose software existing as a standalone device (not bundled with hardware). FDA, EU MDR, and TFDA all adopt this classification. AI image analysis, CDSS, symptom checkers fall here; classified I–IV, with higher risk facing stricter review.

🛡️

Class IIb / III (MDR)

EU MDR 2017/745 對醫材的風險分類：IIa（一般風險）/ IIb（高風險，多數 AI 影像）/ III（最高，植入式或維生）。Class IIb 以上需 notified body 介入 conformity assessment；MDR 與 AI Act 2024/1689 雙重審查 high-risk AI medical devices。

EU MDR 2017/745 risk classes: IIa (medium), IIb (high — most imaging AI), III (highest — implantable / life-sustaining). Class IIb+ requires notified body conformity assessment; MDR + AI Act 2024/1689 jointly review high-risk AI medical devices.

🩻

Whole-Slide Image (WSI)

數位病理掃描的整張玻片，典型解析度 100,000 × 100,000 pixel、10–20 GB。需要 pyramidal tile storage（OpenSlide、DICOM-WSI）；AI 推論必須 tile-based 並設 overlap 處理邊界。

A digitally scanned full pathology slide, typically 100,000 × 100,000 px and 10–20 GB. Requires pyramidal tile storage (OpenSlide, DICOM-WSI); AI inference must be tile-based with overlap to handle boundaries.

🧠

Foundation Model (Imaging)

在大規模影像（百萬到億級）上做 self-supervised pretraining（MAE / SimCLR / DINO / CLIP-style），可下游 fine-tune 到多種任務、需要的標註量大幅降低。是 2023–2026 醫療影像 AI 的範式轉移。

Self-supervised pretraining on large image corpora (millions to billions) using MAE / SimCLR / DINO / CLIP-style; fine-tunable to many downstream tasks with sharply reduced label needs. The 2023–2026 paradigm shift in medical imaging AI.

概覽

醫療影像 foundation 模型是什麼？

2023 年以前，每一個醫療影像 AI 都是「task-specific」——糖網病分級訓一個、肺結節偵測訓一個、乳房 mass 分類再訓一個。每個任務都要從頭收集數萬張標註影像。2024–2026 改變了這一切：foundation model——用大規模自監督 (SSL / MAE / contrastive) 在百萬級影像上預訓練，然後對下游任務只需數百到數千張標註即可 fine-tune 到 SOTA。

三個領域代表：眼科有 RETFound（160 萬張 OCT / fundus, MAE pre-train, Nature 2023）；病理有 CONCH（117 萬圖文對, Nat Med 2024）與 Virchow 2（Paige.AI）；通用醫療多模態有 MedGemma 1.5。再加上 vision-language（圖+報告聯合預訓練）的崛起，影像 AI 從「分類器」進化為「能寫報告、能回答 query」的助手。

Before 2023, every clinical imaging AI was task-specific — one model for diabetic retinopathy grading, another for lung nodule detection, another for breast mass classification. Each needed tens of thousands of labelled images from scratch. 2024–2026 changed this: foundation models are pre-trained with self-supervised learning (SSL / MAE / contrastive) on millions of images, then fine-tuned to SOTA with only hundreds to thousands of labels.

Three flagships per modality: ophthalmology — RETFound (1.6M OCT / fundus, MAE pre-train, Nature 2023); pathology — CONCH (1.17M image-text pairs, Nat Med 2024) and Virchow 2 (Paige.AI); general medical multimodality — MedGemma 1.5. Combined with vision-language joint pre-training, imaging AI moved from classifier to assistant that can write reports and answer queries.

💡
核心心法：foundation 模型把「需 10000 張標註」的任務縮成「200 張」。研究上的價值在於——讓你可以做小樣本研究、稀有疾病、雙盲驗證；但臨床部署仍要過 FDA 510(k) / De Novo / PMA 與 EU MDR + AI Act 雙重 conformity，技術成熟不等於監管通過。 Core mindset: Foundation models cut tasks that needed 10000 labels down to 200. Research value: small-sample studies, rare diseases, double-blind validation. Clinical deployment still requires FDA 510(k) / De Novo / PMA and EU MDR + AI Act dual conformity — technical maturity is not the same as regulatory approval.

三、三個典型載入與推論流程

# RETFound — fine-tune for diabetic retinopathy on 200 fundus images
import torch
from retfound import RETFoundModel

backbone = RETFoundModel.from_pretrained("rmaphoh/RETFound_cfp")
# Freeze backbone, train a 5-class DR head
for p in backbone.parameters(): p.requires_grad = False

head = torch.nn.Linear(1024, 5)            # 5 DR grades
opt = torch.optim.AdamW(head.parameters(), lr=1e-3)

for imgs, lbls in train_loader:        # ~200 labelled images
    feats = backbone.encode(imgs)
    logits = head(feats)
    loss = torch.nn.functional.cross_entropy(logits, lbls)
    opt.zero_grad(); loss.backward(); opt.step()
# Reaches expert-level grading from a few hundred samples.
# CONCH — pathology image captioning
from conch.open_clip_custom import create_model_from_pretrained
import torch, PIL.Image

mdl, preprocess = create_model_from_pretrained("conch_ViT-B-16")
mdl.eval()

img = preprocess(PIL.Image.open("tumor_patch.png")).unsqueeze(0)
candidates = [
  "invasive ductal carcinoma, grade 2",
  "normal mammary tissue",
  "DCIS, intermediate grade",
  "fibroadenoma"]

with torch.no_grad():
    img_feat = mdl.encode_image(img)
    txt_feat = mdl.encode_text(candidates)
    sims = (img_feat @ txt_feat.T).softmax(dim=-1)

print(list(zip(candidates, sims[0].tolist())))
# DICOM → AI inference pipeline (radiology)
import pydicom, torch
from torchvision import transforms

ds = pydicom.dcmread("chest_xray.dcm")
arr = ds.pixel_array                       # H × W (uint16)

# Apply DICOM window-level then normalise to model's expected range
arr = (arr.clip(0, 4000) / 4000).astype("float32")

tfm = transforms.Compose([
    transforms.ToTensor(),
    transforms.Resize((512, 512)),
    transforms.Normalize([0.5], [0.5])])

x = tfm(arr).unsqueeze(0)
model = torch.load("chest_xray_fm_head.pt"); model.eval()
with torch.no_grad():
    findings = model(x)                    # 14-class pathology vector

真實案例

兩個深入案例：RETFound 進入 NHS 試點、CONCH 把罕見癌症報告時間縮短 40%

👁️

案例 1：RETFound 在 diabetic retinopathy 達 ophthalmologist level，2024 進入 NHS 試點

Zhou 等人在 Nature 2023 公開 RETFound（Moorfields Eye Hospital + UCL），用 MAE 在 1.6M OCT + colour fundus 影像上做 self-supervised pretraining。下游糖網病（diabetic retinopathy, DR）5-class grading：僅用 1000 張標註 fine-tune，sensitivity 94.1% / specificity 91.0%，在 EyePACS、Messidor-2 等 hold-out 上達到 ophthalmologist-level。2024 NHS England 在多家信託（Trust）試點：將 RETFound fine-tune 整合到 DR screening 工作流程，AI 對「可能 DR」做初篩、轉介眼科醫師確認；初步報告顯示 screen-positive rate 維持、ophthalmologist workload 降低 35%、平均 turnaround time 從 14 天降到 5 天。關鍵設計：(a) AI 只做 triage，不做最終診斷，符合 SaMD Class IIb；(b) 失敗模式（low-quality image, 影像 ungradable）必須能自動偵測並送回人工；(c) 模型不直接給 patient，而是給 ophthalmologist 一份「待確認 list」；(d) 部署期間做 prospective monitoring（含 false-negative rate）。啟示：foundation model 進入臨床的成功路徑不是「取代醫師」，而是 augmentation + triage + quality assurance。

Case 1: RETFound reaches ophthalmologist level on DR and enters NHS pilots in 2024

Zhou et al. published RETFound in Nature 2023 (Moorfields Eye Hospital + UCL), pretrained with MAE on 1.6M OCT + colour fundus images. Downstream 5-class diabetic retinopathy (DR) grading: with just 1000 labels fine-tuned, sensitivity 94.1% / specificity 91.0% on EyePACS, Messidor-2 hold-outs — ophthalmologist-level. In 2024 NHS England piloted the model across several Trusts, integrating RETFound fine-tunes into DR screening: AI triages likely-DR cases, ophthalmologists confirm. Early reports: screen-positive rate maintained, ophthalmologist workload down 35%, mean turnaround time from 14 to 5 days. Key design choices: (a) AI only triages, never finalises a diagnosis, fitting SaMD Class IIb; (b) failure modes (ungradable / low-quality images) must auto-detect and route back to humans; (c) the model outputs a "to-confirm list" to the ophthalmologist, never directly to patients; (d) prospective monitoring (including false-negative rate) runs throughout deployment. The lesson: foundation models reach the clinic not by replacing physicians, but through augmentation + triage + quality assurance.

🔬

案例 2：CONCH 用於罕見癌症病理 zero-shot captioning 縮短報告時間 40%

Lu 等人 Nat Med 2024 公開 CONCH，用 1.17M 病理 image-text pairs 做 CLIP-style vision-language pretraining。2025 年某 academic medical center 把它整合進「罕見癌症 second opinion 工作流程」：當一線病理師拿到罕見組織學（如 spitzoid melanoma、SETD2-mutated sarcoma），先用 CONCH 對候選 caption（"high-grade spitzoid neoplasm"、"benign spindle cell lesion" 等）做 zero-shot retrieval，並提供 5 個 historical similar cases 的 reference。盲評：(a) diagnostic accuracy 與獨立 second opinion 相當（Cohen's κ = 0.78 vs human-human 0.81）；(b) 平均報告時間從 4.2 hr 降到 2.5 hr（縮短 40%）；(c) 病理師主觀「自信度」提升，特別是 junior staff。但有三個 caveat：(a) 模型對「過於罕見」的 entity 仍會 hallucinate 似是而非 caption；(b) zero-shot retrieval 對 IHC stain panel 敏感，染色組合改變後表現下降；(c) 模型 cannot replace molecular confirmation（NGS、FISH）。啟示：CONCH 類 VLM 在「assist, not replace」的工作流程中最有價值；vision-language pretrain 比純 vision encoder 更適合報告類任務。

Case 2: CONCH cuts rare-cancer pathology reporting time by 40% via zero-shot captioning

Lu et al. Nat Med 2024 published CONCH, a CLIP-style vision-language model pretrained on 1.17M pathology image-text pairs. In 2025 an academic medical centre integrated it into a "rare-cancer second-opinion workflow": when first-line pathologists encountered rare histology (spitzoid melanoma, SETD2-mutated sarcoma), CONCH performed zero-shot retrieval over candidate captions ("high-grade spitzoid neoplasm", "benign spindle cell lesion") and surfaced 5 similar historical cases. Blinded evaluation: (a) diagnostic accuracy matched independent second opinions (Cohen's κ = 0.78 vs human-human 0.81); (b) mean reporting time dropped from 4.2 hr to 2.5 hr (40% reduction); (c) pathologists' subjective confidence rose, especially for junior staff. Caveats: (a) for ultra-rare entities the model still hallucinates plausible-sounding captions; (b) zero-shot retrieval is sensitive to IHC stain panels — changing the panel degrades performance; (c) the model cannot replace molecular confirmation (NGS, FISH). The lesson: CONCH-class VLMs are most valuable in "assist, not replace" workflows; vision-language pretraining suits reporting tasks better than a pure vision encoder.

常見誤區

新手部署醫療影像 FM 最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：把 RETFound 直接用在 OCT 而沒 fine-tune（或反過來，fundus model 用於 OCT）
為什麼錯：RETFound 有兩個 variant：rmaphoh/RETFound_cfp（colour fundus photo）與 rmaphoh/RETFound_oct（OCT）；雖然都是眼科，但 fundus 是 2D 反射、OCT 是 3D 斷層，兩者的 representation 差異巨大。某團隊曾誤把 CFP variant 拿來 fine-tune OCT 任務，AUROC 從預期 0.92 掉到 0.78。
正確做法：(a) 先確認 variant 對應你的 modality；(b) 跨 modality 任務（如 fundus + OCT 聯合分析）需要分別 fine-tune 再 fusion；(c) 對非眼科 modality（如皮膚科、放射科），RETFound 完全不適用——換成 MedGemma、Virchow 或對應領域 FM。
Pitfall 1: Using RETFound on OCT without fine-tuning (or fundus model on OCT)
Why wrong: RETFound ships two variants — rmaphoh/RETFound_cfp (colour fundus) and rmaphoh/RETFound_oct. Both are ophthalmology, but fundus is 2D reflective and OCT is 3D tomographic — vastly different representations. A team once fine-tuned the CFP variant on an OCT task and AUROC dropped from an expected 0.92 to 0.78.
Fix: (a) match the variant to your modality first; (b) cross-modality tasks (joint fundus + OCT) need separate fine-tunes plus fusion; (c) for non-ophthalmology modalities (dermatology, radiology) RETFound doesn't apply — switch to MedGemma, Virchow, or the relevant domain FM.
⚠️
誤區 2：不做 site bias 校正直接 deploy 跨醫院
為什麼錯：醫療影像 site bias 來源眾多：scanner manufacturer / model（GE vs Siemens vs Canon）、acquisition protocol（dose、reconstruction kernel、coil 排列）、染色 protocol（H&E lab 差異）、patient population（age, ethnicity, disease prevalence）。某 chest X-ray AI 在 Stanford training set 上 AUROC 0.93，部署到非洲某醫院後降到 0.71，事後分析發現 75% 性能差距來自 scanner brand 與 X-ray dose 差異（Zech 2018 PLoS Med 經典案例）。
正確做法：(a) 部署前在 target site 做 prospective validation（> 1000 cases）；(b) 用 multi-site training 或 domain adaptation 減少 site bias；(c) 部署後做 continuous monitoring（concept drift、subgroup performance）；(d) 把 site / device metadata 當作 prediction 的 conditioning input。
Pitfall 2: Deploying across hospitals without site-bias correction
Why wrong: medical imaging site bias has many sources — scanner manufacturer / model (GE vs Siemens vs Canon), acquisition protocol (dose, reconstruction kernel, coil layout), staining protocol (H&E lab differences), patient population (age, ethnicity, disease prevalence). A chest X-ray AI scoring AUROC 0.93 at Stanford dropped to 0.71 at an African hospital; 75% of the gap traced to scanner brand and X-ray dose (Zech 2018 PLoS Med).
Fix: (a) prospectively validate at the target site (>1000 cases) before deployment; (b) use multi-site training or domain adaptation to reduce site bias; (c) post-deployment continuous monitoring (concept drift, subgroup performance); (d) include site / device metadata as conditioning input to the prediction.
⚠️
誤區 3：AI 結果寫入 EHR 沒 audit trail
為什麼錯：醫療 AI 部署的 SaMD 規範要求每個 inference 都應有 audit trail：何時、哪個版本模型、輸入影像 hash、輸出 prediction、confidence。某醫院初版只記錄「最終 prediction」沒記模型版本與輸入；半年後模型升級後發現舊預測有 systematic error，因無 audit trail 無法回溯哪些病人受影響。HIPAA、GDPR、EU AI Act 都要求 explainability + auditability。
正確做法：(a) 每個 inference 必須記錄 model version, input hash, output, confidence, timestamp；(b) 至少保存 6 年（HIPAA）；(c) 用 immutable log（Append-only DB、blockchain-like）防 tampering；(d) 對任何模型更新做明確 version bump 並回溯影響評估。
Pitfall 3: AI results writing to EHR without audit trail
Why wrong: medical AI SaMD requires per-inference audit trails: when, which model version, input image hash, output prediction, confidence. One hospital's first version only logged "final prediction" without model version or input. Six months later, after a model upgrade revealed a systematic error in older predictions, with no audit trail they couldn't trace affected patients. HIPAA, GDPR, EU AI Act all require explainability + auditability.
Fix: (a) every inference must log model version, input hash, output, confidence, timestamp; (b) retain at least 6 years (HIPAA); (c) use immutable logs (append-only DB, blockchain-like) to prevent tampering; (d) explicit version bumps and retroactive impact assessment on any model update.
⚠️
誤區 4：把 FDA 510(k) clearance 誤判為「診斷使用」
為什麼錯：FDA 510(k) 通過的 indication for use 有嚴格範圍。多數放射 AI 是 CADt（Computer-Aided Triage — 排序 worklist 用）或 CADe（Detection — 標出可疑區域給醫師看），不是診斷工具。CADt / CADe 的 indication 只允許「輔助醫師決定優先級」或「偵測 candidate lesions」，醫師仍須做最終診斷。某醫院誤把 CADt 工具的「high priority flag」當作 diagnosis 直接通知病人，被 FDA 警告。
正確做法：(a) 仔細閱讀 device 的 indication for use 文件；(b) UI 設計上明確標示「This is a triage aid, not a diagnosis」；(c) 部署前 IRB + risk management plan；(d) 區分 CADt / CADe / CADx（diagnostic）對應不同 510(k) 等級。
Pitfall 4: Mistaking FDA 510(k) clearance for "diagnostic use"
Why wrong: an FDA 510(k)-cleared device has a strictly scoped "indication for use". Most radiology AI is CADt (Computer-Aided Triage — worklist prioritisation) or CADe (Detection — flag suspicious regions), not diagnostic. CADt / CADe indications only permit "assist physician with priority" or "detect candidate lesions"; the clinician makes the final diagnosis. One hospital treated a CADt tool's "high priority flag" as a diagnosis and notified the patient directly — FDA warning letter ensued.
Fix: (a) read the device's indication-for-use document carefully; (b) UI must clearly say "This is a triage aid, not a diagnosis"; (c) IRB + risk management plan before deployment; (d) distinguish CADt / CADe / CADx (diagnostic) — they correspond to different 510(k) tiers.
⚠️
誤區 5：EU MDR + AI Act 雙重審查未考量
為什麼錯：在歐盟部署醫療 AI 需要同時通過 (a) MDR 2017/745 醫材 conformity（多數影像 AI 是 Class IIb，須 notified body 參與），與 (b) AI Act 2024/1689 高風險 AI 義務（風險管理系統、data governance、technical documentation、human oversight、post-market monitoring）。某 startup 以為通過 FDA 510(k) 就能直接賣到歐洲，被擋在 CE marking 階段，加上 AI Act 高風險條款 2026/8/2 生效，整套 documentation 需重做。
正確做法：(a) 早期就把 EU 路徑納入產品 roadmap；(b) MDR 與 AI Act 共用很多 documentation（risk management、data quality），但 AI Act 多了 transparency 與 human oversight；(c) 找一家 EU-based notified body（如 BSI、TÜV）儘早 engage；(d) 注意 AI Act 對 GPAI（general-purpose AI）的特別規定，如基於 frontier LLM 的醫療 AI 可能受 GPAI 條款影響。
Pitfall 5: Forgetting the EU MDR + AI Act dual review
Why wrong: deploying medical AI in the EU requires both (a) MDR 2017/745 conformity (most imaging AI is Class IIb, requiring a notified body), and (b) AI Act 2024/1689 high-risk AI obligations (risk management system, data governance, technical documentation, human oversight, post-market monitoring). A startup assumed FDA 510(k) was enough for Europe and got stopped at CE marking; with high-risk AI Act provisions effective 2 Aug 2026, they had to redo the documentation.
Fix: (a) build the EU pathway into the roadmap early; (b) MDR and AI Act share much documentation (risk management, data quality), but AI Act adds transparency and human oversight; (c) engage an EU notified body (BSI, TÜV) as early as possible; (d) watch for AI Act's special provisions on GPAI (general-purpose AI) — medical AI built on frontier LLMs may fall under GPAI rules.
資深讀者深入

進階細節

🔬 MAE vs SimCLR vs CLIP-style 在醫療影像的權衡、FDA PCCP、Virchow 2 dataset 細節

① Self-supervised pretrain 選擇：MAE vs SimCLR vs DINOv2 vs CLIP-style。四條主流路徑：(a) MAE (He 2022)——隨機 mask 75% patches，重建像素。優點：強迫模型學 global structure，對 medical imaging 特別 work（因為 anatomy 是 structural）；RETFound 用這條。(b) SimCLR / MoCo (Chen 2020, He 2020)——contrastive learning，同一張影像 augmentation pair → close，不同影像 → far。早期 SOTA 但 batch size 要大、augmentation 設計關鍵。(c) DINO / DINOv2 (Caron 2021, Oquab 2024)——self-distillation，student 預測 teacher 對 augmented view 的 representation。對 dense prediction（segmentation）友善；Virchow 2 後段使用 DINOv2-style。(d) CLIP-style (Radford 2021)——image-text contrastive，需要 paired image + caption；CONCH、Med-Gemini 用這條，獲得 zero-shot caption / retrieval 能力。選擇原則：純 classification → MAE 或 DINO；要 zero-shot caption / retrieval → CLIP-style（需 paired data）；dense prediction（segmentation）→ DINO。

② FDA PCCP（Predetermined Change Control Plan）。傳統 510(k)：每次模型升級都要重新申請。AI/ML 模型若無法持續更新等於 dead-on-arrival。FDA 2023 推出 PCCP（FDA Guidance "Predetermined Change Control Plans for Machine Learning-Enabled Device Software Functions"）：廠商可在初次申請時提交「預定變更計畫」（哪些可更新、如何驗證、performance 不下降的 threshold、retraining trigger），通過後可在範圍內自動更新而不需重新申請。但 PCCP 也有限：(a) 不允許「擴張 indication for use」；(b) 不允許改 model architecture；(c) retraining data 必須符合預先聲明的 distribution。Virchow 2 等 frontier 病理 FM 已開始試用 PCCP。

③ Virchow 2 dataset 細節（Vorontsov 2024 / Paige.AI 2025）。Virchow 2 在 3.1M WSI 上預訓練，來自 Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center（MSKCC）內部累積的數位病理 archive，涵蓋 17 個 organ system、~200 種 cancer subtypes、IHC + H&E + 部分 immunofluorescence。Pretrain objective: DINOv2-style + register tokens。技術細節：(a) ViT-H/14, 632M params；(b) 224×224 patch input；(c) data sampling 平衡 organ system 與 disease rarity；(d) 釋出 ViT-H 與 ViT-G 兩個尺寸。下游應用：HER2 quantification、PD-L1 scoring、MSI prediction、tumour budding、survival prediction。Paige.AI 商業 license 模式，部分產品（PaigeProstate 等）已通過 FDA 510(k)。CONCH 與 Virchow 的差別：CONCH 是 vision-language（接 1.17M 圖文對），擅長 captioning；Virchow 是純 vision encoder（無 text），擅長 feature extraction 後接下游 task。

④ Foundation model 通過 FDA 後再 fine-tune 的監管問題。關鍵爭議：原始 foundation backbone 通過 510(k)，下游醫院 fine-tune 後是否需重新申請？目前 FDA 立場（2024 RFI / 2025 draft guidance）大致為：(a) 若 fine-tune 改變 indication for use → 必須重新申請；(b) 若僅在原 indication 內 site-specific tune 且符合 PCCP → 可不重新申請；(c) clinical-deployed 模型 fine-tune 必須是「locked」狀態，動態 learning（online learning）目前 FDA 一般不允許醫療用途。歐盟 AI Act 對 substantial modification 也有類似條款，臨床部署中的 fine-tune 屬於敏感區。

① SSL pretraining choices: MAE vs SimCLR vs DINOv2 vs CLIP-style. Four mainstream paths: (a) MAE (He 2022) — randomly mask 75% of patches and reconstruct pixels. Strength: forces the model to learn global structure, especially fitting for medical imaging where anatomy is structural. RETFound uses this. (b) SimCLR / MoCo (Chen 2020, He 2020) — contrastive learning, augmentation pairs of the same image → close, different images → far. Early SOTA but needs large batch size and careful augmentations. (c) DINO / DINOv2 (Caron 2021, Oquab 2024) — self-distillation: student predicts teacher's representation of augmented views. Friendly to dense prediction (segmentation); Virchow 2 uses DINOv2-style later. (d) CLIP-style (Radford 2021) — image-text contrastive on paired data; CONCH and Med-Gemini take this route, gaining zero-shot caption / retrieval. Selection rule: pure classification → MAE or DINO; zero-shot caption / retrieval → CLIP-style (needs paired data); dense prediction (segmentation) → DINO.

② FDA PCCP (Predetermined Change Control Plan). Traditional 510(k) requires re-submission for every model update — a dead-on-arrival constraint for AI/ML that needs continuous improvement. FDA's 2023 guidance "Predetermined Change Control Plans for Machine Learning-Enabled Device Software Functions" lets vendors pre-declare in the initial submission: which updates are allowed, how they're validated, performance non-degradation thresholds, retraining triggers. After PCCP clearance, updates within scope don't require new submissions. Limits: (a) cannot expand indication for use; (b) cannot change model architecture; (c) retraining data must match the pre-declared distribution. Virchow 2-class frontier pathology FMs are starting to use PCCP.

③ Virchow 2 dataset details (Vorontsov 2024 / Paige.AI 2025). Virchow 2 pretrains on 3.1M WSI from Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (MSKCC)'s internal digital pathology archive, spanning 17 organ systems, ~200 cancer subtypes, IHC + H&E + some immunofluorescence. Pretrain: DINOv2-style + register tokens. Technical: (a) ViT-H/14, 632M params; (b) 224×224 patch input; (c) sampling balances organ system + disease rarity; (d) released in ViT-H and ViT-G. Downstream applications: HER2 quantification, PD-L1 scoring, MSI prediction, tumour budding, survival prediction. Paige.AI commercial license; some products (PaigeProstate) have FDA 510(k). CONCH vs Virchow: CONCH is vision-language (1.17M image-text pairs), great for captioning; Virchow is a pure vision encoder (no text), great for downstream feature extraction.

④ Regulatory question: fine-tuning a 510(k)-cleared foundation model. Key controversy: if the original foundation backbone passes 510(k) and a downstream hospital fine-tunes it, does it need re-submission? Current FDA stance (2024 RFI / 2025 draft guidance): (a) fine-tune that changes indication for use → re-submission required; (b) site-specific tune within the original indication and PCCP scope → no re-submission needed; (c) clinically deployed models must be "locked" — dynamic (online) learning is currently not permitted for medical use. EU AI Act has parallel "substantial modification" provisions; clinical-deployment fine-tunes are a sensitive zone.

Q2: 影像 AI 該負哪些責任？醫師、廠商、醫院如何分擔？

Q2: Who's responsible when imaging AI fails — physician, vendor, hospital?

An AI imaging system misses a lung nodule, marked benign; 6 months later the patient is diagnosed with late-stage lung cancer. Q3: Site bias 校正該由廠商還是醫院負責？

Q3: Who fixes site bias — vendor or hospital?

Zech 2018 showed a chest X-ray AI dropped from AUROC 0.93 at Stanford to 0.71 at an African hospital — site bias drove most of the gap. Q4: How open should medical-AI model weights be?

RETFound, CONCH, MedGemma release research-use weights; Virchow is commercial-license; Med-Gemini is API-only.
