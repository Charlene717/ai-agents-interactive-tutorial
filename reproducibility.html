隱私、法規與可重現性

HIPAA、EU AI Act、Model Card、Prompt 版控——讓你的 AI 研究在 5 年後仍能被同行重現。

HIPAA, EU AI Act, model cards, prompt version control — make your AI research reproducible by peers five years from now.

術語速查

隱私 / 法規 / 可重現性必懂十二詞

HIPAA、GDPR、EU AI Act、Model Card——這 12 個術語橫跨美 / 歐法規與工程實踐，是讀懂本章 SOP 與 advanced 章節的基礎。

HIPAA, GDPR, EU AI Act, Model Card — these 12 terms span US / EU regulation and engineering practice, and they unlock the SOPs and advanced section in this chapter.

🇺🇸

HIPAA

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act（1996）。美國對 PHI（Protected Health Information）的核心隱私法。Privacy Rule + Security Rule + Breach Notification Rule 三大支柱。違規可罰 $100–$50K 每筆 + 年上限 $1.5M + 刑責。

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (1996). The US privacy law for PHI (Protected Health Information). Three pillars: Privacy Rule, Security Rule, Breach Notification Rule. Penalties $100–$50K per record, $1.5M annual cap, plus criminal exposure.

📝

BAA

Business Associate Agreement。HIPAA 要求 covered entity 與處理 PHI 的第三方（含 cloud AI）簽訂。BAA 規定 vendor 的安全責任、breach notification、auditing。沒 BAA 的服務不可處理 PHI。

Business Associate Agreement. HIPAA requires covered entities to sign one with any third party (including cloud AI) handling PHI. The BAA defines vendor security responsibilities, breach notification, and auditing. Services without a BAA cannot process PHI.

🇪🇺

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation（Reg 2016/679，2018/5/25 適用）。歐盟的個人資料保護法，比 HIPAA 範圍更廣（所有個資而非僅 health）。違規可罰營業額 4%（上限 €20M）。跨境傳輸需 SCC / Adequacy Decision。

General Data Protection Regulation (Reg 2016/679, applicable 25 May 2018). The EU's personal-data law — broader scope than HIPAA (all personal data, not just health). Fines up to 4% of revenue (max €20M). Cross-border transfers need SCCs / Adequacy Decisions.

⚖️

EU AI Act

Reg 2024/1689（2024/8/1 entry into force）。世界第一個橫向 AI 法。risk-based：unacceptable / high / limited / minimal。高風險義務 2026/8/2 一般生效；嵌入 MDR / IVDR 醫材的 AI 延至 2027/8/2。

Reg 2024/1689 (entry into force 1 Aug 2024). The world's first horizontal AI law. Risk-based: unacceptable / high / limited / minimal. General high-risk obligations apply 2 Aug 2026; AI embedded in MDR / IVDR devices is extended to 2 Aug 2027.

🔴

High-risk AI

EU AI Act Annex III 列舉的 high-risk 領域：醫療診斷、招聘、教育、執法等。義務含 risk management、data governance、technical documentation（Annex IV）、human oversight、accuracy & robustness。

Annex III of the EU AI Act lists high-risk domains: medical diagnosis, hiring, education, law enforcement. Obligations include risk management, data governance, technical documentation (Annex IV), human oversight, and accuracy & robustness.

🏛️

IRB

Institutional Review Board（美）= REC / Ethics Committee（歐）。對 human subject research 做事前 ethical review。對 AI 研究，「使用 patient data」與「對病人提供 AI 服務」皆須申請。Common Rule 2018 修訂允許 exempt / expedited path。

Institutional Review Board (US) = REC / Ethics Committee (EU). Pre-approves human-subject research. For AI studies, both "using patient data" and "providing AI services to patients" require submission. The 2018 Common Rule revision allows exempt / expedited tracks.

🎭

De-identification

移除個人識別資訊使資料無法回溯到個人。HIPAA 兩條路徑：Safe Harbor（移除 18 identifiers）或 Expert Determination（統計學家證明 risk minimal）。GDPR 對 anonymization 標準更嚴。

Removing identifying information so data cannot be linked back to an individual. HIPAA offers two paths: Safe Harbor (remove 18 identifiers) or Expert Determination (statistician certifies minimal risk). GDPR's anonymization standard is stricter.

🆔

PHI

Protected Health Information。可識別個人的健康相關資料（姓名、日期、地址、MRN、IP address、photo 等 18 類 Safe Harbor identifiers）。任何 PHI 處理皆受 HIPAA 規範。

Protected Health Information. Identifiable health-related data (name, dates, address, MRN, IP, photo — the 18 Safe Harbor identifier categories). Any PHI handling falls under HIPAA.

📇

Model Card

Mitchell 2019 FAccT 提出的 AI 模型「公開規格表」：intended use、training data、performance、limitations、ethical considerations。HuggingFace Hub 標準化此格式；2024 後成 EU AI Act Annex IV 技術文件主流範本。

Mitchell 2019 FAccT: a public "spec sheet" for AI models covering intended use, training data, performance, limitations, ethical considerations. HuggingFace Hub standardised this format; since 2024 it has become the de facto Annex IV template under the EU AI Act.

📊

Data Card

對應於 Model Card 的 dataset 版本（Gebru 2021 Datasheets for Datasets）。記錄資料來源、demographics、annotation process、known biases、licensing。FAIR 原則的 metadata 實踐工具。

The dataset counterpart to a Model Card (Gebru 2021 Datasheets for Datasets). Records sources, demographics, annotation process, known biases, and licensing. A practical tool for FAIR metadata.

📌

Prompt Versioning

把 prompt 當 code 一樣 version control。Git + YAML schema（prompt_id、model、version、temperature、seed、cutoff、created_at）為 2024 後社群共識。LangSmith、PromptLayer、Helicone 為工具。

Version-controlling prompts like code. Git + a YAML schema (prompt_id, model, version, temperature, seed, cutoff, created_at) is the post-2024 community consensus. Tools: LangSmith, PromptLayer, Helicone.

♻️

FAIR

Findable、Accessible、Interoperable、Reusable（Wilkinson 2016 Sci Data）。NIH DMS Policy 2023 與 EU AI Act Annex IV 皆引用 FAIR 為 data / model metadata 標準。Zenodo / OSF / Dataverse 提供 FAIR-compliant archiving。

Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable (Wilkinson 2016 Sci Data). The NIH 2023 DMS Policy and EU AI Act Annex IV both cite FAIR for data / model metadata. Zenodo / OSF / Dataverse offer FAIR-compliant archiving.

概覽

把 AI 當成「實驗變數」管理

恭喜你走到最後一步。前面 19 章教你怎麼用 AI；這一章教你怎麼讓你的 AI 工作不被未來打臉——五年後別人想重現你的結果，會看到完整 prompt、模型版本、temperature、seed、retrieval index 版本。沒有這些，你的研究就和「2010 年某篇沒附原始程式碼的 paper」一樣不可重現。

You made it to the last step. The previous 19 modules taught you to use AI; this one teaches you to make your AI work not haunt you — five years out, a peer who tries to reproduce your result will see the full prompt, model version, temperature, seed, and retrieval index version. Without these, your study is as irreproducible as a 2010 paper with no source code.

Three simultaneous pressures: (1) HIPAA / GDPR — public ChatGPT does not satisfy HIPAA. In January 2026 OpenAI launched ChatGPT for Healthcare with BAA + data residency + KMS + audit; (2) EU AI Act (Reg 2024/1689) — high-risk obligations apply from 2 Aug 2026 (extended to 2 Aug 2027 for AI embedded in MDR Class IIb/III or IVDR Class C/D devices); (3) FAIR + reproducibility — version-control prompts like code. 30 minutes here saves 5 years of peer review pain later.

💡
核心心法：把 prompt + 模型版本 + temperature + seed + retrieval index 版本視為實驗變數。它們和你的 R 腳本、計算機 random seed 一樣，必須存 Git、寫進 Methods、跟著論文一起 archive。 Core mindset: Treat prompt + model version + temperature + seed + retrieval index version as experimental variables. They are as important as your R scripts and random seeds — commit to Git, write into Methods, archive alongside the paper.

① 釐清用途

寫一行話：「我要用 AI 做什麼？」例：「用 Claude 從 50 篇 PubMed 文獻萃取 effect size」。

Write one sentence: "What am I using AI for?" E.g., "Use Claude to extract effect sizes from 50 PubMed papers."

② 檢查法規

輸入是否含 PHI？是否跨境（GDPR / EU AI Act）？是否需 IRB / BAA / DUA？

Does input contain PHI? Cross-border (GDPR / EU AI Act)? Need IRB / BAA / DUA?

③ 安全資料路徑

選有 BAA 的版本 / on-prem / 去識別化；確認資料駐留位置與 KMS。

Pick BAA-covered / on-prem / de-identified path; verify data residency and KMS.

④ 版本化 prompt

把 prompt + model version + temperature + seed 存成 YAML 進 Git。

Store prompt + model version + temperature + seed as YAML in Git.

⑤ Methods 揭露

把 prompt YAML 路徑、commit hash、model ID 寫進 Methods（範本見上）。

Cite the prompt YAML path, commit hash, model ID inside Methods (template above).

⑥ Archive + FAIR

論文投稿時把 prompt repo 上 Zenodo / OSF，取 DOI；資料、模型、prompt 達 FAIR。

On submission, push the prompt repo to Zenodo / OSF for a DOI; data, model, prompts all FAIR.

真實案例

兩個深入案例

🏥

案例 1：某 medical center 用 ChatGPT Enterprise + BAA 處理 de-identified discharge summary

2025 年某美國 academic medical center（500 床，HHS-defined hybrid entity）想用 ChatGPT 加速 discharge summary 草擬。Compliance、IT、Legal 三部門 6 個月協調出以下 SOP（已通過 HHS OCR 諮詢與 IRB QI exempt 審查）。架構：(1) 採購 ChatGPT Enterprise（$60/user/month），與 OpenAI 簽 BAA + Data Processing Addendum，啟用 zero-retention（API 不留 input/output 30+ days）+ SOC 2 Type II 稽核；(2) 工作流程：admission note + progress + procedure 從 Epic 經內部 de-identification middleware（自寫，移除 18 個 Safe Harbor identifiers + date shifting + ZIP3 generalisation）後寫入 internal LLM-gateway；(3) gateway 呼叫 ChatGPT Enterprise，回傳 draft；(4) Epic 內 attending 看 draft，必填回 PHI（病人姓名 / 日期 / MRN）後 sign-off；(5) 全程 EHR audit trail（誰、何時、用什麼 prompt、是否修改）；(6) 每月 sample 100 份做 bias check（race / sex / age 子群）。結果：90 天後，discharge summary 寫作時間從中位數 22 分鐘降至 11 分鐘（−50%）；attending 滿意度 NPS +48；無 HIPAA breach；醫師端「實質編輯」（> 5% 改動）比例 63%——說明 AI 是助手而非 ghost-writer。關鍵教訓：BAA 不是「簽完就好」，是與 de-identification pipeline + audit trail + bias monitoring 共構的 SOP；compliance 的 6 個月看似漫長，但避免了一次 HIPAA breach 的 $1M+ 罰款 + reputation damage。

Case 1: An academic medical center running de-identified discharge summaries through ChatGPT Enterprise + BAA

In 2025 a US academic medical center (500 beds, HHS-defined hybrid entity) wanted to accelerate discharge-summary drafting. Compliance, IT, and Legal spent 6 months designing the SOP (passed HHS OCR consultation and an IRB QI-exempt review). Architecture: (1) procure ChatGPT Enterprise ($60/user/month), sign a BAA + Data Processing Addendum with OpenAI, enable zero-retention (API discards input/output after 30+ days) + SOC 2 Type II audit; (2) workflow: admission note + progress + procedures move from Epic through an in-house de-identification middleware (removing the 18 Safe Harbor identifiers + date shifting + ZIP3 generalisation) into an internal LLM-gateway; (3) the gateway calls ChatGPT Enterprise and returns a draft; (4) inside Epic, the attending reviews the draft, fills back PHI (name / dates / MRN) and signs; (5) full EHR audit trail throughout (who, when, what prompt, what edits); (6) monthly sample of 100 drafts for race / sex / age subgroup bias checks. Outcome: 90 days in, median discharge-summary time dropped from 22 to 11 minutes (−50%); attending NPS +48; no HIPAA breach; "substantive edit" rate (>5% change) was 63% — AI is an assistant, not a ghost-writer. Lessons: a BAA isn't "sign and forget" — it must be embedded in an SOP with de-identification + audit trail + bias monitoring; the 6-month compliance pass looked long but avoided a single HIPAA breach worth $1M+ in fines + reputation damage.

📇

案例 2：HuggingFace Model Card → EU AI Act Annex IV compliance 工具的真實過渡

Model Card 的概念來自 Mitchell 2019 FAccT——當時的設計是 ML 社群自願性公開規格表。HuggingFace 2020 把它變成 Hub 內所有 model 的標準 metadata YAML / Markdown，2023 已涵蓋 ≥ 30 萬 model，事實上成為社群 norm。2024/8 EU AI Act entry into force 後，Annex IV「Technical Documentation for High-Risk AI Systems」明確列出 11 項 mandatory disclosure：(a) general description；(b) detailed description（含 design specification、training data sources）；(c) computational resources + CO2 emissions；(d) risk management documentation；(e) data and data governance；(f) human oversight measures；(g) accuracy / robustness / cybersecurity；(h) post-market monitoring plan；(i) instructions for use；(j) compliance declaration；(k) lineage（intended modifications history）。真實過渡案例：HuggingFace 2024/11 釋出 Model Card v2 template，明確 map 到 Annex IV 11 項；BigCode、Mistral、Meta（Llama 系列）2025 起 model card 即按此格式發布。某 EU biotech 部署 medical NLP 模型，整套 Annex IV 文件 80% 直接重用 HuggingFace Model Card 內容，只需補 risk management、post-market monitoring、CO2 emissions 三項。意義：自願性社群規範與強制法規收斂，HuggingFace 從研究分享平台變成 compliance infrastructure。對小實驗室，這意味著 publishing on Hub 即近似於 AI Act 合規起點；對 EU 企業，這降低了 high-risk AI 文件成本約 60%。但仍有 gap：CO2 計算、lineage tracking、post-market monitoring 需自建。

Case 2: HuggingFace Model Card → EU AI Act Annex IV compliance — a real transition

Model Cards started with Mitchell 2019 FAccT as a voluntary community spec sheet. HuggingFace turned it into the standard YAML / Markdown metadata for every Hub model in 2020; by 2023 it covered 300K+ models and was the de facto community norm. After the EU AI Act entered into force on 1 Aug 2024, Annex IV "Technical Documentation for High-Risk AI Systems" lists 11 mandatory disclosures: (a) general description; (b) detailed description (design spec, training data sources); (c) computational resources + CO2 emissions; (d) risk management; (e) data and data governance; (f) human oversight measures; (g) accuracy / robustness / cybersecurity; (h) post-market monitoring plan; (i) instructions for use; (j) declaration of conformity; (k) lineage (intended modifications history). The real transition: HuggingFace released Model Card v2 in November 2024, explicitly mapped to Annex IV's 11 items; BigCode, Mistral, and Meta (Llama series) ship model cards in this format from 2025. An EU biotech deploying a medical NLP model reused 80% of its Annex IV technical file from its HuggingFace Model Card, supplementing only risk management, post-market monitoring, and CO2 emissions. Why this matters: voluntary community norms converged with mandatory regulation, turning HuggingFace from a research-sharing platform into compliance infrastructure. For small labs, publishing on Hub is now close to an AI Act starting point; for EU companies, this cut high-risk AI documentation cost ~60%. Gaps remain: CO2 calculation, lineage tracking, and post-market monitoring still require custom build.

常見誤區

隱私 / 法規 / 可重現性最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：把 patient name 替換成「Patient A」但留下日期 / 地點仍可重識別
為什麼錯：HIPAA Safe Harbor 列 18 個必須移除的 identifier，包含 dates more specific than year、ZIP 前 3 碼以外、IP、device serial、photo、unique characteristics。只替換姓名而留日期 + 地點 + age > 89 + 罕病組合，可被 reverse-identified（Sweeney 2000 證明 87% 美國人可由 ZIP + 性別 + 生日唯一識別）。Narayanan 2008 用 Netflix Prize 資料 + IMDb 完成 large-scale re-identification。
正確做法：用 Safe Harbor 18 項全清單；date 全部 shift 到固定起點；ZIP 取 ZIP3（< 20k 人口的 ZIP3 還需 generalize）；age > 89 統一為「> 89」；罕病 + 多重 quasi-identifier 需 Expert Determination。
Pitfall 1: Replacing patient name with "Patient A" but leaving dates / locations re-identifiable
Why wrong: HIPAA Safe Harbor lists 18 identifiers to remove, including dates more specific than year, ZIP beyond the first 3 digits, IPs, device serials, photos, unique characteristics. Replacing only the name while keeping dates + location + age >89 + rare disease can be reverse-identified (Sweeney 2000 showed 87% of Americans are uniquely identifiable by ZIP + sex + birthday). Narayanan 2008 did large-scale re-identification on the Netflix Prize dataset + IMDb.
Fix: apply the full Safe Harbor 18-item list; shift all dates to a fixed origin; use ZIP3 (and generalise further for ZIP3 with <20K population); collapse age >89 to ">89"; rare disease + multiple quasi-identifiers require Expert Determination.
⚠️
誤區 2：用 ChatGPT 公開版（沒 BAA）
為什麼錯：ChatGPT Plus（個人版）、Claude.ai 個人版、Gemini 個人版的 ToS 不簽 BAA，HIPAA 規範下不可處理 PHI。一筆 PHI 進入公開 LLM API 即觸發 HIPAA breach（即使「不會被訓練」也算 unauthorized disclosure）。OCR 2024 已對多個醫院罰款 $50K–$240K，因員工把 chart 貼進 ChatGPT 公開版「只是想 summarize」。
正確做法：BAA-covered 路徑——ChatGPT Enterprise（OpenAI 簽 BAA）、Azure OpenAI（Microsoft 簽 BAA + EU data residency 選項）、Claude for Work（Anthropic 簽 BAA）、Google Cloud Vertex AI（Google Cloud BAA）；機構必先簽 BAA 再讓員工用。所有員工 onboarding 應 explicitly 列「禁止公開 LLM 處理 PHI」。
Pitfall 2: Using public ChatGPT (no BAA) for PHI
Why wrong: ChatGPT Plus, personal Claude.ai, and personal Gemini don't sign BAAs; HIPAA forbids them for PHI. A single PHI record sent to a public LLM API triggers a HIPAA breach (even "won't be trained on" counts as unauthorized disclosure). OCR fined multiple hospitals $50K–$240K in 2024 because staff pasted charts into public ChatGPT "just to summarize."
Fix: BAA-covered options — ChatGPT Enterprise (OpenAI BAA), Azure OpenAI (Microsoft BAA + EU data residency), Claude for Work (Anthropic BAA), Google Cloud Vertex AI (Google Cloud BAA); the institution signs BAA before staff use. Onboarding must explicitly list "no public LLM for PHI."
⚠️
誤區 3：prompt 沒存版本，paper 寫法無法重現
為什麼錯：LLM output 對 prompt + model + temperature + seed 都極敏感；換 GPT-4 → GPT-5 結果可能 30%+ 不同。Paper 只寫「used GPT-4 to summarize」5 年後別人無法重現——這就是 2024 後 NIH DMS Policy + ICMJE + AI Act Annex IV 都要求 prompt + 模型版本 + 參數 archive 的原因。
正確做法：把 prompt 當 code，存 Git + YAML schema（prompt_id、model、version、temperature、seed、cutoff、created_at）；每次 paper 投稿前對應 prompt commit hash 存 Zenodo / OSF 取 DOI；Methods 引用該 DOI。LangSmith、PromptLayer、Helicone 提供 enterprise 工具。
Pitfall 3: No prompt versioning — the paper's recipe is irreproducible
Why wrong: LLM output is highly sensitive to prompt + model + temperature + seed; swapping GPT-4 → GPT-5 can shift outputs 30%+. A paper that says "used GPT-4 to summarize" can't be reproduced five years later — exactly why NIH DMS Policy + ICMJE + AI Act Annex IV all require prompts + model versions + parameters to be archived from 2024.
Fix: treat prompts like code — Git + YAML schema (prompt_id, model, version, temperature, seed, cutoff, created_at); before submission, archive the prompt commit hash on Zenodo / OSF with a DOI; cite the DOI in Methods. LangSmith, PromptLayer, Helicone offer enterprise tooling.
⚠️
誤區 4：Model Card 沒寫 CO2 / lineage 違反 EU AI Act
為什麼錯：EU AI Act Annex IV 11 項中明確含「computational resources + CO2 emissions」與「lineage（intended modifications history）」。HuggingFace Model Card 預設 template 已 cover 9/11，但 CO2 emissions（kWh + kgCO2eq）與 lineage tracking 需自填。2026/8/2 後在 EU 部署 high-risk AI 卻沒寫這兩項即違規。
正確做法：訓練時記錄 GPU-hours × power (kW) × grid carbon intensity（如 415 gCO2/kWh for EU avg）；fine-tune 時也記錄 modification history（base model → LoRA → DPO 等步驟）；用 ML CO2 Impact Calculator（Lacoste 2019 + mlco2.github.io）或 CodeCarbon 工具。
Pitfall 4: Model Card missing CO2 / lineage — EU AI Act non-compliance
Why wrong: Annex IV's 11 items explicitly include "computational resources + CO2 emissions" and "lineage (intended modifications history)." The default HuggingFace Model Card covers 9/11, but CO2 emissions (kWh + kgCO2eq) and lineage tracking must be added. From 2 Aug 2026, deploying high-risk AI in the EU without these two = non-compliant.
Fix: log GPU-hours × power (kW) × grid carbon intensity (e.g. 415 gCO2/kWh EU avg) during training; record modification history during fine-tune (base → LoRA → DPO); use the ML CO2 Impact Calculator (Lacoste 2019, mlco2.github.io) or CodeCarbon.
⚠️
誤區 5：把 IRB-approved 範圍外用法當「研究」
為什麼錯：IRB approval 通常 narrow——「retrospective chart review for X disease + de-identified data」與「prospective AI-assisted diagnosis」是兩個 protocol。把 approved retrospective 範圍的 AI tool 用在 prospective decision support 即超範圍，須 amendment 或 new IRB submission。某 lab 在 2024 因此被機構暫停 IRB 1 年。
正確做法：每個 AI use case 對應 specific IRB protocol；scope 變動（資料類別、處理方式、prospective/retrospective、patient-facing/not）皆 trigger amendment；保留 IRB approval letter version 與 protocol diff。
Pitfall 5: Treating out-of-scope use as "still research"
Why wrong: IRB approval is usually narrow — "retrospective chart review for X disease + de-identified data" vs "prospective AI-assisted diagnosis" are two protocols. Using an approved-retrospective AI tool for prospective decision support exceeds scope and requires an amendment or new IRB submission. A 2024 lab was suspended from IRB approval for 1 year over exactly this.
Fix: each AI use case maps to a specific IRB protocol; any scope change (data types, processing, prospective/retrospective, patient-facing/not) triggers an amendment; keep IRB approval letter versions and protocol diffs.

🔬 Safe Harbor 18 識別碼、AI Act 2026/8 vs 2027/8、Model Card 三角色、prompt-as-code YAML

① HIPAA Safe Harbor 18 個 identifier。45 CFR § 164.514(b)(2) 列舉：(1) 姓名；(2) 比州小的地理單位（除 ZIP 前 3 碼，且該 ZIP3 人口 > 20k）；(3) 比 year 更精細的日期（含出生、死亡、入院、出院日）；(4) 電話；(5) 傳真；(6) email；(7) SSN；(8) MRN；(9) 健保號；(10) 帳號；(11) 證書 / 駕照；(12) 車牌 / VIN；(13) device serial number；(14) URL；(15) IP；(16) 生物識別（指紋、聲紋）；(17) 完整臉部 photo；(18) 任何 unique identifying number / characteristic / code。年齡 > 89 必統一為「> 89」（因為 90+ 人口稀疏可重識別）。Expert Determination 路徑：合格 statistician 證明「risk of identification is very small」並存放 method documentation。GDPR 對 anonymization 更嚴：Recital 26 要求「即使結合所有可合理使用 means 也無法 re-identify」，這比 HIPAA Safe Harbor 嚴——多數 HIPAA de-identified 資料在 GDPR 下仍算 pseudonymized。

② EU AI Act 兩個生效時點。Reg 2024/1689 共有 5 個重要時點：(a) 2024/8/1 entry into force；(b) 2025/2/2 Chapter II（prohibited AI practices）+ Chapter III, Section 4（AI literacy obligation）；(c) 2025/8/2 一般用途 AI model（GPAI）義務 + Notifying authorities + 主要 penalty 條款；(d) 2026/8/2 一般 high-risk AI 義務（Article 6(2) Annex III lists：employment、education、law enforcement、medical diagnosis 等）；(e) 2027/8/2 嵌入 MDR Class IIb/III 或 IVDR Class C/D 醫材的 AI 義務（Article 6(1) 涵蓋 safety component of products under existing harmonised legislation）。這「兩階段」是 EU 給醫材廠商額外 1 年遷移時間。對研究實驗室影響：純內部研究多落 minimal-risk，不直接受高風險義務；但若部署 prospective patient-facing AI 即可能落入 Annex III medical diagnosis 高風險範疇。

③ Model Card 三角色。Mitchell 2019 與 HuggingFace 2024 v2 都強調 Model Card 服務三類受眾：(a) Model developers——技術細節（architecture、training data、hyperparameters、CO2、lineage、known limitations）；(b) Sociotechnic / ethics reviewers——bias、fairness、societal impact、misuse potential、demographics 子群表現；(c) Project organizers / deployment teams——intended use、out-of-scope use、operational requirements、licensing、cost、maintenance plan。HuggingFace v2 template 把這三類分為 sections，避免「all-in-one block 沒人讀」。EU AI Act Annex IV 對應這三類角色：(b) 是 risk management + data governance + human oversight；(c) 是 instructions for use + post-market monitoring。

Q1: How should you design a lab SOP for using ChatGPT?

Q2: EU AI Act 對小實驗室的可行性？

Annex IV 11 項要求對 Google / Anthropic / OpenAI 等大廠不難，但 3 人實驗室部署 prospective patient-facing AI 即可能落入高風險範疇，需要 risk management、post-market monitoring、CO2 / lineage 文件。某 EU biotech 估計 high-risk AI 文件約 60% 可由 HuggingFace Model Card 重用，但仍需 ~ 80 工時自填剩下 40%。請思考：你支持 AI Act 對小實驗室嚴格管制嗎？是否該有 SME / academic exemption？怎樣的「compliance-as-a-service」生態系能讓小團隊負擔得起？
