Topic 02

🔤 Tokens 與 Context Window 🔤 Tokens & Context Window 前往該主題教學 →

🧠 進階 Prompt 與 RAG 🧠 Advanced Prompting & RAG 前往該主題教學 →

📖
本主題重點：進階 prompt 三大武器：CoT（讓模型推理）、Self-Consistency（多次取樣投票）、RAG（外部知識接地）。在生醫研究中，RAG 是把 LLM 從「會編造 PubMed ID」變成「會引用真實文獻」的關鍵。請按順序讀 ⭐ Wei 2022 (CoT) → ⭐ Lewis 2020 (RAG) → ⭐ Gao 2024 (RAG Survey) → Asai 2024 (Self-RAG)，再讀 Es 2024 (RAGAS) 學會如何評測你的 RAG 系統。
What matters here: Three advanced prompt weapons — CoT (force reasoning), Self-Consistency (vote across samples), and RAG (ground in external knowledge). In biomedical research RAG is the key step that turns an LLM from "fabricates PubMed IDs" to "cites real literature". Read in order: ⭐ Wei 2022 (CoT) → ⭐ Lewis 2020 (RAG) → ⭐ Gao 2024 (RAG Survey) → Asai 2024 (Self-RAG), then Es 2024 (RAGAS) to learn how to evaluate your RAG system.

📌 教學註記與細節

本節彙整本教程在撰寫過程中發現的觀念盲區、文獻爭議與必須提醒讀者的脈絡。請以此處最新理解為準。

This section consolidates conceptual blind spots, literature controversies, and context that readers must keep in mind. Treat the notes here as the current understanding, overriding earlier chapter framing where they conflict.

① LLM 幻覺分類學 (Ji 2023, ACM Computing Surveys)

教程中 Topic 05「幻覺與事實核查」原以「事實 vs 非事實」二分敘述，建議補上 Ji et al. 2023 在 ACM Computing Surveys (DOI: 10.1145/3571730) 提出的分層分類：(i) Intrinsic hallucination（輸出與來源材料相矛盾）vs (ii) Extrinsic hallucination（輸出無法被來源驗證）。生醫情境特別容易踩到 extrinsic 類型——模型編造看似合理但不存在的 PubMed ID、DOI、基因符號。請對 RAG 系統的「來源檢索」與「最終生成」分別評估幻覺率，不要僅看總體準確度。

Topic 05 used a binary "factual vs not" framing for hallucination. Augment it with the layered taxonomy from Ji et al. 2023, ACM Computing Surveys (DOI: 10.1145/3571730): (i) intrinsic hallucinations contradict the source, (ii) extrinsic hallucinations cannot be verified against the source. Biomedical workflows are especially exposed to extrinsic cases — fabricated but plausible PubMed IDs, DOIs, gene symbols. Evaluate retrieval and generation hallucination rates separately in any RAG system; do not collapse them into a single accuracy number.

② 訓練截止日期 vs 即時資料

所有基礎模型都有 training cutoff（如 GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4 截止日各自不同）。教程多處示範「問模型最新藥物 / 臨床試驗 / preprint」未強調這個限制：cutoff 之後的文獻、撤稿通知 (retraction) 或 FDA 警告，模型無法知道。請務必：(1) 在 Prompt 內明示「請告訴我你的知識截止日」並讓模型先回報； (2) 涉及監管或臨床決策時改用具有 web-search / RAG 接地能力的工具（Perplexity, Claude with web search, ChatGPT search）； (3) 對撤稿訊息獨立查詢 Retraction Watch 資料庫。Vectara HHEM-2.1 與 LLM-AggreFact (2024) 等 leaderboard 顯示「即時搜尋接地」可將幻覺率從個位數百分比降至 1 % 以下，但仍非零。

Every foundation model has a training cutoff (GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet 4, Gemini 2.5 each differ). Several chapters demo "ask the model about the latest drug / trial / preprint" without flagging this. Anything after the cutoff — new literature, retraction notices, FDA warnings — is invisible to the model. Mandatory practices: (1) ask the model to state its knowledge cutoff at the start of the prompt; (2) for regulatory or clinical work, switch to a tool with web-search or RAG grounding (Perplexity, Claude with web search, ChatGPT search); (3) query Retraction Watch independently for retraction status. Vectara HHEM-2.1 and LLM-AggreFact (2024) leaderboards show grounding pushes hallucination from low single digits to below 1 %, but never to zero.

③ Prompt Injection 與 OWASP LLM Top 10

Topic 10「Agents 與 MCP」原本將安全議題輕描淡寫。請補上 Greshake et al. 2023「Not what you've signed up for: Compromising Real-World LLM-Integrated Applications with Indirect Prompt Injection」(arXiv:2302.12173) 提出的 indirect prompt injection：攻擊者把惡意指令藏在網頁、PDF、Email 內，當 agent 讀取這些外部資料時被劫持。OWASP LLM Top 10 for LLM Applications (v2025) 將 LLM01 Prompt Injection 列為首要風險，其次為 LLM02 Sensitive Information Disclosure、LLM05 Improper Output Handling。在 MCP / 工具呼叫情境下，務必：(a) 對 model 看得到的所有外部輸入做來源標註並降權； (b) 對寫入操作（檔案、Email、shell）採人工確認； (c) 不要讓 agent 用同一 context 處理「公開網頁」與「敏感病患資料」。

Topic 10 (Agents & MCP) understated security. Add Greshake et al. 2023, "Not what you've signed up for: Compromising Real-World LLM-Integrated Applications with Indirect Prompt Injection" (arXiv:2302.12173), which introduces indirect prompt injection: attackers hide instructions in web pages, PDFs, or emails so an agent ingesting those documents gets hijacked. The OWASP LLM Top 10 for LLM Applications (v2025) lists LLM01 Prompt Injection as the top risk, followed by LLM02 Sensitive Information Disclosure and LLM05 Improper Output Handling. For MCP / tool-calling workflows: (a) label and down-weight every external input the model can see; (b) require human confirmation for write actions (file, email, shell); (c) never let an agent share a single context across "public web" and "sensitive patient data".

④ RLHF Reward Hacking 與 Sycophancy

Topic 01「AI 與 LLM 基礎」描述 RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) 時偏向正面敘述。實際上 Casper et al. 2023「Open Problems and Fundamental Limitations of RLHF」(arXiv:2307.15217) 系統整理了 32 項 RLHF 限制，包括：人類標註者偏誤、reward model 過擬合、policy 對 reward 的 reward hacking。Sharma et al. 2024「Towards Understanding Sycophancy in Language Models」(arXiv:2310.13548, ICLR 2024) 進一步證實 RLHF 訓練會增強 sycophancy：模型傾向附和使用者既有觀點而非陳述正確答案。對研究者意涵：(i) 不要把模型「同意你的假設」當作獨立驗證； (ii) 在 prompt 中刻意引入反方論點（red-team prompt）以測試穩健性； (iii) 對統計判讀、診斷推理任務，使用 confidence-aware prompting 並要求模型列出不確定來源。

Topic 01 framed RLHF positively. In reality, Casper et al. 2023, "Open Problems and Fundamental Limitations of RLHF" (arXiv:2307.15217), catalogues 32 RLHF limitations — annotator bias, reward-model overfitting, and reward hacking by the policy. Sharma et al. 2024, "Towards Understanding Sycophancy in Language Models" (arXiv:2310.13548, ICLR 2024), confirms RLHF amplifies sycophancy: models echo the user's stated view rather than the correct answer. Implications: (i) do not treat "the model agreed with my hypothesis" as independent validation; (ii) deliberately inject counter-arguments (red-team prompts) to probe robustness; (iii) for statistical or diagnostic reasoning, use confidence-aware prompting and require the model to list uncertainty sources.

⑤ AI Bill of Rights 是非約束性藍圖 vs EU AI Act / FDA

Topic 20「隱私、法規與可重現性」勿將 White House Blueprint for an AI Bill of Rights (OSTP, 2022) 與具強制力的法規混為一談——它是 non-binding policy framework，無法執行罰則。真正具拘束力的：(a) EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, 生效 2024-08-01, 多數條款 2026-08 適用)：採風險分級，醫療設備內建 AI 多屬 High-Risk，需 CE 標誌、技術文件、上市後監測； (b) FDA Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) 框架 + 2024 年 Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP) 指引允許在預先核可範圍內持續更新模型； (c) HIPAA (US) 與 GDPR (EU) 對 PHI / personal data 的處理限制。GenAI 模型若涉及臨床決策支持 (CDS)，多數情境會被 FDA 視為醫療設備並需 510(k) 或 De Novo 路徑。NIST AI RMF 1.0 (2023) 與 Generative AI Profile (NIST AI 600-1, 2024) 是建議性風險管理框架，但常被合約引用為實作基準。

Topic 20 must not conflate the White House Blueprint for an AI Bill of Rights (OSTP, 2022) with binding regulation — it is a non-binding policy framework with no enforcement teeth. Binding instruments: (a) the EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, in force 2024-08-01, most provisions applicable 2026-08), with risk tiers — AI embedded in medical devices is generally High-Risk and requires CE marking, technical file, and post-market monitoring; (b) the FDA Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) framework plus the 2024 Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP) guidance allowing continuous model updates within a pre-approved envelope; (c) HIPAA (US) and GDPR (EU) constraints on PHI / personal data. A GenAI model that performs clinical decision support (CDS) usually falls under FDA medical-device oversight and must clear a 510(k) or De Novo pathway. NIST AI RMF 1.0 (2023) and the Generative AI Profile (NIST AI 600-1, 2024) are voluntary risk frameworks but are increasingly cited contractually as the implementation baseline.

⑥ WHO LMM Guidance 2024

原教程未引用 WHO 的最新立場。2024 年 1 月 WHO 發布 Ethics and governance of artificial intelligence for health: Guidance on large multi-modal models (ISBN 978-92-4-008475-9)，是國際組織首份針對 GenAI / LMM 在健康場景的官方指引。重點：(1) 列出 LMM 在五大健康應用情境（診斷、患者導向、行政、教育、研究）並對每項提出風險清單； (2) 強調對 LMICs (low- and middle-income countries) 的可近性與數位殖民風險； (3) 建議由非開發者進行獨立評估與審計； (4) 提醒對 LMM 使用 informed consent 框架需重新設計，現行同意書多無法涵蓋資料被用於再訓練的情境。台灣與多數華語地區雖非 WHO 會員，但 IRB 與期刊審查越來越多採此指引為參考。

The tutorial did not cite WHO's latest position. In January 2024 the WHO released Ethics and governance of artificial intelligence for health: Guidance on large multi-modal models (ISBN 978-92-4-008475-9), the first international-body guidance specifically for GenAI / LMM in health. Highlights: (1) catalogs five health-use scenarios (diagnosis, patient-facing, administrative, education, research) with risk lists per scenario; (2) emphasises access and digital-colonialism risks for LMICs; (3) recommends evaluation and audit by parties other than the developer; (4) flags that informed-consent frameworks need redesign — most current forms do not cover reuse of data for retraining. Even where WHO membership does not directly apply, IRBs and journals increasingly cite this guidance.

⑦ TRIPOD+AI 2024 報告標準

Topic 09 / 13 / 20 涉及預測模型報告時，應將 Collins et al. 2024「TRIPOD+AI statement: updated guidance for reporting clinical prediction models that use regression or machine learning methods」(BMJ 2024;385:e078378, DOI: 10.1136/bmj-2023-078378) 列為主要寫作清單。TRIPOD+AI 在原 TRIPOD 2015 基礎上新增針對機器學習 / AI 模型的條目：模型不公平性 (fairness) 評估、資料漂移 (data drift) 監測、外部驗證（不能僅靠內部 cross-validation）、開源程式碼與訓練資料的 FAIR 揭露。配套：影像 AI 用 CLAIM 2024 update (Mongan et al., Radiology: AI 2024)、診斷準確度研究用 STARD-AI (Salim et al. 2025, Nat Med)、隨機試驗用 CONSORT-AI (Liu et al. 2020) 與 SPIRIT-AI。投稿前對照清單填寫，可避免 reviewer 退稿主因「報告不透明」。

For any chapter touching prediction-model reporting (Topics 09, 13, 20), use Collins et al. 2024, "TRIPOD+AI statement: updated guidance for reporting clinical prediction models that use regression or machine learning methods" (BMJ 2024;385:e078378, DOI: 10.1136/bmj-2023-078378) as the master checklist. TRIPOD+AI extends TRIPOD 2015 with ML/AI-specific items: fairness evaluation, data-drift monitoring, mandatory external validation (internal cross-validation is not enough), and FAIR-style disclosure of code and training data. Pair with CLAIM 2024 update (Mongan et al., Radiology: AI 2024) for imaging, STARD-AI (Salim et al. 2025, Nat Med) for diagnostic accuracy, and CONSORT-AI / SPIRIT-AI (Liu et al. 2020) for trials. Completing these checklists before submission averts the most common reviewer rejection reason — "insufficient reporting transparency".

⑪ ICMJE 2025 揭露範本標準化

ICMJE 在 2025 修訂版（icmje.org/recommendations）統一了「AI / LLM 使用揭露」的格式——必須在 Methods 列出：(1) 工具名與版本（如 "ChatGPT-4o, June 2024 version"）、(2) 使用目的（"language polishing", "literature search", "code generation"）、(3) 處理的內容範圍。Nature / JAMA / Lancet / Cell Press 均已採此格式。2022 年舊範本（僅 acknowledgment 段落單句揭露）不再被接受——投稿時請確認你的揭露段落符合 ICMJE 2025 三點要求。

The ICMJE 2025 revision (icmje.org/recommendations) standardized the AI / LLM disclosure format: in Methods, authors must state (1) tool name and version (e.g. "ChatGPT-4o, June 2024 version"), (2) purpose ("language polishing", "literature search", "code generation"), and (3) scope of content processed. Nature, JAMA, Lancet, and Cell Press all adopt this format. The 2022 template (single-sentence disclosure in Acknowledgments only) is no longer accepted — at submission, verify that your disclosure paragraph satisfies all three ICMJE 2025 points.

⑫ Llama 4 雖開源權重但有 700M MAU 條款

引用 Meta Llama 系列為「開源 LLM」時須謹慎——Llama 2/3/4 釋出的 weights 雖可下載，授權條款（Llama Community License）規定：當你的產品月活躍用戶數 (MAU) 超過 7 億時，需另外申請商業授權。這嚴格上不符合 OSI 對 open source 的定義，Nature 2024 多篇社論已點名稱為 "open weights" 而非 "open source"。教學中若強調「真正開源」應改用 Mistral 7B (Apache 2.0)、Qwen 系列（部分 Apache 2.0、部分 Tongyi Qianwen License）、DeepSeek-V3 (MIT)、OLMo (Apache 2.0 + 完全公開訓練資料)。

Be careful when calling Meta Llama "open source" — Llama 2/3/4 weights are downloadable but the Llama Community License requires a separate commercial license once your product exceeds 700 million monthly active users (MAU). Strictly this does not meet the OSI definition of open source, and several Nature 2024 editorials now insist on calling these "open weights", not "open source". For genuinely open licensing prefer Mistral 7B (Apache 2.0), Qwen (mixed Apache 2.0 / Tongyi Qianwen License), DeepSeek-V3 (MIT), or OLMo (Apache 2.0 with fully public training data).

⑬ scFM zero-shot 性能爭議仍未定論

Topic 13 將 scGPT / Geneformer 標為「⭐ 必讀」，但 Kedzierska et al. 2024 (bioRxiv 2023.10.16.561085) 與 Boiarsky et al. 2024 兩個獨立 benchmark 顯示：在 cell type annotation 與 batch correction 任務上，這些 FM 的 zero-shot 表現有時不如 PCA + Harmony 等傳統管線。原因可能是：(1) 訓練資料的批次效應未充分校正；(2) 模型容量過大造成 overfit-to-pretraining-distribution；(3) gene token vocabulary 偏向人類常見組織。實務建議：(a) 對 scFM 使用 fine-tuning 而非 zero-shot；(b) 同時跑 PCA + Harmony / scVI 做為對照；(c) 引用時並列 Cui 2024 / Theodoris 2023 與 Kedzierska 2024，呈現現狀爭議。

Topic 13 marks scGPT / Geneformer as ⭐ must-read, but two independent benchmarks — Kedzierska et al. 2024 (bioRxiv 2023.10.16.561085) and Boiarsky et al. 2024 — show that on cell-type annotation and batch correction these FMs in zero-shot mode can underperform classical pipelines such as PCA + Harmony. Plausible causes: (1) residual batch effects in pretraining data; (2) model capacity overfitting to the pretraining distribution; (3) gene-token vocabulary skewed toward common human tissues. Practice: (a) prefer fine-tuning over zero-shot for scFMs; (b) always benchmark against PCA + Harmony / scVI; (c) cite Cui 2024 / Theodoris 2023 alongside Kedzierska 2024 to reflect the unresolved state.
