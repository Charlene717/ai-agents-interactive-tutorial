Step 6 · RAG 檢索 recall 天花板

教學介紹 RAG 流程通常聚焦於 chunk size、embedding model、reranker 選擇。需補充一個常被忽略的根本限制：RAG 系統最終答案品質的上限，由 retrieval recall@k 直接框定——若正確段落根本沒被檢索進來，無論 LLM 多強都無法生成正確答案。

Gao et al. 2024 「Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Large Language Models: A Survey」系統綜整了三大「retrieval-bound」失敗模式：(1) 查詢-文件分布偏移（query 用口語、文件用術語）；(2) chunk 切割切斷語意（答案橫跨兩個 chunk）；(3) 多跳推理（單一查詢無法一次撈齊所需證據）。建議：

• 評估 RAG 時同時測 retrieval metrics (recall@k, MRR) 與 generation metrics，不要只看最終 EM/F1。
• 對複雜查詢採 query decomposition / HyDE / multi-hop retrieval。
• 監控「答錯題」是檢索錯還是生成錯——這兩種錯需用不同藥方。

RAG tutorials typically focus on chunk size, embedding model, and reranker choice. An often-overlooked structural limit: the ceiling on RAG answer quality is set by retrieval recall@k — if the correct passage is never retrieved, no LLM can fix it downstream.

Gao et al. 2024 "Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Large Language Models: A Survey" catalogs three canonical "retrieval-bound" failure modes: (1) query-document distribution shift (colloquial query vs. technical corpus); (2) chunking severs semantics (answer spans two chunks); (3) multi-hop reasoning (single query can't fetch all needed evidence). Practical advice:

• Evaluate retrieval metrics (recall@k, MRR) alongside generation metrics; don't rely on end-to-end EM/F1 alone.
• For complex queries, use query decomposition / HyDE / multi-hop retrieval.
• Attribute failures: is the wrong answer a retrieval miss or a generation error? Each needs a different fix.

來源：Gao et al. 2024 RAG Survey。

Source: Gao et al. 2024 RAG Survey.

Reasoning patterns · CoT / ReAct / Reflexion 區別

教學介紹 agent 推理時常把 CoT、ReAct、Reflexion 混在一起。這三者是不同層次的策略，應分清：

• CoT (Chain-of-Thought, Wei et al. 2022 NeurIPS)：純推理模式。要求 LLM「step-by-step」展開推理鏈再給最終答案；沒有外部工具、沒有環境互動。適用 GSM8K 等純文字推理題。
• ReAct (Yao et al. 2022 ICLR / arXiv)：推理 + 行動交錯。在 CoT 的「Thought」之間插入「Action」（呼叫 tool）與「Observation」（工具回傳）。這是大多數 agent 框架（LangGraph、AutoGen）的執行模板。
• Reflexion (Shinn et al. 2023 NeurIPS)：失敗後的自我修正。在 ReAct 之後加一層「verbal self-reflection」——任務失敗時，由 LLM 用自然語言寫下「下次應該如何改」存到 episodic memory，下次 trajectory 載入此反思。不是替代 ReAct，而是疊加。

三者關係：CoT ⊂ ReAct（思考＋行動）⊂ Reflexion（思考＋行動＋學習）。設計 agent 時應明確選擇所需層次，而非全套套用。

Tutorials often blur CoT, ReAct, and Reflexion together. These are strategies at different levels and should be distinguished:

• CoT (Chain-of-Thought, Wei et al. 2022 NeurIPS): pure reasoning. The model unrolls a "step-by-step" reasoning chain before the final answer; no external tools, no environment interaction. Suited to text-only reasoning tasks like GSM8K.
• ReAct (Yao et al. 2022 ICLR / arXiv): reasoning interleaved with action. Inserts Action (tool call) and Observation (tool return) between CoT-style Thoughts. This is the execution template for most modern agent frameworks (LangGraph, AutoGen).
• Reflexion (Shinn et al. 2023 NeurIPS): self-correction after failure. Layered on top of ReAct: when a task fails, the LLM writes a natural-language self-reflection ("next time, try X") to episodic memory, which is loaded on the next trajectory. It augments ReAct rather than replacing it.

Relationship: CoT ⊂ ReAct (reason + act) ⊂ Reflexion (reason + act + learn). Design agents by deliberately choosing the level you need, not by stacking every pattern.

來源：Wei et al. 2022 CoT；Yao et al. 2022 ReAct；Shinn et al. 2023 Reflexion。

Sources: Wei et al. 2022 CoT; Yao et al. 2022 ReAct; Shinn et al. 2023 Reflexion.
