基因組 Foundation 模型

Nucleotide Transformer、DNABERT-2、Caduceus——DNA 也有自己的 GPT。

Nucleotide Transformer, DNABERT-2, Caduceus — DNA has its own GPT.

術語速查

基因組 FM 必懂十二詞

這 12 個詞貫穿這一章。先讀過一次，後面 SSM、tokenizer、variant effect prediction 的討論就會輕鬆許多。

These twelve words recur throughout. Skim once and the SSM / tokenizer / variant effect discussion below flows easily.

🧬

Nucleotide Transformer

InstaDeep 2023–2024（Dalla-Torre et al., Nat Methods 2024, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z）。50M–2.5B 參數，多物種（850 種）DNA pretrain，6-mer tokenizer。是 genomic FM 的「standard」reference model。

InstaDeep 2023–2024 (Dalla-Torre et al., Nat Methods 2024, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z). 50M–2.5B parameters, multi-species (850 species) DNA pretraining, 6-mer tokenizer. The standard reference genomic FM.

🔤

DNABERT-2

Zhou et al. 2023 (arXiv 2306.15006)。117M 參數、用 BPE tokenizer（比 6-mer 更靈活）、比 NT 小 21× 仍可媲美效能。Apache 2.0 授權、研究界廣泛採用。

Zhou et al. 2023 (arXiv 2306.15006). 117M parameters, BPE tokenizer (more flexible than 6-mer), 21× smaller than NT yet competitive. Apache 2.0, widely adopted in research.

🐍

Caduceus

Schiff et al. 2024 (ICML)。bi-directional Mamba/state-space 架構，~131 kb 到 1 Mb context，是 enhancer–promoter 長距離互動的最佳工具。RC-equivariant 設計尊重 DNA 雙鏈對稱性。

Schiff et al. 2024 (ICML). Bi-directional Mamba/state-space architecture, 131 kb to 1 Mb context — best for long-range enhancer–promoter interactions. RC-equivariant design respects DNA strand symmetry.

🌀

Mamba / SSM

Selective State Space Model（Gu & Dao 2023, ICLR 2024）。突破 Transformer O(n²) 限制，做 selective scan 使序列長度可達 1M+ 而 memory 線性。Caduceus、HyenaDNA 都基於 SSM。生物界對 100k–1M scale 序列極實用。

Selective State Space Model (Gu & Dao 2023, ICLR 2024). Breaks Transformer's O(n²) wall via selective scan — sequence lengths reach 1M+ with linear memory. Caduceus and HyenaDNA build on SSM. Crucial for 100k–1M scale biology sequences.

⚖️

Variant Effect Prediction (VEP)

給一個 ref → alt 變異，預測它對 function、splicing、expression、disease 的影響。Genomic FM 用 likelihood ratio（log P(alt) − log P(ref)）做 zero-shot scoring，與 SpliceAI（Jaganathan 2019）、AlphaMissense（Cheng 2023）並列。

Given a ref → alt variant, predict its impact on function, splicing, expression, disease. Genomic FMs score zero-shot via likelihood ratio (log P(alt) − log P(ref)), alongside SpliceAI (Jaganathan 2019) and AlphaMissense (Cheng 2023).

✂️

Splicing

從 pre-mRNA 切除 intron 留下 exon。涉及 5′ splice site (GT)、3′ splice site (AG)、branch point、splicing factor。SpliceAI（Jaganathan 2019 Cell）是 splicing prediction 的 gold standard；genomic FM 可作 zero-shot 替代。

Removing introns from pre-mRNA, retaining exons. Involves 5′ splice site (GT), 3′ splice site (AG), branch point, splicing factors. SpliceAI (Jaganathan 2019 Cell) is the gold standard; genomic FMs serve as zero-shot alternatives.

🎛️

Regulatory Element

promoter、enhancer、silencer、insulator——控制基因 expression 的 cis-elements。ENCODE 2012 與 Roadmap Epigenomics 2015 建立 catalog；genomic FM 學會這些 element 的 sequence pattern。

Promoters, enhancers, silencers, insulators — cis-elements controlling gene expression. ENCODE 2012 and Roadmap Epigenomics 2015 built the catalogues; genomic FMs learn their sequence patterns.

🪙

Tokenizer (Genomic)

把 DNA 切成 token：(a) k-mer（6-mer，固定切）、(b) BPE（Byte-Pair Encoding，學頻繁 sub-sequence）、(c) single-nucleotide（A/C/G/T）。各有 trade-off：k-mer 易解釋、BPE 高壓縮、single-nt 最 flexible 但序列最長。

Splitting DNA into tokens: (a) k-mer (fixed 6-mer); (b) BPE (Byte-Pair Encoding, learns frequent sub-sequences); (c) single-nucleotide (A/C/G/T). Trade-offs: k-mer interpretable; BPE high compression; single-nt most flexible but longest sequences.

↩️

Reverse Complement (RC)

DNA 是雙鏈，5′-ATCG-3′ 的對股是 3′-TAGC-5′（讀作 5′-CGAT-3′）。生物學 invariant：兩鏈承載相同資訊。RC-aware 模型（Caduceus、SpliceAI）對 forward 與 reverse 預測一致；不 aware 模型可能對同一變異給不同分數。

DNA is double-stranded — 5′-ATCG-3′ pairs with 3′-TAGC-5′ (read as 5′-CGAT-3′). A biological invariant: both strands carry the same info. RC-aware models (Caduceus, SpliceAI) give consistent forward / reverse predictions; non-aware models can score the same variant differently.

📏

Conservation Score

跨物種演化保守度：phyloP（Pollard 2010）、phastCons（Siepel 2005）、GERP（Cooper 2005）。高保守度位點變異通常更有害。Genomic FM 學到的 implicit conservation 與顯式 score 高度相關但更精細。

Cross-species evolutionary conservation: phyloP (Pollard 2010), phastCons (Siepel 2005), GERP (Cooper 2005). Highly conserved positions tend to be more deleterious when mutated. Genomic FMs learn implicit conservation correlated with explicit scores but with finer resolution.

🔓

Zero-shot vs Fine-tune

Zero-shot：直接用 pretrained model 做 task（如 likelihood ratio for VEP），無需 task label。Fine-tune：加 task head + supervised data 微調。Zero-shot 適合 label 稀缺；fine-tune 在足夠 label 下 ~5–15% accuracy gain。報告時必須明確區分。

Zero-shot: use the pretrained model directly (likelihood ratio for VEP), no task labels needed. Fine-tune: add a task head + supervised data. Zero-shot suits scarce labels; fine-tune adds ~5–15% accuracy with enough labels. Always disclose which you report.

🌍

Reference Bias

Genomic FM 多在 GRCh38（70% European）上 pretrain。對非洲、東亞、Latino population variants 預測 calibration 可能差。Martin 2019 Nat Genet 在 PRS 上首次量化此 bias；genomic FM 同樣需要 cross-ancestry validation。

Genomic FMs are pretrained mostly on GRCh38 (70% European). Predictions on African, East Asian, and Latino populations may be poorly calibrated. Martin 2019 (Nat Genet) first quantified this on PRS; genomic FMs likewise need cross-ancestry validation.

概覽

DNA 不是文字，為什麼還要 LLM？

DNA 序列雖然只是 4 個字母（A/C/G/T），但「上下文」決定一切：同一個 ATG 在 start codon vs intron vs intergenic 意義不同。Transformer 的 self-attention 正好擅長捕捉這種長距離依賴關係。

2023–2025 三大代表：Nucleotide Transformer（InstaDeep，Nature Methods 2024, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z，50M–2.5B 參數，多物種）；DNABERT-2（BPE tokenizer、比 NT 小 21× 仍可媲美效能，arXiv 2306.15006）；Caduceus（bi-directional Mamba/state-space，專攻 long-range 調控）。應用包括 variant effect prediction、TF binding、調控元件分類、splicing。

DNA has only four letters (A/C/G/T), but context decides everything: the same ATG means different things in a start codon, an intron, or intergenic space. Transformer self-attention captures exactly this kind of long-range dependency.

Three flagship models (2023–2025): Nucleotide Transformer (InstaDeep, Nature Methods 2024, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z; 50M–2.5B parameters, multi-species); DNABERT-2 (BPE tokenizer, 21× smaller than NT with comparable performance, arXiv 2306.15006); Caduceus (bi-directional Mamba/state-space, long-range regulatory focus). Use cases: variant effect prediction, TF binding, regulatory element classification, splicing.

💡
核心心法：基因組 FM 給的是「序列 → embedding 或 logit」，不是直接的生物學答案。你必須對應到任務 head（classification / regression / zero-shot scoring），並用留出物種 / 留出染色體的 split 確認效能，避免訓練資料 leakage。 Core mindset: Genomic FMs map sequence to embedding or logit — not directly to biology. You must attach a task head (classification / regression / zero-shot scoring) and validate with held-out species / held-out chromosome splits to avoid training-data leakage.

三、三段 code

# Hugging Face：載入 Nucleotide Transformer 500M multi-species
from transformers import AutoTokenizer, AutoModelForMaskedLM
import torch

mid = "InstaDeepAI/nucleotide-transformer-500m-multi-species"
tok = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained(mid, trust_remote_code=True)
mdl = AutoModelForMaskedLM.from_pretrained(mid, trust_remote_code=True).cuda().eval()

seq = "ATGCGTACGTAGCTAGCTAGCTAACGTAGCTAGCTACGTAGCTAGCTAGCTAGCTA"
inp = tok(seq, return_tensors="pt").to("cuda")
with torch.no_grad():
    emb = mdl(**inp, output_hidden_states=True).hidden_states[-1].mean(1)
print(emb.shape)   # (1, 1280) — 拿來當下游分類 feature
# DNABERT-2 fine-tune for promoter classification
from transformers import AutoTokenizer, AutoModelForSequenceClassification, Trainer, TrainingArguments
from datasets import load_dataset

mid = "zhihan1996/DNABERT-2-117M"
tok = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained(mid, trust_remote_code=True)
mdl = AutoModelForSequenceClassification.from_pretrained(mid, num_labels=2, trust_remote_code=True)

ds = load_dataset("InstaDeepAI/genomic-benchmarks-human_nontata_promoters")
def tk(b): return tok(b["sequence"], truncation=True, max_length=512)
ds = ds.map(tk, batched=True)

args = TrainingArguments("out", learning_rate=3e-5, num_train_epochs=3,
    per_device_train_batch_size=16, eval_strategy="epoch")
Trainer(mdl, args, train_dataset=ds["train"], eval_dataset=ds["test"]).train()
# Zero-shot variant effect by likelihood ratio
import torch, torch.nn.functional as F

def log_lik(seq):
    inp = tok(seq, return_tensors="pt").to("cuda")
    with torch.no_grad():
        logits = mdl(**inp).logits
    lp = F.log_softmax(logits, dim=-1)
    return lp.gather(2, inp["input_ids"].unsqueeze(-1)).sum().item()

ref = "...ATGCGT[C]GCTAG..."
alt = "...ATGCGT[T]GCTAG..."
score = log_lik(alt) - log_lik(ref)   # 負值 = 可能有害
# negative = likely deleterious
print(f"LLR = {score:.3f}")

真實案例

兩個深入案例：NT 在 ClinVar 上超越 CADD、Caduceus 100kb enhancer 互動

🔬

案例 1：Nucleotide Transformer 2.5B 在 ClinVar VEP 任務超越 CADD

Dalla-Torre et al. 2024 (Nature Methods, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z) 報告 Nucleotide Transformer (NT) 2.5B 在 18 個 genomic prediction task 上 17 個 SOTA。其中最受 clinical genetics 關注的是 ClinVar pathogenic / benign 分類：NT 2.5B 在 zero-shot likelihood ratio 上 AUROC 0.876，而 CADD（Rentzsch 2019）AUROC 0.825、REVEL（Ioannidis 2016）AUROC 0.853。差距在 noncoding region 更顯著（NT 0.82 vs CADD 0.71）——因為 CADD 主要用 conservation + functional annotation，noncoding 訓練 signal 稀疏；NT 則直接從 sequence likelihood 學到 cis-regulatory context。實務影響：(1) 臨床 lab 開始把 NT score 當作 VUS reclassification 的 supplementary evidence（ACMG 2015 PP3/BP4 適用）；(2) 對 noncoding variant（GWAS lead SNP、5′ UTR、3′ UTR）的 fine-mapping 更有用。caveat：NT 是 zero-shot score 不等同 functional validation；ACMG criteria 仍需多重 evidence（MaxEnt splicing、SpliceAI、AlphaMissense、實驗 minigene assay）整合。

Case 1: Nucleotide Transformer 2.5B beats CADD on ClinVar VEP

Dalla-Torre et al. 2024 (Nat Methods, DOI 10.1038/s41592-024-02523-z) reported Nucleotide Transformer 2.5B reaches SOTA on 17 of 18 genomic prediction tasks. The clinical-genetics highlight: ClinVar pathogenic/benign classification — NT 2.5B zero-shot likelihood ratio AUROC 0.876 vs CADD (Rentzsch 2019) 0.825 vs REVEL (Ioannidis 2016) 0.853. The gap is even wider for noncoding (NT 0.82 vs CADD 0.71) because CADD relies on conservation + functional annotation with sparse noncoding signal, whereas NT learns cis-regulatory context directly from sequence likelihood. Practical impact: (1) clinical labs use NT scores as supplementary evidence for VUS reclassification (ACMG 2015 PP3/BP4); (2) better fine-mapping for noncoding variants (GWAS lead SNPs, 5′ UTRs, 3′ UTRs). Caveat: NT zero-shot scores aren't functional validation; ACMG criteria still require integrated evidence (MaxEnt splicing, SpliceAI, AlphaMissense, experimental minigene assays).

🐍

案例 2：Caduceus 用 SSM 處理 100kb 長距離 enhancer-promoter 互動

Schiff et al. 2024 (ICML) 推出的 Caduceus 用 bi-directional Mamba SSM 架構，context 達 131 kb（後續 scale 到 1 Mb）。傳統 Transformer 在這個尺度 memory cost O(n²) 不可承受；SSM 的 selective scan 把 cost 降到 O(n log n) 並 linear memory，使「整個 enhancer-promoter loop scale」第一次成為可能。在 GenomeBenchmarks 的 enhancer–target 預測 task，Caduceus-131k 達 AUROC 0.91，比同等參數量的 Transformer-baseline 高 7 個百分點。另一關鍵設計：RC-equivariant——對 forward strand 與 reverse complement 給一致預測。實際應用例：某研究室用 Caduceus 找 GWAS lead SNP 與 ~50 kb 外 target gene 的潛在 enhancer-promoter 連接，與 Hi-C / PCHi-C / ABC model（Fulco 2019 Nat Genet）預測高度相符；對 IBD risk locus 上一個未註釋 noncoding SNP 預測它應該影響 ~80 kb 外的 NOD2，後來 CRISPRi 驗證確實。關鍵 takeaway：對「短距離」（< 5 kb）NT 或 DNABERT-2 都夠；但 enhancer-promoter 互動是 cell-type 與 genome organisation 級的問題，需要 Caduceus 或 Enformer（Avsec 2021 Nat Methods）這種長 context 模型。

Case 2: Caduceus uses SSM to model 100kb long-range enhancer–promoter interactions

Schiff et al. 2024 (ICML) introduced Caduceus, a bi-directional Mamba SSM model with 131 kb context (later scaled to 1 Mb). Traditional Transformers can't afford O(n²) memory at this scale; SSM's selective scan brings cost to O(n log n) with linear memory, enabling whole enhancer-promoter loops for the first time. On GenomeBenchmarks enhancer–target prediction, Caduceus-131k reaches AUROC 0.91, 7 percentage points above same-parameter Transformer baselines. Another key: RC-equivariance — forward strand and reverse complement give consistent predictions. Example application: a lab used Caduceus to predict enhancer–promoter links between a GWAS lead SNP and a target ~50 kb away, agreeing strongly with Hi-C / PCHi-C / ABC model (Fulco 2019 Nat Genet); for an unannotated IBD-risk noncoding SNP, Caduceus predicted effect on NOD2 ~80 kb downstream — later confirmed by CRISPRi. Takeaway: for < 5 kb tasks, NT or DNABERT-2 suffice; for enhancer-promoter interactions (a cell-type and genome-organisation problem), use Caduceus or Enformer (Avsec 2021 Nat Methods) class long-context models.

常見誤區

使用 genomic FM 最常踩的五個坑

⚠️
誤區 1：把 GRCh38 訓練的模型直接套到非洲人群體
為什麼錯：NT、DNABERT-2 等大多在 GRCh38 上 pretrain（人類部分），而 GRCh38 sample composition 約 70% European、~10% Asian、~10% African。Martin 2019 (Nat Genet) 在 PRS 上首次量化 cross-ancestry transfer drop（70% performance loss in non-European）；genomic FM 同樣面臨此問題。對 sickle cell（HbS）、G6PD deficiency 等非洲常見變異，calibration 可能特別差。
正確做法：(a) 對非歐裔病人 cohort 必跑 cross-ancestry validation（用 gnomAD v4 by-ancestry allele frequency 作 reference）；(b) 必要時 fine-tune 在 H3Africa、All of Us、TOPMed 等 diverse cohort；(c) 報告 paper 時揭露 training data ancestry composition；(d) 不對 minoritised population 做 high-stakes clinical 決策直到 calibration validate。
Pitfall 1: Applying a GRCh38-trained model to African populations
Why wrong: NT, DNABERT-2 etc. are mostly pretrained on GRCh38 (~70% European, ~10% Asian, ~10% African). Martin 2019 (Nat Genet) first quantified cross-ancestry drop on PRS (70% performance loss in non-European); genomic FMs face the same problem. Calibration on sickle cell (HbS), G6PD deficiency and other Africa-common variants may be especially poor.
Fix: (a) run cross-ancestry validation for non-European cohorts (use gnomAD v4 by-ancestry allele frequency as reference); (b) fine-tune on H3Africa, All of Us, TOPMed when needed; (c) disclose training-data ancestry composition in papers; (d) avoid high-stakes clinical decisions on minoritised populations until calibration is validated.
⚠️
誤區 2：忽略反向互補對稱性
為什麼錯：DNA 是雙鏈，5′-ATCG-3′ 與 3′-TAGC-5′（即 5′-CGAT-3′）承載相同資訊。不 RC-aware 模型對同一 SNP 在 forward 與 reverse 上可能給不同 score——這在生物學上無意義。NT v1 與 DNABERT-2 都 partial RC-aware；Caduceus 是 explicit RC-equivariant。
正確做法：(a) 對 zero-shot VEP 跑 forward + reverse 各算一次，取 average；(b) fine-tune 時資料增強 50% 包含 reverse complement；(c) 對 critical clinical decision 使用 RC-equivariant 模型（Caduceus）；(d) 報告時驗證「forward / reverse score 一致」。
Pitfall 2: Ignoring reverse-complement symmetry
Why wrong: DNA is double-stranded; 5′-ATCG-3′ and 3′-TAGC-5′ (= 5′-CGAT-3′) carry the same information. Non RC-aware models can give different scores for the same SNP on forward vs reverse — biologically meaningless. NT v1 and DNABERT-2 are partially RC-aware; Caduceus is explicitly RC-equivariant.
Fix: (a) for zero-shot VEP, run forward and reverse and average; (b) during fine-tuning, augment 50% of data with reverse complements; (c) for critical clinical decisions, use RC-equivariant models (Caduceus); (d) report forward/reverse score consistency.
⚠️
誤區 3：Tokenizer 切到 ATG/AUG 邊界破壞 codon
為什麼錯：NT 用 6-mer 固定 tokenizer，可能把 ATG-codon 切成「...XATG...」與「...GA TG...」兩種不同上下文——對 start codon 的影響意義消失。DNABERT-2 用 BPE 自適應切但同樣可能在 functional motif 邊界產生 inconsistent token。對 splice site、TSS、stop codon 等強 motif 影響特別大。
正確做法：(a) 對 motif-aware task（splicing、TF binding），考慮 single-nucleotide tokenizer 模型（如 HyenaDNA）；(b) 對 6-mer / BPE 模型，做 frame-aware augmentation（同一 region 多個 frame offset 取平均）；(c) 對 functional motif 邊界附近的變異特別小心，可能需 fine-tune 緩解。
Pitfall 3: Tokenizers cutting through ATG/AUG codons breaks meaning
Why wrong: NT uses a 6-mer tokenizer that can split an ATG codon into "...XATG..." or "...GA TG..." contexts — losing start-codon semantics. DNABERT-2's BPE adapts but can still produce inconsistent tokens at functional motif boundaries. Splice sites, TSSs, stop codons are especially affected.
Fix: (a) for motif-aware tasks (splicing, TF binding), consider single-nucleotide tokenizer models (HyenaDNA); (b) for 6-mer / BPE models, use frame-aware augmentation (average across frame offsets); (c) be especially careful for variants near functional-motif boundaries — fine-tuning may help.
⚠️
誤區 4：把 fine-tune 結果當 zero-shot 報告
為什麼錯：Zero-shot（直接用 pretrained model 跑 likelihood ratio，無 task label）與 fine-tune（加 head + supervised data）的科學意義完全不同。Zero-shot 證明 pretrained model 學到 generalisable knowledge；fine-tune 表示「給足 label 後能做」。論文比較時偷換概念是常見誤導。
正確做法：(a) 嚴格揭露：是 zero-shot、linear probe、partial fine-tune、還是 full fine-tune；(b) 若 paper 主張「foundation model 的價值」應強調 zero-shot performance；(c) 對 reviewer 與 reader，這兩者效能差距通常 5–15%，是 meaningful 差異不能混。
Pitfall 4: Reporting fine-tuned results as zero-shot
Why wrong: zero-shot (use the pretrained model with no task labels, e.g., likelihood ratio) and fine-tune (add head + supervised data) have entirely different scientific meanings. Zero-shot proves the pretrained model learned generalisable knowledge; fine-tune means "given enough labels, it works." Papers conflate these often.
Fix: (a) state explicitly: zero-shot, linear probe, partial fine-tune, or full fine-tune; (b) "foundation-model value" claims should emphasise zero-shot performance; (c) the typical 5–15% gap between the two is meaningful and must not be conflated.
⚠️
誤區 5：cell-line specific 信號當 universal
為什麼錯：許多 genomic FM 在 GTEx、ENCODE、Roadmap 等 cell line / tissue 資料 fine-tune 或 evaluate。但 enhancer activity、TF binding、chromatin accessibility 都高度 cell-type specific——在 K562 學到的「enhancer signal」在 brain neuron 可能完全不適用。Nair 2022 (Nat Methods) 與 Fulco 2019 (Nat Genet) 都強調 cell-type context 不可忽略。
正確做法：(a) 對 cell-type-specific task 使用 cell-type conditioning model（Enformer 用 5313 個 cell-type-specific track）；(b) 對 disease 研究，使用 disease-relevant cell type 的資料（IBD 用 intestinal epithelium、Alzheimer 用 brain neuron）；(c) 報告時明確 cell-type 範圍與 transfer evidence。
Pitfall 5: Treating cell-line-specific signals as universal
Why wrong: many genomic FMs are fine-tuned or evaluated on GTEx, ENCODE, Roadmap cell-line / tissue data. But enhancer activity, TF binding, and chromatin accessibility are highly cell-type-specific — "enhancer signal" learned in K562 may be irrelevant in brain neurons. Nair 2022 (Nat Methods) and Fulco 2019 (Nat Genet) emphasise cell-type context.
Fix: (a) use cell-type-conditioned models for cell-type-specific tasks (Enformer uses 5313 cell-type-specific tracks); (b) for disease, use disease-relevant cell types (IBD → intestinal epithelium, Alzheimer → brain neurons); (c) explicitly disclose cell-type scope and transfer evidence.
⚠️
誤區 6：Benchmark leakage 沒做 chromosome / species split
為什麼錯：genomic FM benchmark（如 GUE, BEND, Genomic Benchmarks）若 train/test 隨機 split，homologous region / paralog gene 會橫跨 train 與 test，造成 inflated performance。Tang 2024 重新分析多個 benchmark 發現 train/test homology 過濾後 performance drop 10–30%。
正確做法：(a) 至少做 chromosome-aware split（chr 1–4 vs chr 5–22）；(b) 跨物種任務做 species-aware split；(c) 對 protein-coding region 用 BLAST + threshold 過濾 train ↔ test homologous pair；(d) 報告兩種 split 的 performance 比較。
Pitfall 6: Benchmark leakage from missing chromosome / species splits
Why wrong: when genomic-FM benchmarks (GUE, BEND, Genomic Benchmarks) use random train/test splits, homologous regions / paralog genes straddle train and test — inflating performance. Tang 2024 re-analysed several benchmarks and saw 10–30% drops after train/test homology filtering.
Fix: (a) at minimum, chromosome-aware splits (chr 1–4 vs chr 5–22); (b) species-aware splits for cross-species tasks; (c) for protein-coding regions, BLAST-filter train ↔ test homologous pairs above a threshold; (d) report performance under both random and proper splits.
資深讀者深入

進階細節

🔬 SSM (Mamba) vs Transformer、k-mer vs BPE、reference bias、cross-species transfer

① SSM (Mamba) vs Transformer 在長基因組序列的 trade-off。Transformer self-attention O(n²) memory，使序列長度 > 32k 時 GPU 不可承受（Caduceus 之前 NT 上限約 6 kb）。Mamba（Gu & Dao 2023, ICLR 2024）用 selective state space model：將 attention 替換為 SSM scan，memory 線性、time complexity O(n log n)。對 100 kb–1 Mb scale 的 enhancer-promoter / TAD / chromosome compartment 是 game-changer。但 Mamba 在 short-range「needle in haystack」retrieval 弱於 Transformer——這是 Lieber 2024 (Jamba) 推出 hybrid SSM+Transformer 的動機。對 genomic FM，Caduceus（pure SSM）vs Enformer（Transformer with dilated convolutions）vs HyenaDNA（SSM 變體）各有優勢；選型取決於 task scale。

② k-mer vs BPE genomic tokenizer 的權衡。k-mer（NT 用 6-mer）：固定切，每個 token 對應 6 個碱基，vocabulary 4⁶ = 4096。優點：易解釋（每個 token 有明確序列）、frame-aware（若用 6 = 3×2 框內三聯）；缺點：在 motif 邊界產生 fragmentation。BPE（DNABERT-2 用）：學頻繁 sub-sequence、可變長度，typically 1–10 bp/token。優點：高壓縮率、auto adapt 到 frequent motif；缺點：boundary 不可預期、interpretability 差。Single-nucleotide（HyenaDNA, Caduceus 等）：最 flexible、無 boundary 問題，但序列長度爆炸，需 SSM 來處理。Tang 2024 systematic comparison 顯示沒有 universal winner，task-dependent。

③ 訓練資料 reference bias。Genomic FM 訓練資料主要來自 GRCh38（人類部分）、其他物種 ENA / NCBI 序列。GRCh38 sample composition 約 70% European、10% Asian、10% African、10% Latino。Martin 2019 (Nat Genet) PRS 跨 ancestry transfer 性能 drop 50–80%。對 genomic FM，Wang & Goldstein 2025 (medRxiv) 重新評估 NT 在 H3Africa cohort 上發現 VEP AUROC 從 European 的 0.876 降到 African 的 0.78——10 pp drop。修正方法：(a) cross-ancestry fine-tune（H3Africa、All of Us、TOPMed）；(b) ancestry-stratified evaluation；(c) 在臨床部署前 mandatory cross-ancestry calibration check。Cooper 2025 (Nat Genet) commentary 呼籲 genomic FM benchmark 必須 by-ancestry 報告。

④ Cross-species transfer。NT 在 850 物種 pretrain（從 plant、microbe 到 mammals），希望學到 universal sequence pattern。實務發現：(a) 同 vertebrate（mouse → human）transfer 強，TF binding 與 promoter feature 可 zero-shot transfer；(b) cross-domain（plant → mammal）transfer 弱，因為 cis-regulatory grammar 不同；(c) microbe → eukaryote transfer 中等，core elements（promoter Shine-Dalgarno vs Kozak）差異大。設計 multi-species pretrain 的最佳實踐：依生物學距離 weighting（mammals 多於 distant species）+ ancestry / strand-aware augmentation。

Q1: Genomic FM 真的學到生物意義還是 sequence statistics？

NT、DNABERT-2 在 benchmark 上很強，但它們真的「理解」biology 嗎？批評者（如 Linder 2024 commentary）認為它們 captures 的是 motif statistics 與 conservation pattern，不是 mechanistic understanding。支持者認為這就是 biology——sequence determines function。請思考：sequence statistics 與「真正的 biology understanding」邊界在哪？如何設計 experiments 區分？是否該對 genomic FM 提出比 LLM 更嚴格的 mechanistic interpretability 要求？

Q1: Do genomic FMs learn biology or just sequence statistics?

NT and DNABERT-2 perform well on benchmarks — but do they "understand" biology? Critics (Linder 2024 commentary) argue they capture motif statistics and conservation patterns, not mechanistic understanding. Supporters reply: that is biology — sequence determines function. Consider: where is the line between "sequence statistics" and "real biological understanding"? How would you design experiments to tell them apart? Should genomic FMs face stricter mechanistic-interpretability requirements than LLMs?

Q2: 什麼時候 fine-tune 才值得？

對 lab 而言，fine-tune 一個 2.5B NT 需 ~8× A100 GPU days、~$2k cost、~1 個月時間。Zero-shot 立即可用。某 PI 問：「我要做 BRCA1 missense pathogenicity prediction，有 ~500 label，該 fine-tune 嗎？」博後算 zero-shot 已達 AUROC 0.85，fine-tune 估計 0.89——4 pp gain 值得 $2k 嗎？請思考：什麼 label 數量、performance threshold、application context 下 fine-tune 才值得？是否該先 zero-shot + literature search？某 clinical decision 要不要等 fine-tune validate？

Q2: When is fine-tuning worth it?

Fine-tuning a 2.5B NT costs ~8× A100 GPU-days, ~$2k, ~1 month. Zero-shot is instant. A PI asks: "I want BRCA1 missense pathogenicity prediction with ~500 labels — should I fine-tune?" The postdoc shows zero-shot already at AUROC 0.85, fine-tune estimated 0.89 — is a 4 pp gain worth $2k? Consider: at what label count, performance threshold, and application context is fine-tuning worth it? Should you always try zero-shot + literature first? Should a clinical decision wait for fine-tune validation?

Q3: GRCh38 ancestry bias 該如何彌補？

NT、DNABERT-2 主要在 GRCh38（~70% European）pretrain，對 African / Asian / Latino population 預測 calibration 差（Wang & Goldstein 2025 顯示 10 pp AUROC drop）。某 medical center 想用 NT 做 clinical genome interpretation：(a) 等下一代 NT 用 1000G + All of Us 重 train？(b) 自己 fine-tune？(c) 限制應用到 European cohort？(d) 對非 European patient 額外 manual review？請思考：哪個方案 cost-effective？倫理上是否該對 minoritised population 增加更多 QC？這對 clinical trial diversity 要求有何影響？

Q3: How should GRCh38 ancestry bias be remediated?

NT and DNABERT-2 are pretrained mostly on GRCh38 (~70% European), poorly calibrated on African / Asian / Latino populations (Wang & Goldstein 2025 show 10 pp AUROC drop). A medical center considering NT for clinical genome interpretation: (a) wait for a next-gen NT retrained on 1000G + All of Us? (b) fine-tune in-house? (c) restrict deployment to European cohorts? Implications for clinical-trial diversity mandates?

Q4: SSM 會取代 Transformer 嗎？

Caduceus 在 100 kb–1 Mb 長 context 上勝過 Transformer，但 Transformer 在 short-range retrieval 仍有優勢。生物學上，short-range（TF motif 6 bp）與 long-range（enhancer-promoter 100 kb）都需要。Lieber 2024 (Jamba) 推 hybrid SSM+Transformer。請思考：未來 5 年 genomic FM 主導 architecture 會是什麼？對 short + long task 都要做的研究者，該 invest 哪個 stack？是否該等 hybrid 架構成熟再投入？

Q4: Will SSM replace Transformer?

Caduceus beats Transformers on 100 kb–1 Mb context, but Transformers retain edges on short-range retrieval. Biology needs both short-range (6-bp TF motifs) and long-range (100-kb enhancer-promoter). Lieber 2024 (Jamba) introduced hybrid SSM+Transformer. Consider: what architecture will dominate genomic FMs in the next 5 years? For researchers spanning short- and long-range tasks, which stack to invest in? Should you wait for hybrid architectures to mature?
